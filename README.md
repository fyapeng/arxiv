### 贝叶斯说服的公理基础
**[Axiomatic Foundations of Bayesian Persuasion](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23409)**

**Authors**: Youichiro Higashi, Kemal Ozbek, Norio Takeoka

**Abstract**: In this paper, we study axiomatic foundations of Bayesian persuasion, where a principal (i.e., sender) delegates the task of choice making after informing a biased agent (i.e., receiver) about the payoff relevant uncertain state (see, e.g., Kamenica and Gentzkow (2011)). Our characterizations involve novel models of Bayesian persuasion, where the principal can steer the agent's bias after acquiring costly information. Importantly, we provide an elicitation method using only observable menu-choice data of the principal, which shows how to construct the principal's subjective costs of acquiring information even when he anticipates managing the agent's bias.

**摘要**: 在本文中，我们研究了贝叶斯说服的公理基础，其中委托人（即发送者）在通知有偏见的代理人（即接收者）关于与收益相关的不确定状态后，委托其进行选择（参见，例如，Kamenica和Gentzkow（2011））。我们的特征涉及贝叶斯说服的新模型，其中委托人可以在获取成本高昂的信息后引导代理人的偏见。重要的是，我们提供了一种仅使用委托人可观察的菜单选择数据的激励方法，该方法展示了如何在委托人预期管理代理人偏见时构建委托人获取信息的主观成本。

### 中产阶级的政治经济学定义
**[A Political Economy Definition of the Middle Class](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23523)**

**Authors**: Alejandro Corvalan

**Abstract**: Economists often define the middle class based on income distribution, yet selecting which segment constitutes the `middle' is essentially arbitrary. This paper proposes a definition of the middle class based solely on the properties of income distribution. It argues that for a collection of unequal societies, the poor and rich extremes of the distribution unambiguously worsen or improve their respective income shares with inequality. In contrast, such an effect is moderated at the center. I define the middle class as the segment of the income distribution whose income shares are insensitive to changes in inequality. This unresponsiveness property allows one to single out, endogenously and with minimal arbitrariness, the location of the middle class. The paper first provides a theoretical argument for the existence of such a group. It then uses detailed percentile data from the World Income Database (WID) to empirically characterize the world middle class: a group skewed toward the upper part of the distribution - comprising much of the affluent population below the very rich - with stable borders over time and across countries. The definition aligns with the prevailing view in political economy of the middle class as as a moderating actor, given their null incentives to engage in distributive conflict.

**摘要**: 经济学家通常根据收入分配来定义中产阶级，但选择哪个部分构成“中间”基本上是任意的。本文提出了一个完全基于收入分配属性的中产阶级定义。它认为，对于一系列不平等的社会，分配的穷人和富人极端在不平等的情况下，他们的收入份额会明确地恶化或改善。相比之下，这种效应在中心位置被缓和。我将中产阶级定义为收入分配中对不平等变化不敏感的收入份额部分。这种无反应属性允许人们以最小的任意性，内生地单独确定中产阶级的位置。文章首先为这样一个群体的存在提供了理论论证。然后，它使用世界收入数据库（WID）的详细百分位数据，对世界中产阶级进行实证描述：一个向分布的上部倾斜的群体——包括非常富有的人口中的大部分——随着时间和跨国家的稳定边界。这个定义与政治经济学中关于中产阶级作为缓和行为者的主要观点一致，因为他们没有参与分配冲突的动机。

### 总体对象分配的显示偏好理论
**[The Revealed Preference Theory of Aggregate Object Allocations](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23352)**

**Authors**: Umutcan Salman

**Abstract**: I develop a revealed preference framework to test whether an aggregate allocation of indivisible objects satisfies Pareto efficiency and individual rationality (PI) without observing individual preferences. Exploiting the type-based preferences of Echenique et. al. (2013), I derive necessary and sufficient conditions for PI-rationalizability. I show that an allocation is PI-rationalizable if and only if its allocation graph is acyclic. Next, I analyse non-PI-rationalizable allocations. First, I study the three respective problems: removal of a minimum size of subset of individuals/types/objects to restore PI-rationalizability. I prove that these three problems are NP-complete. Then, I provide an alternative goodness-of-fit measure, namely Critical Exchange Index (CEI). The CEI assess the highest portion of individuals who can involve exchanging their final objects to reach PI. This measure shows the extent of inefficiencies. The results yield the first complete revealed preference analysis for Pareto efficiency and individual rationality in matching markets and provide an implementable tool for empirical applications.

**摘要**: 我开发了一个显示偏好框架，用于测试在不观察个体偏好的情况下，不可分割物品的总体分配是否满足帕累托效率和个体理性（PI）。利用Echenique等人（2013年）基于类型的偏好，我推导出了PI合理化的充分必要条件。我证明了一个分配如果是PI合理化的，当且仅当其分配图是无环的。接下来，我分析了非PI合理化的分配。首先，我研究了三个各自独立的问题：移除最小数量的个体/类型/物品子集以恢复PI合理性。我证明了这三个问题是NP完全的。然后，我提供了一个替代的拟合优度度量，即临界交换指数（CEI）。CEI评估了最高比例的个体，他们可以通过交换他们最终的物品来达到PI。这个度量显示了效率低下的程度。这些结果为匹配市场中的帕累托效率和个体理性提供了第一个完整的显示偏好分析，并为实证应用提供了一个可实施的工具。

### 婚姻话题中的三大巨头：LLM辅助分析微博和小红书上的道德伦理和情感
**[The Big Three in Marriage Talk: LLM-Assisted Analysis of Moral Ethics and Sentiment on Weibo and Xiaohongshu](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23609)**

**Authors**: Frank Tian-Fang Ye, Xiaozi Gao

**Abstract**: China's marriage registrations have declined dramatically, dropping from 13.47 million couples in 2013 to 6.1 million in 2024. Understanding public attitudes toward marriage requires examining not only emotional sentiment but also the moral reasoning underlying these evaluations. This study analyzed 219,358 marriage-related posts from two major Chinese social media platforms (Sina Weibo and Xiaohongshu) using large language model (LLM)-assisted content analysis. Drawing on Shweder's Big Three moral ethics framework, posts were coded for sentiment (positive, negative, neutral) and moral dimensions (Autonomy, Community, Divinity). Results revealed platform differences: Weibo discourse skewed positive, while Xiaohongshu was predominantly neutral. Most posts across both platforms lacked explicit moral framing. However, when moral ethics were invoked, significant associations with sentiment emerged. Posts invoking Autonomy ethics and Community ethics were predominantly negative, whereas Divinity-framed posts tended toward neutral or positive sentiment. These findings suggest that concerns about both personal autonomy constraints and communal obligations drive negative marriage attitudes in contemporary China. The study demonstrates LLMs' utility for scaling qualitative analysis and offers insights for developing culturally informed policies addressing marriage decline in Chinese contexts.

**摘要**: 中国的结婚登记数量急剧下降，从2013年的1347万对夫妇降至2024年的610万对。要理解公众对婚姻的态度，不仅需要考察情感倾向，还需要考察这些评价背后的道德推理。本研究利用大型语言模型（LLM）辅助内容分析，分析了来自两个主要中国社交媒体平台（新浪微博和小红书）的219358条与婚姻相关的帖子。借鉴Shweder的三大道德伦理框架，帖子被编码为情感（积极、消极、中立）和道德维度（自主性、社区、神性）。结果显示平台差异：微博话语偏向积极，而小红书主要是中立。两个平台上的大多数帖子缺乏明确的道德框架。然而，当道德伦理被引用时，与情感的显著关联出现了。引用自主性伦理和社区伦理的帖子主要是消极的，而以神性框架的帖子则倾向于中立或积极的情感。这些发现表明，对个人自主性限制和社区义务的担忧推动了当代中国对婚姻的消极态度。该研究展示了LLM在扩展定性分析方面的实用性，并为制定针对中国婚姻下降的文化知情政策提供了见解。

### 不同企业生产率下的R&D网络
**[R&D Networks under Heterogeneous Firm Productivities](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23337)**

**Authors**: M. Sadra Heydari, Zafer Kanik, Santiago Montoya-Blandón

**Abstract**: We introduce heterogeneous R&D productivities into an endogenous R&D network formation model, generalizing the framework in Goyal and Moraga-Gonzalez (2001). Heterogeneous productivities endogenously create asymmetric gains for connecting firms: the less productive firm benefits disproportionately, while the more productive firm exerts greater R&D effort and incurs higher costs. For sufficiently large productivity gaps between two firms, the more productive firm experiences reduced profits from being connected to the less productive one. This overturns the benchmark results on pairwise stable networks: for sufficiently large productivity gaps, the complete network becomes unstable, whereas the Positive Assortative (PA) network -- where firms cluster by productivity levels -- emerges as stable. Simulations show that the PA structure delivers higher welfare than the complete network; nevertheless, welfare under PA formation follows an inverted U-shape in the fraction of high-productivity firms, reflecting crowding-out effects at high fractions. Altogether, a counterintuitive finding emerges: economies with higher average R&D productivity may exhibit lower welfare through (i) the formation of alternative stable R&D network structures or (ii) a crowding-out effect of high-productivity firms. Our findings highlight that productivity-enhancing policies should account for their impact on endogenous R&D alliances and effort, as such endogenous responses may offset or even reverse the intended welfare gains.

**摘要**: 我们将异质性研发生产率引入到一个内生的研发网络形成模型中，从而推广了Goyal和Moraga-Gonzalez（2001）的框架。异质性生产率内生地为连接公司创造了不对称的收益：生产率较低的公司获得不成比例的好处，而生产率较高的公司则投入更多的研发努力并承担更高的成本。对于两家公司之间足够大的生产率差距，生产率较高的公司从与生产率较低的公司连接中获得的利润减少。这颠覆了关于成对稳定网络的基准结果：对于足够大的生产率差距，完整的网络变得不稳定，而正向分类（PA）网络——公司按生产率水平聚集——则作为稳定的网络出现。模拟表明，PA结构比完整网络提供更高的福利；尽管如此，在PA形成下的福利随着高生产率公司的比例呈现倒U形，反映了高比例下的挤出效应。总的来说，一个违反直觉的发现出现了：平均研发生产率较高的经济体可能通过（i）形成替代的稳定研发网络结构或（ii）高生产率公司的挤出效应表现出较低的福利。我们的发现强调，提高生产率的政策应该考虑其对内生研发联盟和努力的影响，因为这种内生反应可能会抵消甚至逆转预期的福利收益。

### 多维筛选中的动态解耦
**[Dynamic Decoupling in Multidimensional Screening](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23274)**

**Authors**: Eric Gao

**Abstract**: I study multidimensional sequential screening. A monopolist contracts with an agent endowed with private information about the distribution of their eventual valuations of different goods; a contract is written and the agent reports their initial private information before drawing and reporting their valuations. In these settings, the monopolist frontloads surplus extraction: Any information rents given to the agent to elicit their post-contractual valuations can be extracted in expectation before valuations are drawn. This significantly simplifies the multidimensional screening problem. If the agent's valuations satisfy invariant dependencies (valuations can be dependent across dimensions, but how valuations are coupled cannot vary in their initial private information), the optimal mechanism coincides with independently offering the optimal sequential screening mechanism for each good, regardless of the dependency structure.

**摘要**: 我研究的是多维顺序筛选问题。一个垄断者与一个拥有关于不同商品最终估值分布的私人信息的代理人签订合同；在抽取并报告他们的估值之前，合同被起草，代理人报告他们的初始私人信息。在这些情况下，垄断者预先提取剩余：任何给予代理人以引出他们合同后估值的信息租金都可以在估值抽取之前预期提取。这大大简化了多维筛选问题。如果代理人的估值满足不变依赖性（估值可以在不同维度上相互依赖，但是估值如何耦合不能在他们的初始私人信息中变化），最优机制与独立提供每种商品的最优顺序筛选机制相一致，无论依赖结构如何。

### 无需外生产品特征的非参数需求识别
**[Nonparametric Identification of Demand without Exogenous Product Characteristics](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23211)**

**Authors**: Kirill Borusyak, Jiafeng Chen, Peter Hull, Lihua Lei

**Abstract**: We study the identification of differentiated product demand with exogenous supply-side instruments, allowing product characteristics to be endogenous. Past analyses have argued that exogenous characteristic-based instruments are essentially necessary given a sufficiently flexible demand model with a suitable index restriction. We show, however, that price counterfactuals are nonparametrically identified by recentered instruments -- which combine exogenous shocks to prices with endogenous product characteristics -- under a weaker index restriction and a new condition we term faithfulness. We argue that faithfulness, like the usual completeness condition for nonparametric identification with instruments, can be viewed as a technical requirement on the richness of identifying variation rather than a substantive economic restriction, and we show that it holds under a variety of non-nested conditions on either price-setting or the index.

**摘要**: 我们研究了在供应侧外生工具下差异化产品需求的识别问题，允许产品特性是内生的。过去的分析认为，鉴于需求模型足够灵活且具有适当的指数限制，外生特性基础工具基本上是必需的。然而，我们展示了价格反事实可以通过重新中心化的工具非参数识别——这些工具结合了价格的外生冲击和内生产品特性——在较弱的指数限制和我们称之为忠诚度的新条件下。我们认为，忠诚度，像通常的完备性条件一样，可以被视为对识别变化丰富性的技术要求，而不是实质性的经济限制，我们展示了它在价格设定或指数的多种非嵌套条件下都成立。

### 有向网络中的公共品提供：核方法
**[Public Goods Provision in Directed Networks: A Kernel Approach](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23193)**

**Authors**: Jingmin Huang, Yang Sun, Fanqi Xu, Wei Zhao

**Abstract**: This paper investigates the decentralized provision of public goods in directed networks. We establish a correspondence between kernels in graph theory and specialized equilibria in which players either contribute a fixed threshold amount or free-ride entirely. Leveraging this relationship, we derive sufficient conditions for the existence and uniqueness of specialized equilibria in deterministic networks and prove that specialized equilibria exist almost surely in large random networks. We further demonstrate that enhancing network reciprocity weakly expands the set of specialized equilibria without destroying existing ones. Moreover, we propose an iterative elimination algorithm that simplifies the network while preserving equilibrium properties. Finally, we show that a Nash equilibrium is stable only if it is specialized, thereby providing dynamic justification for our focus on this equilibrium class.

**摘要**: 本文研究了在有向网络中公共物品的分散提供问题。我们建立了图论中的核与特定均衡之间的对应关系，在这种特定均衡中，参与者要么贡献一个固定的阈值金额，要么完全搭便车。利用这种关系，我们推导出了确定性网络中特定均衡存在和唯一性的必要条件，并证明了在大型随机网络中，特定均衡几乎肯定存在。我们进一步证明，增强网络互惠性会弱化地扩大特定均衡的集合，而不会破坏现有的均衡。此外，我们提出了一种迭代消除算法，该算法简化了网络，同时保留了均衡属性。最后，我们展示了纳什均衡只有在它是特定的均衡时才是稳定的，从而为我们关注这一类均衡提供了动态的理由。

### 使用面板数据分析评估宏观经济变量对D-8国家儿童死亡率的影响
**[Assessing the Effects of Macroeconomic Variables on Child Mortality in D-8 Countries Using Panel Data Analysis](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23110)**

**Authors**: M. Waseem Akram, Binita Shahi, M. Javed Akram

**Abstract**: This research analyses the axiomatic link among health expenditures, inflation rate, and gross national income (GNI) per capita concerning the child mortality (CMU5) rate in D-8 nations, employing panel data analysis from 1995 to 2014. Utilising conventional panel unit root tests and linear regression models, we establish that education expenditures, in conjunction with health expenditures, inflation rate, and GNI per capita, display stationarity at level. **[Choice Modeling and Pricing for Scheduled Services](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.22271)**

**Authors**: Adam N. Elmachtoub, Kumar Goutam, Roger Lederman

**Abstract**: We describe a novel framework for discrete choice modeling and price optimization for settings where scheduled service options (often hierarchical) are offered to customers, which is applicable across many businesses including some within Amazon. In such business settings, the customers would see multiple options, often substitutable, with their features and their prices. These options typically vary in the start and/or end time of the service requested, such as the date of service or a service time window. The costs and demand can vary widely across these different options, resulting in the need for different prices. We propose a system which allows for segmenting the marketplace (as defined by the particular business) using decision trees, while using parametric discrete choice models within each market segment to accurately estimate conversion behavior. Using parametric discrete choice models allows us to capture important behavioral aspects like reference price effects which naturally occur in scheduled service applications. In addition, we provide natural and fast heuristics to do price optimization. For one such Amazon business where we conducted a live A/B experiment, this new framework outperformed the existing pricing system in every key metric, increasing our target performance metric by 19%, while providing a robust platform to support future new services of the business. The model framework has now been in full production for this business since Q4 2023.

**摘要**: 我们描述了一个新颖的离散选择建模和价格优化框架，适用于向客户提供预定服务选项（通常是分层的）的多种业务场景，包括亚马逊内部的一些业务。在这些商业环境中，客户会看到多个选项，通常是可替代的，包括它们的特性和价格。这些选项通常在服务的开始和/或结束时间上有所不同，例如服务的日期或服务时间窗口。这些不同选项的成本和需求可能会有很大差异，从而导致需要不同的价格。我们提出了一个系统，允许使用决策树对市场（由特定业务定义）进行细分，同时在每个市场细分中使用参数离散选择模型来准确估计转化行为。使用参数离散选择模型使我们能够捕捉到在预定服务应用中自然发生的参考价格效应等重要行为方面。此外，我们提供了自然且快速的启发式方法来进行价格优化。对于我们进行实时A/B测试的亚马逊业务之一，这个新框架在每个关键指标上都优于现有的定价系统，将我们的目标性能指标提高了19%，同时提供了一个强大的平台来支持业务未来的新服务。自2023年第四季度以来，该模型框架已全面投入该业务的生产。

### 波动性对基于时间的交易排序政策的影响
**[Impact of Volatility on Time-Based Transaction Ordering Policies](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23386)**

**Authors**: Sunghun Ko, Jinsuk Park

**Abstract**: We study Arbitrum's Express Lane Auction (ELA), an ahead-of-time second-price auction that grants the winner an exclusive latency advantage for one minute. Building on a single-round model with risk-averse bidders, we propose a hypothesis that the value of priority access is discounted relative to risk-neutral valuation due to the difficulty of forecasting short-horizon volatility and bidders' risk aversion. **摘要**: 我们研究了Arbitrum的快速通道拍卖（ELA），这是一种提前的第二价格拍卖，它为胜者提供了一分钟内独家的低延迟优势。基于一个包含风险规避竞拍者的单轮模型，我们提出了一个假设，即由于预测短期波动的困难和竞拍者的风险规避，优先访问的价值相对于风险中性估值是打折的。我们使用与高频ETH价格相匹配的ELA出价记录来测试这些预测，并发现结果与模型一致。

### 离线强化学习中V-Learning的Bellman校准
**[Bellman Calibration for V-Learning in Offline Reinforcement Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23694)**

**Authors**: Lars van der Laan, Nathan Kallus

**Abstract**: We introduce Iterated Bellman Calibration, a simple, model-agnostic, post-hoc procedure for calibrating off-policy value predictions in infinite-horizon Markov decision processes. Bellman calibration requires that states with similar predicted long-term returns exhibit one-step returns consistent with the Bellman equation under the target policy. We adapt classical histogram and isotonic calibration to the dynamic, counterfactual setting by repeatedly regressing fitted Bellman targets onto a model's predictions, using a doubly robust pseudo-outcome to handle off-policy data. This yields a one-dimensional fitted value iteration scheme that can be applied to any value estimator. Our analysis provides finite-sample guarantees for both calibration and prediction under weak assumptions, and critically, without requiring Bellman completeness or realizability.

**摘要**: 我们介绍了迭代贝尔曼校准，这是一种简单、模型不可知的事后处理过程，用于校准无限视界马尔可夫决策过程中的离策略价值预测。贝尔曼校准要求具有相似预测长期回报的状态在目标策略下表现出与贝尔曼方程一致的一步回报。我们通过反复将拟合的贝尔曼目标回归到模型预测上，使用双重鲁棒的伪结果来处理离策略数据，将经典直方图和等调校准适应到动态、反事实的设置中。这产生了一个一维拟合值迭代方案，可以应用于任何价值估计器。我们的分析在弱假设下为校准和预测提供了有限样本保证，并且关键地，不需要贝尔曼完整性或可实现性。

### 深度学习在艺术品市场估值中的应用
**[Deep Learning for Art Market Valuation](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23078)**

**Authors**: Jianping Mei, Michael Moses, Jan Waelty, Yucheng Yang

**Abstract**: We study how deep learning can improve valuation in the art market by incorporating the visual content of artworks into predictive models. Using a large repeated-sales dataset from major auction houses, we benchmark classical hedonic regressions and tree-based methods against modern deep architectures, including multi-modal models that fuse tabular and image data. We find that while artist identity and prior transaction history dominate overall predictive power, visual embeddings provide a distinct and economically meaningful contribution for fresh-to-market works where historical anchors are absent. Interpretability analyses using Grad-CAM and embedding visualizations show that models attend to compositional and stylistic cues. Our findings demonstrate that multi-modal deep learning delivers significant value precisely when valuation is hardest, namely first-time sales, and thus offers new insights for both academic research and practice in art market valuation.

### 从模型选择到模型信仰：为基于大型语言模型的研究建立新的度量标准
**[From Model Choice to Model Belief: Establishing a New Measure for LLM-Based Research](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23184)**

**Authors**: Hongshen Sun, Juanjuan Zhang

**Abstract**: Large language models (LLMs) are increasingly used to simulate human behavior, but common practices to use LLM-generated data are inefficient. Treating an LLM's output ("model choice") as a single data point underutilizes the information inherent to the probabilistic nature of LLMs. This paper introduces and formalizes "model belief," a measure derived from an LLM's token-level probabilities that captures the model's belief distribution over choice alternatives in a single generation run. The authors prove that model belief is asymptotically equivalent to the mean of model choices (a non-trivial property) but forms a more statistically efficient estimator, with lower variance and a faster convergence rate. Analogous properties are shown to hold for smooth functions of model belief and model choice often used in downstream applications. The authors demonstrate the performance of model belief through a demand estimation study, where an LLM simulates consumer responses to different prices. In practical settings with limited numbers of runs, model belief explains and predicts ground-truth model choice better than model choice itself, and reduces the computation needed to reach sufficiently accurate estimates by roughly a factor of 20. The findings support using model belief as the default measure to extract more information from LLM-generated data.

**摘要**: 大型语言模型（LLM）越来越多地被用来模拟人类行为，但使用LLM生成数据的常见做法效率不高。将LLM的输出（“模型选择”）视为一个数据点，未能充分利用LLM固有的概率性质中的信息。本文引入并形式化了“模型信念”，这是一种从LLM的标记级概率中派生出来的度量，它捕捉了模型在单次生成运行中对选择替代方案的信念分布。作者证明了模型信念在渐近意义上等同于模型选择的平均值（一个非平凡属性），但形成了一个统计效率更高的估计器，具有更低的方差和更快的收敛速度。对于模型信念和模型选择的平滑函数（通常用于下游应用）也显示出类似的属性。作者通过一个需求估计研究展示了模型信念的性能，其中LLM模拟了消费者对不同价格的反应。在运行次数有限的实际设置中，模型信念比模型选择本身更好地解释和预测了真实的模型选择，并且将达到足够准确估计所需的计算量减少了大约20倍。这些发现支持使用模型信念作为默认度量，以从LLM生成的数据中提取更多信息。

### 面板耦合矩阵-张量聚类模型及其在资产定价中的应用
**[Panel Coupled Matrix-Tensor Clustering Model with Applications to Asset Pricing](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.23567)**

**Authors**: Liyuan Cui, Guanhao Feng, Yuefeng Han, Jiayan Li

**Abstract**: We tackle the challenge of estimating grouping structures and factor loadings in asset pricing models, where traditional regressions struggle due to sparse data and high noise. Existing approaches, such as those using fused penalties and multi-task learning, often enforce coefficient homogeneity across cross-sectional units, reducing flexibility. Clustering methods (e.g., spectral clustering, Lloyd's algorithm) achieve consistent recovery under specific conditions but typically rely on a single data source. To address these limitations, we introduce the Panel Coupled Matrix-Tensor Clustering (PMTC) model, which simultaneously leverages a characteristics tensor and a return matrix to identify latent asset groups. By integrating these data sources, we develop computationally efficient tensor clustering algorithms that enhance both clustering accuracy and factor loading estimation. Simulations demonstrate that our methods outperform single-source alternatives in clustering accuracy and coefficient estimation, particularly under moderate signal-to-noise conditions. Empirical application to U.S. equities demonstrates the practical value of PMTC, yielding higher out-of-sample total $R^2$ and economically interpretable variation in factor exposures.

**摘要**: 我们应对了在资产定价模型中估计分组结构和因子载荷的挑战，传统回归方法由于数据稀疏和高噪声而难以应对。现有的方法，如使用融合惩罚和多任务学习的方法，通常在跨截面单元中强制执行系数同质性，降低了灵活性。聚类方法（例如，谱聚类、Lloyd算法）在特定条件下能够实现一致的恢复，但通常依赖于单一数据源。为了解决这些限制，我们引入了面板耦合矩阵-张量聚类（PMTC）模型，该模型同时利用特征张量和回报矩阵来识别潜在的资产组。通过整合这些数据源，我们开发了计算效率高的张量聚类算法，提高了聚类准确性和因子载荷估计。模拟结果表明，我们的方法在聚类准确性和系数估计方面优于单一数据源的替代方案，特别是在中等信噪比条件下。对美国股票的实证应用展示了PMTC的实际价值，产生了更高的样本外总$R^2$和经济上可解释的因子暴露变化。
