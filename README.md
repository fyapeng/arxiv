### 退出国家扶贫运动、社会支持和改善心理健康
**[Exiting National Anti-Poverty Campaign, Social Support, and Improved Mental Health](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20292)**

**Authors**: Zhengwen Liu, Castiel Chen Zhuang, Yibo Wu

**Abstract**: We study the psychological and social impacts of exiting a national anti-poverty campaign, leveraging China's phase-out of its national poverty assistance as a natural experiment. Using a regression discontinuity design, we find that exiting the national campaign improves mental wellbeing. These improvements are accompanied by stronger social and family ties -- such as greater perceived support and communication, while income and material conditions remain largely unchanged. Our findings offer insights into the design of policy exits and underscore the importance of incorporating measures that sustain community- and family-based support systems when implementing or ending assistance programs.

**摘要**: 我们研究了一个国家反贫困运动退出对心理和社会影响，利用中国逐步取消国家扶贫援助作为自然实验。通过使用回归不连续设计，我们发现退出国家运动改善了心理健康。这些改善伴随着更强的社会和家庭联系——例如更大的感知支持和沟通，而收入和物质条件基本保持不变。我们的发现为政策退出的设计提供了洞见，并强调了在实施或结束援助计划时纳入维持社区和家庭支持系统的措施的重要性。

### 人工智能驱动的集成电动出行、可再生能源和电网管理运营数字平台的成本效益分析
**[Cost-benefit analysis of an AI-driven operational digital platform for integrated electric mobility, renewable energy, and grid management](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20631)**

**Authors**: Arega Getaneh Abate, Xiaobing Zhang, Xiufeng Liu, Dogan Keles

**Abstract**: Integrating electric mobility (electric vehicles (EVs), electric trucks (ETs)) and renewable energy sources (RES) with the power grid is paramount for achieving decarbonization, efficiency, and stability. Given the rapid growth of decentralized technologies and their critical role in decarbonization, two critical challenges emerge: first, the development of a digital platform for operational coordination; and second, rigorous research into their cost-benefit profile. This paper addresses this by presenting a comprehensive cost-benefit analysis (CBA) of an AI-driven operational digital platform (ODP) designed for holistic, cross-sectoral optimization. The ODP aims to enhance energy efficiency, grid reliability, and environmental sustainability. A seven-step CBA framework, aligned with EU guidelines, quantifies economic, reliability, and environmental benefits against capital and operational expenditures, explicitly linking benefit magnitude to AI-driven ODP and optimization efficiencies, such as quantified improvements in market arbitrage from ODP, enabled forecasting, and enhanced operational efficiencies across various services.

**摘要**: 将电动出行（电动汽车（EVs）、电动卡车（ETs））和可再生能源（RES）与电网整合对于实现脱碳、效率和稳定性至关重要。鉴于去中心化技术的快速增长及其在脱碳中的关键作用，出现了两个关键挑战：首先，开发一个用于运营协调的数字平台；其次，对他们的成本效益状况进行严格的研究。本文通过提出一个全面的成本效益分析（CBA），来解决这些问题，该分析针对为整体、跨部门优化而设计的人工智能驱动的运营数字平台（ODP）。ODP旨在提高能源效率、电网可靠性和环境可持续性。一个与欧盟指南一致的七步CBA框架，量化了经济、可靠性和环境效益与资本和运营支出的对比，明确将效益大小与人工智能驱动的ODP和优化效率联系起来，例如从ODP量化的市场套利改进、启用的预测和各种服务中增强的运营效率。

### 一个关于价格感知错误的实验
**[An experiment in price perception error](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.19953)**

**Authors**: Shawn Berry

**Abstract**: The process of consumer decision-making is multidimensional, and price perception is a very important but still not well-understood dimension for both marketers and consumers. Although heuristics or mental shortcuts are seen as biased and can cause decision errors, consumers tend to use price knowledge heuristics for purchase decisions, sometimes relying on old information. This study examined the effects of individual attitudinal and demographic factors on the ability of 351 respondents to correctly guess the prices of 13 products and services using ANOVA and the development of a seven-factor price perception model using latent variable analysis. While most respondents preferred to either research prices first or compare prices to a similar product, they either systematically underestimated or overestimated the prices of the products. In the ANOVA, brand loyalty, importance of substitute products and knockoff products, how financially well-off the respondent household was growing up, product quality importance, haggling tendency, and level of income were statistically significant predictors of price perception error. Latent variable analysis revealed that demographics, decision making, and price sensitivity factors had the greatest influence on price perception error. Level of education and income, frequency of regret, coupon importance, and how respondents chose to save or spend money were significant latent variables.

**摘要**: 消费者决策过程是多维的，而价格感知是一个非常重要但仍未被充分理解的维度，对于营销人员和消费者都是如此。尽管启发式或心理捷径被视为有偏见，并可能导致决策错误，但消费者倾向于使用价格知识启发式来进行购买决策，有时依赖于过时的信息。本研究使用方差分析（ANOVA）和潜在变量分析开发了一个七因素价格感知模型，检验了个体态度和人口统计因素对351名受访者正确猜测13种产品和服务价格能力的影响。大多数受访者倾向于先研究价格或将价格与类似产品进行比较，但他们要么系统性地低估，要么高估了产品的价格。在方差分析中，品牌忠诚度、替代产品和仿冒产品的重要性、受访者家庭在成长过程中的财务状况、产品质量的重要性、讨价还价的倾向以及收入水平是价格感知误差的统计显著预测因素。潜在变量分析揭示了人口统计学、决策制定和价格敏感性因素对价格感知误差的影响最大。教育和收入水平、遗憾的频率、优惠券的重要性以及受访者选择储蓄或消费的方式是显著的潜在变量。

### 一个针对选择性报告的敏锐且稳健的检验
**[A Sharp and Robust Test for Selective Reporting](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20035)**

**Authors**: Stefan Faridani

**Abstract**: This paper proposes a test that is consistent against every detectable form of selective reporting and remains interpretable even when the t-scores are not exactly normal. The test statistic is the distance between the smoothed empirical t-curve and the set of all t-curves that would be possible in the absence of any selective reporting. This novel projection test can only be evaded in large meta-samples by selective reporting that also evades all other valid tests of restrictions on the t-curve. A second benefit of the projection test is that under the null we can interpret the projection residual as noise plus bias incurred from approximating the t-score's exact distribution with the normal. Applying the test to the Brodeur et al. (2020) meta-data, we find that the t-curves for RCTs, IVs, and DIDs are more distorted than could arise by chance. But an Edgeworth Expansion reveals that these distortions are small enough to be plausibly explained by the only approximate normality of the individual t-scores. The detection of selective reporting in this meta-sample is therefore more fragile than previously known.

**摘要**: 本文提出了一种测试方法，该方法能够一致地对抗每一种可检测的选择性报告行为，并且即使t分数不是完全正态分布，该测试结果仍然具有可解释性。测试统计量是平滑的经验t曲线与在没有任何选择性报告的情况下可能存在的所有t曲线集合之间的距离。这种新颖的投影测试只能通过在大型元样本中选择性报告来规避，而这种选择性报告也能规避所有其他有效的t曲线限制测试。投影测试的第二个好处是，在零假设下，我们可以将投影残差解释为噪声加上由于用正态分布近似t分数的确切分布而产生的偏差。将该测试应用于Brodeur等人（2020年）的元数据，我们发现随机对照试验（RCTs）、工具变量（IVs）和双重差分（DIDs）的t曲线比偶然出现的情况更加扭曲。但Edgeworth展开显示，这些扭曲足够小，可以合理地用个体t分数的近似正态性来解释。因此，在这个元样本中检测选择性报告比之前已知的更为脆弱。

### 泰国的每日天气波动与次国家层面的经济增长：证据
**[Daily Fluctuations in Weather and Economic Growth at the Subnational Level: Evidence from Thailand](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20105)**

**Authors**: Sarun Kamolthip

**Abstract**: This study investigates the historical relationship between temperature fluctuations and Thailand's aggregate economic output. The results show inverted-U shape temperature effects on the annual growth rate of provincial output per capita. The estimated marginal change in the annual growth rate of provincial output per capita of a 1 degree Celsius temperature increase is between -1.248 and -3.799 percentage points, depending on the functional form used. I find that the warming temperature may affect the growth of economic output rather than its level. The results also suggest that temperature changes affect output growth only in the agricultural sector, but not in the industrial and service sectors. Province-level projections under both the RCP4.5 and RCP8.5 emission scenarios show that climate change will affect half of the Thai population by 2050. Projections show that climate change will make 62% (RCP4.5) and 86% (RCP8.5) of the Thai population poorer in per-capita terms than they would be in the absence of climate change in 2090. Nonetheless, these projections are sensitive to potential biases in future climate projections, particularly in models that do not account for lagged effects. All projections should be interpreted with caution.

**摘要**: 这项研究调查了温度波动与泰国总体经济产出之间的历史关系。结果显示，温度对人均省际产出年增长率的影响呈倒U形。根据所使用的函数形式，1摄氏度温度上升对人均省际产出年增长率的边际变化估计在-1.248到-3.799个百分点之间。我发现，变暖的温度可能会影响经济产出的增长，而不是其水平。结果还表明，温度变化只影响农业部门的产出增长，而不影响工业和服务部门。在RCP4.5和RCP8.5两种排放情景下，省级预测显示，到2050年气候变化将影响一半的泰国人口。预测显示，到2090年，气候变化将使62%（RCP4.5）和86%（RCP8.5）的泰国人口在人均基础上比没有气候变化的情况下更贫穷。尽管如此，这些预测对潜在的未来气候预测偏见很敏感，特别是那些没有考虑到滞后效应的模型。所有预测都应该谨慎解释。

### 自适应实验中的随时有效推断：协变量调整和平衡能力
**[Anytime-Valid Inference in Adaptive Experiments: Covariate Adjustment and Balanced Power](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20523)**

**Authors**: Daniel Molitor, Samantha Gold

**Abstract**: Adaptive experiments have become central to modern experimental design, enabling researchers to efficiently identify optimal treatments, improve statistical power, and maximize respondent welfare. However, adaptive experiments compromise valid inference and leave sub-optimal treatments underpowered. We introduce two methodological advances for adaptive experimentation. First, covariate-adjusted Mixture Adaptive Design (MADCovar) achieves substantial improvements in average treatment effect (ATE) precision by incorporating covariate adjustment within an anytime-valid inference framework. Second, power-modified MAD (MADMod) reallocates sample to underpowered treatment arms, improving statistical power across all treatments while maintaining error control. Both methods provide anytime-valid guarantees, enabling continuous monitoring without inflating Type 1 error rates. Simulation studies and empirical analyses demonstrate that MADCovar delivers significant precision gains and that MADMod ensures robust inference even for suboptimal treatments. Together, these methods address key limitations of adaptive experiments and equip researchers with practical tools for precise and reliable causal inference. Our proposed methods are implemented through an open-source software package.

**摘要**: 自适应实验已成为现代实验设计的核心，使研究人员能够有效地识别最佳治疗方案，提高统计功效，并最大化受访者的福利。然而，自适应实验会损害有效的推断，并使次优治疗方案的功效不足。我们为自适应实验引入了两种方法论进步。首先，协变量调整的混合自适应设计（MADCovar）通过在任何时候都有效的推断框架内纳入协变量调整，实现了平均治疗效果（ATE）精度的大幅改进。其次，功率修改的MAD（MADMod）重新分配样本到功效不足的治疗组，提高了所有治疗方案的统计功效，同时保持误差控制。这两种方法都提供了任何时候都有效的保证，使得可以持续监控而不增加第一类错误率。模拟研究和实证分析表明，MADCovar提供了显著的精度增益，而MADMod确保了即使对于次优治疗方案也能进行稳健的推断。这些方法共同解决了自适应实验的关键局限性，并为研究人员提供了精确可靠的因果推断的实用工具。我们提出的方法通过一个开源软件包实现。

### 介绍RobustiPy：一个高效的下一代多宇宙库，具备模型选择、平均化、重采样和可解释的人工智能功能。
**[Introducing RobustiPy: An efficient next generation multiversal library with model selection, averaging, resampling, and explainable artificial intelligence](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.19958)**

**Authors**: Daniel Valdenegro Ibarra, Jiani Yan, Duiyi Dai, Charles Rahal

**Abstract**: We present RobustiPy, a next generation Python-based framework for model uncertainty quantification and multiverse analysis, released under the GNU GPL v3.0. Through the integration of efficient bootstrap-based confidence intervals, combinatorial exploration of dependent-variable specifications, model selection and averaging, and two complementary joint-inference routines, RobustiPy transcends existing uncertainty-quantification tools. Its design further supports rigorous out-of-sample evaluation and apportions the predictive contribution of each covariate. We deploy the library across five carefully constructed simulations and ten empirically grounded case studies drawn from high-impact literature and teaching examples, including a novel re-analysis of "unexplained discrepancies" in famous prior work. To illustrate its performance, we time-profile RobustiPy over roughly 672 million simulated linear regressions. These applications showcase how RobustiPy not only accelerates robust inference but also deepens our interpretive insight into model sensitivity across the vast analytical multiverse within which scientists operate.

**摘要**: 我们介绍了RobustiPy，这是一个基于Python的下一代模型不确定性量化和多元宇宙分析框架，根据GNU GPL v3.0发布。通过集成高效的基于bootstrap的置信区间、依赖变量规范的组合探索、模型选择和平均，以及两种互补的联合推断程序，RobustiPy超越了现有的不确定性量化工具。它的设计进一步支持严格的样本外评估，并分配每个协变量的预测贡献。我们在五个精心构建的模拟和十个基于实证的案例研究中部署了这个库，这些案例研究来自高影响力的文献和教学示例，包括对著名先前工作中“无法解释的差异”的新分析。为了说明其性能，我们对大约6.72亿个模拟线性回归进行了RobustiPy的时间分析。这些应用展示了RobustiPy不仅加速了稳健推断，还加深了我们对模型在科学家操作的广阔分析多元宇宙中的敏感性的解释性洞察。

### 叙事转变检测：动态主题模型与大型语言模型的混合方法
**[Narrative Shift Detection: A Hybrid Approach of Dynamic Topic Models and Large Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20269)**

**Authors**: Kai-Robin Lange, Tobias Schmidt, Matthias Reccius, Henrik Müller, Michael Roos, Carsten Jentsch

**Abstract**: With rapidly evolving media narratives, it has become increasingly critical to not just extract narratives from a given corpus but rather investigate, how they develop over time. While popular narrative extraction methods such as Large Language Models do well in capturing typical narrative elements or even the complex structure of a narrative, applying them to an entire corpus comes with obstacles, such as a high financial or computational cost. We propose a combination of the language understanding capabilities of Large Language Models with the large scale applicability of topic models to dynamically model narrative shifts across time using the Narrative Policy Framework. We apply a topic model and a corresponding change point detection method to find changes that concern a specific topic of interest. Using this model, we filter our corpus for documents that are particularly representative of that change and feed them into a Large Language Model that interprets the change that happened in an automated fashion and distinguishes between content and narrative shifts. We employ our pipeline on a corpus of The Wall Street Journal news paper articles from 2009 to 2023. Our findings indicate that a Large Language Model can efficiently extract a narrative shift if one exists at a given point in time, but does not perform as well when having to decide whether a shift in content or a narrative shift took place.

**摘要**: 随着媒体叙事的快速演变，不仅仅是从给定语料库中提取叙事变得日益关键，更重要的是研究它们是如何随时间发展的。尽管流行的叙事提取方法，如大型语言模型，在捕捉典型叙事元素甚至复杂叙事结构方面做得很好，但将它们应用于整个语料库会遇到障碍，例如高昂的财务或计算成本。我们提出了一种结合大型语言模型的语言理解能力与主题模型的大规模适用性的方法，使用叙事政策框架动态模拟随时间变化的叙事转变。我们应用了一个主题模型和相应的变化点检测方法来发现与特定主题相关的变化。利用这个模型，我们筛选出特别代表该变化的文档，并将它们输入到一个大型语言模型中，该模型以自动化的方式解释发生的变化，并区分内容变化和叙事变化。我们将我们的流程应用于2009年至2023年《华尔街日报》新闻文章的语料库。我们的发现表明，如果存在叙事转变，大型语言模型可以高效地提取出来，但在决定是内容转变还是叙事转变发生时，其表现并不尽如人意。
