### 潘多拉魔盒重开：稳健搜索与选择过载
**[Pandora's Box Reopened: Robust Search and Choice Overload](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.21192)**

**Authors**: Sarah Auster, Yeon-Koo Che

**Abstract**: This paper revisits the classic Pandora's box problem, studying a decision-maker (DM) who seeks to minimize her maximal ex-post regret. The DM decides how many options to explore and in what order, before choosing one or taking an outside option. We characterize the regret-minimizing search rule and show that the likelihood of opting out often increases as more options become available for exploration. We show that this ``choice overload" is driven by the DM's fear of ``selection error" -- the regret from searching the wrong options -- suggesting that steering choice via recommendations or cost heterogeneity can mitigate regret and encourage search.

**摘要**: 本文重新审视了经典的潘多拉盒子问题，研究了一个决策者（DM）如何寻求最小化她的最大事后遗憾。DM决定探索多少选项以及以何种顺序，在最终选择一个或采取外部选项之前。我们描述了最小化遗憾的搜索规则，并展示了随着可供探索的选项数量增加，选择退出的可能性往往增加。我们表明，这种“选择过载”是由DM对“选择错误”——搜索错误选项的遗憾——的恐惧所驱动的，这表明通过推荐或成本异质性来引导选择可以减少遗憾并鼓励搜索。

### 经济生产力的规模法则：在大型语言模型辅助的咨询、数据分析和管理任务中的实验证据
**[Scaling Laws for Economic Productivity: Experimental Evidence in LLM-Assisted Consulting, Data Analyst, and Management Tasks](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.21316)**

**Authors**: Ali Merali

**Abstract**: This paper derives `Scaling Laws for Economic Impacts' -- empirical relationships between the training compute of Large Language Models (LLMs) and professional productivity. In a preregistered experiment, over 500 consultants, data analysts, and managers completed professional tasks using one of 13 LLMs. We find that each year of AI model progress reduced task time by 8%, with 56% of gains driven by increased compute and 44% by algorithmic progress. However, productivity gains were significantly larger for non-agentic analytical tasks compared to agentic workflows requiring tool use. These findings suggest continued model scaling could boost U.S. productivity by approximately 20% over the next decade.

**摘要**: 本文推导出了“经济影响的规模法则”——即大型语言模型（LLMs）的训练计算与专业生产力之间的经验关系。在一个预先注册的实验中，超过500名顾问、数据分析人员和经理使用13种LLMs之一完成了专业任务。我们发现，AI模型每进步一年，任务时间就减少了8%，其中56%的增益是由增加的计算能力驱动的，44%是由算法进步驱动的。然而，与需要使用工具的代理工作流程相比，非代理分析任务的生产力增益要大得多。这些发现表明，持续的模型扩展可能会在未来十年内将美国的生产力提高约20%。

### 福利风险：政策干预的分配影响
**[Welfare at Risk: Distributional impact of policy interventions](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.20918)**

**Authors**: Costas Lambros, Emerson Melo

**Abstract**: This paper proposes a framewrok for analyzing how the welfare effects of policy interventions are distributed across individuals when those effects are unobserved. Rather than focusing solely on average outcomes, the approach uses readily available information on average welfare responses to uncover meaningful patterns in how gains and losses are distributed across different populations. The framework is built around the concept of superquantile and applies to a broad class of models with unobserved individual heterogeneity. It enables policymakers to identify which groups are most adversely affected by a policy and to evaluate trade-offs between efficiency and equity. We illustrate the approach in three widely studied economic settings: price changes and compensated variation, treatment allocation with self-selection, and the cost-benefit analysis of social programs. In this latter application, we show how standard tools from the marginal treatment effect and generalized Roy model literature are useful for implementing our bounds for both the overall population and for individuals who participate in the program.

**摘要**: 本文提出了一个框架，用于分析在政策干预的福利效应未被观察到时，这些效应是如何在个体之间分配的。该方法不仅仅关注平均结果，而是利用关于平均福利反应的现成信息，揭示不同人群之间收益和损失分配的有意义的模式。该框架围绕超分位数概念构建，并适用于具有未观察到的个体异质性的广泛模型类别。它使政策制定者能够识别哪些群体受政策影响最大，并评估效率与公平之间的权衡。我们在三个广泛研究的经济环境中说明了这种方法：价格变化和补偿变化、具有自我选择的治疗分配，以及社会计划的成本效益分析。在最后一个应用中，我们展示了边际处理效应和广义罗伊模型文献中的标准工具如何有助于实施我们对整体人群以及参与计划的个体的界限。

### 在未知干扰存在时的差异中的差异
**[Difference-in-Differences in the Presence of Unknown Interference](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.21176)**

**Authors**: Fabrizia Mealli, Javier Viviens

**Abstract**: The stable unit treatment value (SUTVA) is a crucial assumption in the Difference-in-Differences (DiD) research design. It rules out hidden versions of treatment and any sort of interference and spillover effects across units. Even if this is a strong assumption, it has not received much attention from DiD practitioners and, in many cases, it is not even explicitly stated as an assumption, especially the no-interference assumption. In this technical note, we investigate what the DiD estimand identifies in the presence of unknown interference. We show that the DiD estimand identifies a contrast of causal effects, but it is not informative on any of these causal effects separately, without invoking further assumptions. Then, we explore different sets of assumptions under which the DiD estimand becomes informative about specific causal effects. We illustrate these results by revisiting the seminal paper on minimum wages and employment by Card and Krueger (1994).

**摘要**: 稳定单位处理值（SUTVA）是差异中的差异（DiD）研究设计中的关键假设。它排除了隐藏版本的处理和任何形式的干扰以及单位间的溢出效应。尽管这是一个强假设，但它并没有得到DiD实践者的太多关注，而且在许多情况下，它甚至没有被明确地表述为一个假设，特别是无干扰假设。在这篇技术注释中，我们探讨了在存在未知干扰的情况下，DiD估计量识别的是什么。我们展示了DiD估计量识别的是一种因果效应的对比，但如果没有进一步的假设，它并不能分别提供关于这些因果效应的任何信息。然后，我们探索了在不同假设集下，DiD估计量变得能够提供关于特定因果效应的信息。我们通过重新审视Card和Krueger（1994年）关于最低工资和就业的开创性论文，来说明这些结果。

### 学习宏观经济学语言
**[Learning the Macroeconomic Language](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.21031)**

**Authors**: Siddhartha Chib, Fei Tan

**Abstract**: We show how state-of-the-art large language models (LLMs), seemingly inapplicable to the small samples typical of macroeconomics, can be trained to learn the language of macroeconomy. We estimate a large-scale dynamic stochastic general equilibrium (DSGE) model on an initial segment of the data and obtain a posterior distribution over structural parameters. We sample from this posterior to generate millions of theory-consistent synthetic panels that, when mixed with actual macroeconomic data, form the training corpus for a time-series transformer with attention. The trained model is then used to forecast out-of-sample through 2025. The results show that this hybrid forecaster, which combines the theoretical coherence of DSGE models with the representational power of modern LLMs, successfully learns the macroeconomic language.

**摘要**: 我们展示了如何将最先进的大型语言模型（LLMs）训练成能够学习宏观经济学语言的工具，尽管它们似乎不适用于宏观经济学中典型的小样本数据。我们首先在数据的初始部分上估计一个大规模动态随机一般均衡（DSGE）模型，并得到结构参数的后验分布。我们从这个后验分布中抽样，生成数百万符合理论的综合面板数据，当这些数据与实际的宏观经济数据混合时，就形成了一个时间序列变换器（带注意力机制）的训练语料库。然后，我们使用这个训练好的模型来预测2025年之前的样本外数据。结果显示，这种混合预测器结合了DSGE模型的理论一致性和现代LLMs的表示能力，成功地学习了宏观经济语言。

### 在潜在结果模型中测试排除和形状限制
**[Testing Exclusion and Shape Restrictions in Potential Outcomes Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.20851)**

**Authors**: Hiroaki Kaido, Kirill Ponomarev

**Abstract**: Exclusion and shape restrictions play a central role in defining causal effects and interpreting estimates in potential outcomes models. To date, the testable implications of such restrictions have been studied on a case-by-case basis in a limited set of models. In this paper, we develop a general framework for characterizing sharp testable implications of general support restrictions on the potential response functions, based on a novel graph-based representation of the model. The framework provides a unified and constructive method for deriving all observable implications of the modeling assumptions. We illustrate the approach in several popular settings, including instrumental variables, treatment selection, mediation, and interference. As an empirical application, we revisit the US Lung Health Study and test for the presence of spillovers between spouses, specification of exposure maps, and persistence of treatment effects over time.

**摘要**: 排除和形状限制在定义潜在结果模型中的因果效应和解释估计中起着核心作用。迄今为止，这些限制的可测试含义在有限的模型集中以案例为基础进行了研究。在本文中，我们基于模型的新颖基于图的表示，开发了一个用于描述一般支持限制对潜在响应函数的尖锐可测试含义的通用框架。该框架为推导建模假设的所有可观察含义提供了统一且有建设性的方法。我们在几种流行的环境中说明了这种方法，包括工具变量、治疗选择、中介和干扰。作为实证应用，我们重新审视了美国肺部健康研究，并测试了配偶间的溢出效应、暴露图的规范以及治疗效果随时间的持续性。

### 经济范围的反弹效应与美国商业周期：一项时变分析
**[The economy-wide rebound effect and U.S. business cycles: A time-varying exercise](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.20765)**

**Authors**: Marcio Santetti

**Abstract**: Energy efficiency gains in production and consumption are undisputed economic and environmental goals. However, potential energy savings derived from efficiency innovations may have short-lasting effects due to increased demand for more affordable energy services. Measuring the size of this rebound effect is a critical tool for better assessing the reliability of energy-saving technological change for global warming mitigation. This paper estimates the size of the economy-wide rebound effect using time-varying Vector Autoregressive (VAR) models with stochastic volatility for U.S. business-cycle peak and trough periods. All models estimate a rebound effect close to 100%, with reductions in energy use lasting no longer than three years following energy efficiency innovations. The latter, therefore, are an insufficient tool for effectively changing historical energy use patterns.

**摘要**: 生产和消费中的能源效率提升是无可争议的经济和环境目标。然而，由于对更实惠的能源服务需求的增加，从效率创新中获得的潜在能源节省可能具有短暂的效果。测量这种回弹效应的大小是更好地评估节能技术变革对全球变暖缓解的可靠性的关键工具。本文使用随时间变化的向量自回归（VAR）模型和随机波动性来估算美国商业周期高峰和低谷期间经济范围内的回弹效应。所有模型估计的回弹效应接近100%，能源使用量的减少在能源效率创新之后不会超过三年。因此，后者是不足以有效改变历史能源使用模式的工具。

### 通过多功能CES生产函数对输入驱动的产出风险进行计量经济学建模
**[Econometric Modeling of Input-Driven Output Risk through a Versatile CES Production Function](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.20910)**

**Authors**: Ali Zeytoon-Nejad, Barry Goodwin

**Abstract**: The conventional functional form of the Constant-Elasticity-of-Substitution (CES) production function is a general production function nesting a number of other forms of production functions. Examples of such functions include Leontief, Cobb-Douglas, and linear production functions. Nevertheless, the conventional form of the CES production specification is still restrictive in multiple aspects. One example is the fact that the marginal effect of increasing input use always has to be to increase the variability of output quantity by the conventional construction of this function. This paper proposes a generalized variant of the CES production function that allows for various input effects on the probability distribution of output. Failure to allow for this possible input-output risk structure is indeed one of the limitations of the conventional form of the CES production function. This limitation may result in false inferences about input-driven output risk. In light of this, the present paper proposes a solution to this problem. First, it is shown that the familiar CES formulation suffers from very restrictive structural assumptions regarding risk considerations, and that such restrictions may lead to biased and inefficient estimates of production quantity and production risk. Following the general theme of Just and Pope's approach, a CES-based production-function specification that overcomes this shortcoming of the original CES production function is introduced, and a three-stage Nonlinear Least-Squares (NLS) estimation procedure for the estimation of the proposed functional form is presented. To illustrate the proposed approaches in this paper, two empirical applications in irrigation and fertilizer response using the famous Hexem-Heady experimental dataset are provided. Finally, implications for modeling input-driven production risks are discussed.

**摘要**: 恒定弹性替代（CES）生产函数的传统形式是一种通用生产函数，它包含了许多其他形式的生产函数。这些函数的例子包括列昂惕夫、柯布-道格拉斯和线性生产函数。尽管如此，CES生产函数的传统形式在多个方面仍然是有限制的。一个例子是，根据这个函数的传统构造，增加输入使用量的边际效应总是必须增加产出数量的变异性。本文提出了一个CES生产函数的广义变体，它允许各种输入对产出概率分布的影响。不允许这种可能的输入-产出风险结构确实是CES生产函数传统形式的一个限制。这种限制可能导致对输入驱动的产出风险的错误推断。鉴于此，本文提出了解决这个问题的方法。首先，展示了熟悉的CES公式在风险考虑方面受到非常严格的结构假设的束缚，这些限制可能导致生产数量和生产风险的估计出现偏差和效率低下。遵循Just和Pope方法的总体主题，引入了一个克服原始CES生产函数这一缺陷的基于CES的生产函数规范，并提出了一个用于估计所提出的函数形式的三阶段非线性最小二乘（NLS）估计程序。为了说明本文提出的两种方法，提供了两个实证应用，分别是灌溉和肥料响应，使用了著名的Hexem-Heady实验数据集。最后，讨论了对建模输入驱动的生产风险的影响。

### 价格风险厌恶与收益风险厌恶：通过实验室实验进行性别比较
**[Price risk aversion vs payoff risk aversion: a gender comparison through a laboratory experiment](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.20909)**

**Authors**: Ali Zeytoon-Nejad

**Abstract**: Purpose: This paper explores gender differences in two distinct forms of risk aversion -- Payoff Risk Aversion (PaRA) and Price Risk Aversion (PrRA) -- in order to provide a more nuanced understanding of how men and women respond to different types of economic uncertainty.
Design/methodology/approach: The study employs a laboratory experiment using Multiple-Choice-List (MCL) risk-elicitation tasks based on both Direct Utility Function (DUF) and Indirect Utility Function (IUF) frameworks. These tasks present stochastic payoffs and stochastic prices, respectively. The analysis uses statistical hypothesis testing to compare gender-specific responses across three experimental designs.
Findings: The key results of the study indicate that women typically exhibit higher degrees of PaRA than men, which is a consistent finding with the mainstream literature. However, remarkably, the results from all the three indirect MCL designs show that women typically exhibit lower degrees of PrRA than men, and this result is robust across different MCL designs. The paper also introduces an 'irrationality gap' as the difference between PaRA and PrRA and explores the size of the irrationality gap within either gender group, finding it larger and statistically significant for men, while smaller and statistically insignificant for women.
Originality/value: This study is the first to distinguish between PaRA and PrRA in a gender comparison, using experimentally validated methods. It provides new behavioral insights into the nature of gender-specific risk preferences and introduces the irrationality gap as a novel concept with implications for understanding financial decision-making and the design of gender-sensitive economic policies.

**摘要**: 目的：本文探讨了男性和女性在两种不同形式的风险厌恶——收益风险厌恶（PaRA）和价格风险厌恶（PrRA）——中的性别差异，以提供对男性和女性如何应对不同类型经济不确定性的更细致的理解。
设计/方法论/方法：该研究采用了基于直接效用函数（DUF）和间接效用函数（IUF）框架的多项选择列表（MCL）风险征询任务的实验室实验。这些任务分别呈现了随机收益和随机价格。分析使用统计假设检验来比较三种实验设计中性别特定的反应。
研究发现：研究的关键结果表明，女性通常表现出比男性更高程度的PaRA，这与主流文献的一致发现相符。然而，值得注意的是，所有三个间接MCL设计的结果都显示，女性通常表现出比男性更低程度的PrRA，并且这一结果在不同的MCL设计中都是稳健的。本文还引入了“非理性差距”作为PaRA和PrRA之间的差异，并探索了两性群体中非理性差距的大小，发现男性的非理性差距更大且统计显著，而女性的非理性差距较小且统计不显著。
原创性/价值：这项研究是第一个在性别比较中区分PaRA和PrRA的研究，使用了实验验证的方法。它为理解性别特定的风险偏好的性质提供了新的的行为见解，并引入了非理性差距作为一个新概念，对理解财务决策和设计性别敏感的经济政策具有影响。

### LLM角色作为实地实验在方法基准测试中的替代品
**[LLM Personas as a Substitute for Field Experiments in Method Benchmarking](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.21080)**

**Authors**: Enoch Hyunwook Kang

**Abstract**: Field experiments (A/B tests) are often the most credible benchmark for methods in societal systems, but their cost and latency create a major bottleneck for iterative method development. LLM-based persona simulation offers a cheap synthetic alternative, yet it is unclear whether replacing humans with personas preserves the benchmark interface that adaptive methods optimize against. We prove an if-and-only-if characterization: when (i) methods observe only the aggregate outcome (aggregate-only observation) and (ii) evaluation depends only on the submitted artifact and not on the algorithm's identity or provenance (algorithm-blind evaluation), swapping humans for personas is just panel change from the method's point of view, indistinguishable from changing the evaluation population (e.g., New York to Jakarta). Furthermore, we move from validity to usefulness: we define an information-theoretic discriminability of the induced aggregate channel and show that making persona benchmarking as decision-relevant as a field experiment is fundamentally a sample-size question, yielding explicit bounds on the number of independent persona evaluations required to reliably distinguish meaningfully different methods at a chosen resolution.

**摘要**: 现场实验（A/B测试）通常是社会系统中方法的最可信基准，但它们的成本和延迟为迭代方法开发创造了一个主要瓶颈。基于大型语言模型（LLM）的角色模拟提供了一种廉价的合成替代方案，然而，尚不清楚用角色替换人类是否保留了适应性方法优化的基准接口。我们证明了一个当且仅当的特征：当（i）方法仅观察总体结果（仅总体观察）和（ii）评估仅依赖于提交的工件，而不依赖于算法的身份或出处（算法盲评估）时，从人类到角色的转换对方法来说只是面板变化，与改变评估人群（例如，从纽约到雅加达）无法区分。此外，我们从有效性转向实用性：我们定义了一个信息论的可区分性，用于诱导的总体渠道，并展示了使角色基准测试与现场实验一样具有决策相关性，本质上是一个样本大小问题，为可靠地区分具有意义差异的方法所需的独立角色评估数量提供了明确的界限。
