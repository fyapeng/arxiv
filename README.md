### 智能城市文献中的国际推广模式：探索地理因素在影响本地驱动因素和智能城市成果中的作用
**[International promotion patterns in the smart city literature: Exploring the role of geography in affecting local drivers and smart cities' outcomes](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.12281)**

**Authors**: Filippo Marchesani, Francesca Masciarelli, Andrea Bikfalvi

**Abstract**: The rise of smart cities represents a significant trend in urban development. However, only in recent years has attention shifted toward the international promotion of these cities. Despite ongoing academic discussions on the impact of smart city development on urban environments, the global recognition of smart cities remains uncertain due to their multidisciplinary nature. To address this, we conducted a systematic literature review of articles published in top-tier peer-reviewed journals from 2008 to December 2021, offering a comprehensive analysis of the existing literature.

**摘要**: 智能城市的兴起代表了城市发展的一个重要趋势。然而，直到最近几年，人们才开始关注这些城市的国际推广。尽管有关智能城市发展对城市环境影响的学术讨论一直在进行，但由于其跨学科的特性，全球对智能城市的认可度仍然不确定。为了解决这个问题，我们对2008年至2021年12月发表在顶级同行评审期刊上的文章进行了系统性文献综述，提供了对现有文献的全面分析。

### 针对规模的案例：大型二级基金表现不佳的实证证据
**[The Case against Scale: Empirical Evidence of Underperformance in Large Secondary Funds](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.12436)**

**Authors**: Jitesh Gurav

**Abstract**: The paper analyses the increasing popularity of large funds in the secondary private equity market, which are pegged on the perceived larger scale advantages of operational efficiency and fewer manager relationships (Reuter & Zitzewitz, 2021). However, it has been proved empirically that smaller funds perform better than the big ones in terms of their internal rates of return (IRRs). This work questions this authoritative view, providing evidence of outperformance of smaller funds relative to their larger counterparts. This research shows us the benefits that smaller funds possess. Thus, investors must revisit their allocation strategy to prioritize larger funds automatically (Gualandris et al.,2021).

**摘要**: 本文分析了大型基金在二级私募股权市场中日益增长的受欢迎程度，这种趋势基于对运营效率更高和管理者关系更少的规模优势的感知（Reuter & Zitzewitz, 2021）。然而，已有实证研究表明，小型基金在内部收益率（IRRs）方面的表现优于大型基金。这项工作质疑了这种权威观点，提供了小型基金相对于大型基金表现更优的证据。这项研究向我们展示了小型基金所具有的优势。因此，投资者必须重新审视他们的配置策略，以自动优先考虑大型基金（Gualandris et al.,2021）。

### 利用图注意力神经网络捕捉串标卡特尔
**[Catching Bid-rigging Cartels with Graph Attention Neural Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.12369)**

**Authors**: David Imhof, Emanuel W Viklund, Martin Huber

**Abstract**: We propose a novel application of graph attention networks (GATs), a type of graph neural network enhanced with attention mechanisms, to develop a deep learning algorithm for detecting collusive behavior, leveraging predictive features suggested in prior research. We test our approach on a large dataset covering 13 markets across seven countries. Our results show that predictive models based on GATs, trained on a subset of the markets, can be effectively transferred to other markets, achieving accuracy rates between 80\% and 90\%, depending on the hyperparameter settings. The best-performing configuration, applied to eight markets from Switzerland and the Japanese region of Okinawa, yields an average accuracy of 91% for cross-market prediction. When extended to 12 markets, the method maintains a strong performance with an average accuracy of 84\%, surpassing traditional ensemble approaches in machine learning. These results suggest that GAT-based detection methods offer a promising tool for competition authorities to screen markets for potential cartel activity.

**摘要**: 我们提出了一种新颖的图注意力网络（GATs）应用，这是一种增强了注意力机制的图神经网络，用于开发深度学习算法，以检测串通行为，利用先前研究中建议的预测特征。我们在涵盖七个国家13个市场的大数据集上测试了我们的方法。我们的结果显示，基于GATs的预测模型，训练在市场的子集上，可以有效地转移到其他市场，准确率在80%到90%之间，具体取决于超参数设置。表现最佳的配置应用于瑞士的八个市场和日本冲绳地区，平均准确率达到91%，用于跨市场预测。当扩展到12个市场时，该方法保持了强大的性能，平均准确率为84%，超过了机器学习中传统的集成方法。这些结果表明，基于GAT的检测方法为竞争管理机构筛查市场潜在的卡特尔活动提供了一个有希望的工具。

### 预测气候政策的不确定性：来自美国的证据
**[Forecasting Climate Policy Uncertainty: Evidence from the United States](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.12276)**

**Authors**: Donia Besher, Anirban Sengupta, Tanujit Chakraborty

**Abstract**: Forecasting Climate Policy Uncertainty (CPU) is essential as policymakers strive to balance economic growth with environmental goals. High levels of CPU can slow down investments in green technologies, make regulatory planning more difficult, and increase public resistance to climate reforms, especially during times of economic stress. This study addresses the challenge of forecasting the US CPU index by building the Bayesian Structural Time Series (BSTS) model with a large set of covariates, including economic indicators, financial cycle data, and public sentiments captured through Google Trends. The key strength of the BSTS model lies in its ability to efficiently manage a large number of covariates through its dynamic feature selection mechanism based on the spike-and-slab prior. To validate the effectiveness of the selected features of the BSTS model, an impulse response analysis is performed. The results show that macro-financial shocks impact CPU in different ways over time. Numerical experiments are performed to evaluate the performance of the BSTS model with exogenous variables on the US CPU dataset over different forecasting horizons. The empirical results confirm that BSTS consistently outperforms classical and deep learning frameworks, particularly for semi-long-term and long-term forecasts.

**摘要**: 预测气候政策不确定性（CPU）对于政策制定者在追求经济增长与环境目标之间取得平衡至关重要。高水平的CPU可能会减缓对绿色技术的投资，使监管规划变得更加困难，并在经济压力时期增加公众对气候改革的抵制。本研究通过构建包含大量协变量的贝叶斯结构时间序列（BSTS）模型来解决预测美国CPU指数的挑战，这些协变量包括经济指标、金融周期数据以及通过谷歌趋势捕获的公众情绪。BSTS模型的关键优势在于其能够通过基于尖峰-板条先验的动态特征选择机制高效管理大量协变量。为了验证BSTS模型选定特征的有效性，进行了脉冲响应分析。结果显示，宏观金融冲击在不同时间对CPU的影响各不相同。进行数值实验以评估BSTS模型在不同预测时限下对美国CPU数据集的外生变量性能。实证结果证实，BSTS在半长期和长期预测中持续优于经典和深度学习框架。

### 使用新颖的机器学习方法对电力市场自由化对电价的因果关系分析
**[Causality analysis of electricity market liberalization on electricity price using novel Machine Learning methods](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.12331)**

**Authors**: Orr Shahar, Stefan Lessmann, Daniel Traian Pele

**Abstract**: Relationships between the energy and the finance markets are increasingly important. Understanding these relationships is vital for policymakers and other stakeholders as the world faces challenges such as satisfying humanity's increasing need for energy and the effects of climate change. In this paper, we investigate the causal effect of electricity market liberalization on the electricity price in the US. By performing this analysis, we aim to provide new insights into the ongoing debate about the benefits of electricity market liberalization. We introduce Causal Machine Learning as a new approach for interventions in the energy-finance field. The development of machine learning in recent years opened the door for a new branch of machine learning models for causality impact, with the ability to extract complex patterns and relationships from the data. We discuss the advantages of causal ML methods and compare the performance of ML-based models to shed light on the applicability of causal ML frameworks to energy policy intervention cases. We find that the DeepProbCP framework outperforms the other frameworks examined. In addition, we find that liberalization of, and individual players' entry to, the electricity market resulted in a 7% decrease in price in the short term.

**摘要**: 能源市场和金融市场之间的关系日益重要。随着世界面临满足人类日益增长的能源需求和气候变化影响等挑战，理解这些关系对于政策制定者和其他利益相关者至关重要。在本文中，我们调查了电力市场自由化对美国电力价格的因果效应。通过进行这项分析，我们旨在为关于电力市场自由化益处的持续辩论提供新的见解。我们引入因果机器学习作为能源金融领域干预的新方法。近年来机器学习的发展为因果影响的机器学习模型开辟了新领域，这些模型能够从数据中提取复杂的模式和关系。我们讨论了因果ML方法的优势，并将基于ML的模型性能进行比较，以阐明因果ML框架在能源政策干预案例中的适用性。我们发现DeepProbCP框架的表现优于其他检查的框架。此外，我们发现电力市场的自由化以及个别参与者进入电力市场在短期内导致了价格下降7%。

### 策划智慧城市的实施
**[Orchestrating the Implementation of the Smart City](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.12267)**

**Authors**: Filippo Marchesani

**Abstract**: This chapter explores the six core dimensions of smart cities (i.e. smart economy, mobility, environment, people, living, and governance) emphasizing their interdependence and the need for holistic orchestration. Building on Giffinger et al. (2007) and subsequent literature, it argues that integrating these dimensions is crucial for sustainable urban development. ICT plays a key enabling role but must be complemented by human and social capital. Through institutional examples, such as the creation of dedicated municipal offices for digital innovation, the chapter illustrates how governance and internal capacity shape smart transitions. A human-centric approach is also essential, ensuring inclusivity, creativity, and active civic participation. Ultimately, smart cities must be viewed as cohesive urban ecosystems where technology, people, and governance interact dynamically.

**摘要**: 本章探讨了智慧城市的六个核心维度（即智能经济、移动性、环境、人、生活和治理），强调了它们的相互依存性以及整体协调的必要性。在Giffinger等人（2007年）及其后续文献的基础上，文章认为整合这些维度对于可持续的城市发展至关重要。信息通信技术（ICT）发挥着关键的促进作用，但必须辅以人力和社会资本。通过制度实例，例如创建专门的市政数字创新办公室，本章说明了治理和内部能力如何塑造智能转型。以人为本的方法也是必不可少的，确保包容性、创造力和积极的公民参与。最终，智慧城市必须被视为技术、人和治理动态互动的连贯城市生态系统。

### 高频数据同步
**[Data Synchronization at High Frequencies](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.12220)**

**Authors**: Xinbing Kong, Cheng Liu, Bin Wu

**Abstract**: Asynchronous trading in high-frequency financial markets introduces significant biases into econometric analysis, distorting risk estimates and leading to suboptimal portfolio decisions. Existing synchronization methods, such as the previous-tick approach, suffer from information loss and create artificial price staleness. We introduce a novel framework that recasts the data synchronization challenge as a constrained matrix completion problem. Our approach recovers the potential matrix of high-frequency price increments by minimizing its nuclear norm -- capturing the underlying low-rank factor structure -- subject to a large-scale linear system derived from observed, asynchronous price changes. Theoretically, we prove the existence and uniqueness of our estimator and establish its convergence rate. A key theoretical insight is that our method accurately and robustly leverages information from both frequently and infrequently traded assets, overcoming a critical difficulty of efficiency loss in traditional methods. Empirically, using extensive simulations and a large panel of S&P 500 stocks, we demonstrate that our method substantially outperforms established benchmarks. It not only achieves significantly lower synchronization errors, but also corrects the bias in systematic risk estimates (i.e., eigenvalues) and the estimate of betas caused by stale prices. Crucially, portfolios constructed using our synchronized data yield consistently and economically significant higher out-of-sample Sharpe ratios. Our framework provides a powerful tool for uncovering the true dynamics of asset prices, with direct implications for high-frequency risk management, algorithmic trading, and econometric inference.

**摘要**: 高频金融市场中的异步交易引入了显著的偏差，扭曲了风险估计，并导致次优的投资组合决策。现有的同步方法，如前一报价方法，因信息丢失和人为创造价格陈旧而受到限制。我们引入了一个新颖的框架，将数据同步挑战重新定义为一个受约束的矩阵补全问题。我们的方法通过最小化其核范数——捕捉潜在的低秩因子结构——来恢复高频价格增量的可能矩阵，同时满足从观察到的异步价格变化中派生出的大规模线性系统。在理论上，我们证明了我们的估计器的存在性和唯一性，并建立了其收敛速度。一个关键的理论洞见是，我们的方法能够准确且稳健地利用频繁和不频繁交易资产的信息，克服了传统方法中效率损失的关键困难。从实证上看，通过广泛的模拟和大量标准普尔500指数股票的数据，我们展示了我们的方法显著优于既定基准。它不仅实现了显著较低的同步误差，还纠正了由于价格陈旧导致的系统性风险估计（即特征值）和贝塔估计的偏差。至关重要的是，使用我们同步数据构建的投资组合在样本外持续且经济上显著地获得了更高的夏普比率。我们的框架为揭示资产价格的真实动态提供了一个强大的工具，对高频风险管理、算法交易和计量经济学推断具有直接的影响。

### 通过平滑对最优政策值和其他不规则泛函进行推断
**[Inference on Optimal Policy Values and Other Irregular Functionals via Smoothing](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.11780)**

**Authors**: Justin Whitehouse, Morgane Austern, Vasilis Syrgkanis

**Abstract**: Constructing confidence intervals for the value of an optimal treatment policy is an important problem in causal inference. Insight into the optimal policy value can guide the development of reward-maximizing, individualized treatment regimes. However, because the functional that defines the optimal value is non-differentiable, standard semi-parametric approaches for performing inference fail to be directly applicable. Existing approaches for handling this non-differentiability fall roughly into two camps. In one camp are estimators based on constructing smooth approximations of the optimal value. These approaches are computationally lightweight, but typically place unrealistic parametric assumptions on outcome regressions. In another camp are approaches that directly de-bias the non-smooth objective. These approaches don't place parametric assumptions on nuisance functions, but they either require the computation of intractably-many nuisance estimates, assume unrealistic $L^\infty$ nuisance convergence rates, or make strong margin assumptions that prohibit non-response to a treatment. In this paper, we revisit the problem of constructing smooth approximations of non-differentiable functionals. By carefully controlling first-order bias and second-order remainders, we show that a softmax smoothing-based estimator can be used to estimate parameters that are specified as a maximum of scores involving nuisance components. In particular, this includes the value of the optimal treatment policy as a special case. Our estimator obtains $\sqrt{n}$ convergence rates, avoids parametric restrictions/unrealistic margin assumptions, and is often statistically efficient.

**摘要**: 在因果推断中，构建最优治疗政策价值的置信区间是一个重要问题。对最优政策价值的洞察可以指导开发最大化奖励、个性化的治疗制度。然而，由于定义最优价值的函数是非可微的，标准的半参数推断方法无法直接应用。现有处理这种非可微性的方法大致分为两类。一类是基于构建最优价值的平滑近似的估计器。这些方法计算量小，但通常对结果回归施加不切实际的参数假设。另一类方法是直接去偏非平滑目标。这些方法不对干扰函数施加参数假设，但它们要么需要计算难以处理的大量干扰估计，假设不切实际的$L^\infty$干扰收敛速率，或者做出强烈的边际假设，禁止对治疗的无响应。在本文中，我们重新审视构建非可微函数的平滑近似的问题。通过仔细控制一阶偏差和二阶余项，我们展示了基于softmax平滑的估计器可以用来估计被指定为涉及干扰分量的分数最大值的参数。特别地，这包括作为特例的最优治疗政策的价值。我们的估计器获得了$\sqrt{n}$的收敛速率，避免了参数限制/不切实际的边际假设，并且在统计上通常是有效的。
