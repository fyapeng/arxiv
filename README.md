### 具有内生同伴选择的离散选择
**[Discrete Choice with Endogenous Peer Selection](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.21446)**

**Authors**: Nail Kashaev, Natalia Lazzati

**Abstract**: We develop a continuous-time peer-effect discrete choice model where peers that affect the preferences of a given agent are randomly selected based on their previous choices. We characterize the equilibrium behavior and study the empirical content of the model. In the model, changes in the choices of peers affect both the set of peers the agent pays attention to and her preferences over the alternatives. We exploit variation in choices coupled with variation in the size of the set of potential peers to recover agents' preferences and the peer selection mechanism. These nonparametric identification results do not rely on exogenous variation of covariates.

**摘要**: 我们开发了一个连续时间的同伴效应离散选择模型，其中影响给定代理偏好的同伴是基于他们之前的选择随机选择的。我们描述了均衡行为并研究了模型的经验内容。在模型中，同伴选择的变化既影响代理关注的同伴群体，也影响她对替代品的偏好。我们利用选择的变化与潜在同伴群体规模的变化来恢复代理的偏好和同伴选择机制。这些非参数识别结果不依赖于协变量的外生变化。

### 隐私受限信号
**[Privacy-Constrained Signals](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.21196)**

**Authors**: Zhang Xu, Wei Zhao

**Abstract**: This paper provides a unified approach to characterize the set of all feasible signals subject to privacy constraints. The Blackwell frontier of feasible signals can be decomposed into minimum informative signals achieving the Blackwell frontier of privacy variables, and conditionally privacy-preserving signals. A complete characterization of the minimum informative signals is then provided. We apply the framework to ex-post privacy (including differential and inferential privacy) and to constraints on posterior means of arbitrary statistics.

**摘要**: 本文提供了一种统一的方法来描述所有在隐私约束下可行信号的集合。可行信号的Blackwell边界可以分解为实现隐私变量Blackwell边界的最小信息信号，以及条件隐私保护信号。然后，我们提供了最小信息信号的完整特征描述。我们将该框架应用于事后隐私（包括差分隐私和推理隐私）以及对任意统计量的后验均值的约束。

### 电力系统中具有随机需求的储能理论
**[The Theory of Storage in a Power System with Stochastic Demand](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.21327)**

**Authors**: Darryl Biggar, Mohammad Reza Hesamzadeh

**Abstract**: Electric power systems are increasingly turning to energy storage systems to balance supply and demand. But how much storage is required? What is the optimal volume of storage in a power system and on what does it depend? In addition, what form of hedge contracts do storage facilities require? We answer these questions in the special case in which the uncertainty in the power system involves successive draws of an independent, identically-distributed random variable. We characterize the conditions for the optimal operation of, and investment in, storage and show how these conditions can be understood graphically using price-duration curves. We also characterize the optimal hedge contracts for storage units.

**摘要**: 电力系统越来越多地转向储能系统以平衡供需。但是需要多少储能呢？电力系统中最优的储能容量是多少，它又取决于什么？此外，储能设施需要何种形式的对冲合同？我们在电力系统中的不确定性涉及独立、同分布随机变量连续抽取的特殊情况中回答这些问题。我们描述了储能的最佳运行和投资的条件，并展示如何使用价格-持续时间曲线图形化地理解这些条件。我们还描述了储能单位的最优对冲合同。

### 高维线性回归中的估计：后双重自回归作为后双重套索的替代方法
**[Estimation in high-dimensional linear regression: Post-Double-Autometrics as an alternative to Post-Double-Lasso](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.21257)**

**Authors**: Sullivan Hué, Sébastien Laurent, Ulrich Aiounou, Emmanuel Flachaire

**Abstract**: Post-Double-Lasso is becoming the most popular method for estimating linear regression models with many covariates when the purpose is to obtain an accurate estimate of a parameter of interest, such as an average treatment effect. However, this method can suffer from substantial omitted variable bias in finite sample. We propose a new method called Post-Double-Autometrics, which is based on Autometrics, and show that this method outperforms Post-Double-Lasso. Its use in a standard application of economic growth sheds new light on the hypothesis of convergence from poor to rich economies.

**摘要**: 后双重套索（Post-Double-Lasso）已成为在许多协变量的情况下估计线性回归模型的最流行方法，当目的是获得感兴趣的参数的准确估计时，例如平均处理效应。然而，这种方法在有限样本中可能会受到大量的遗漏变量偏差的影响。我们提出了一种新的方法，称为后双重自动计量学（Post-Double-Autometrics），该方法基于自动计量学，并表明这种方法优于后双重套索。其在经济增长的标准应用中使用，为从贫困经济体到富裕经济体的收敛假设提供了新的视角。

### 一种广义控制函数方法在生产函数估计中的应用
**[A Generalized Control Function Approach to Production Function Estimation](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.21578)**

**Authors**: Ulrich Doraszelski, Lixiong Li

**Abstract**: We generalize the control function approach to production function estimation. Our generalization accommodates scenarios in which productivity evolves jointly with other unobservable factors such as latent demand shocks and the invertibility assumption underpinning the traditional proxy variable approach fails. We provide conditions under which the output elasticity of the variable input -- and hence the markup -- is nonparametrically point-identified. A Neyman orthogonal moment condition ensures oracle efficiency of our GMM estimator. A Monte Carlo exercise shows a large bias for the traditional proxy variable approach that decreases rapidly and nearly vanishes for our generalized control function approach.

**摘要**: 我们将控制函数方法推广到生产函数估计中。我们的推广适用于生产率与其他不可观测因素（如潜在需求冲击）共同演变的场景，以及在传统代理变量方法背后的可逆性假设失败的情况下。我们提供了在何种条件下，可变输入的产出弹性——因此是加成——是非参数点识别的条件。一个奈曼正交矩条件确保了我们GMM估计器的先知效率。一个蒙特卡洛实验显示，传统代理变量方法存在较大偏差，而我们的推广控制函数方法的偏差迅速减小，几乎消失。

### 具有一般禀赋的不可分割物品分配问题的一致性解决方案
**[Consistent solutions to the allocation of indivisible objects with general endowments](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.21155)**

**Authors**: Jun Zhang

**Abstract**: We apply the consistency principle to examine various core concepts in a general allocation model that subsumes several familiar market design models as special cases. The conventional strong core is consistent but may be empty, whereas the exclusion core proposed by Balbuzanov and Kotowski (2019), although nonempty, is not consistent and may include unintuitive allocations. We therefore propose a refinement of the exclusion core, which is both nonempty and consistent. Our solution offers sharper predictions than existing alternatives and coincides with the strong core and/or the exclusion core in special cases that generalize familiar models.

**摘要**: 我们应用一致性原则来检验一个一般分配模型中的各种核心概念，该模型包含了几个熟悉的市场设计模型作为特殊情况。传统的强核心是一致的，但可能是空的，而Balbuzanov和Kotowski（2019）提出的排除核心虽然非空，但不一致，并且可能包括一些非直观的分配。因此，我们提出了排除核心的一种细化，它既非空又一致。我们的解决方案提供了比现有选择更精确的预测，并且在概括熟悉模型的特殊情况中与强核心和/或排除核心相吻合。

### 庇护分配与负担共享
**[Asylum Assignment and Burden-Sharing](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.21147)**

**Authors**: Gian Caspari, Manshu Khanna

**Abstract**: We analyze the problem of matching asylum seekers to member states, incorporating wait times, preferences of asylum seekers, and the priorities, capacities, and burden-sharing commitments of member states. We identify a unique choice rule that addresses feasibility while balancing priorities and capacities. We examine the effects of both homogeneous and heterogeneous burden-sizes among asylum seekers on the matching process. Our main result shows that when all asylum seekers are treated as having identical burden-sizes, the asylum-seeker-proposing cumulative offer mechanism guarantees both stability and strategy-proofness. In contrast, when burden-sizes vary, there are scenarios where achieving stability or strategy-proofness is no longer possible.

**摘要**: 我们分析了将寻求庇护者与成员国匹配的问题，考虑了等待时间、寻求庇护者的偏好以及成员国的优先事项、容量和分担承诺。我们确定了一种独特的选择规则，它在平衡优先事项和容量的同时解决了可行性问题。我们检验了寻求庇护者中同质和异质负担规模对匹配过程的影响。我们的主要结果显示，当所有寻求庇护者被视为具有相同负担规模时，寻求庇护者提议的累积出价机制保证了稳定性和策略证明性。相比之下，当负担规模不同时，存在无法实现稳定性或策略证明性的场景。

### 关于具有完整偏好的夏普利-斯卡夫住房市场模型的核心
**[On the core of the Shapley-Scarf housing market model with full preferences](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.21158)**

**Authors**: Jun Zhang

**Abstract**: We examine core concepts in the classical model of \cite{shapley1974cores} under full preferences. Among the standard notions, the strong core may be empty, whereas the weak core, though always nonempty, can be overly large and include unreasonable allocations. Our main findings are: (1) The exclusion core of Balbuzanov and Kotowski (2019) -- a recent concept shown to outperform standard cores in complex environments under strict preferences -- can also be empty. We establish a necessary and sufficient condition for its nonemptiness, showing that it is more often nonempty than the strong core. (2) We introduce two new core concepts, built on the exclusion core and the strong core respectively, by refining the assumptions on how indifferent agents may block. Both are nonempty and Pareto efficient, and coincide with the strong core whenever the latter is nonempty. (3) These core concepts are ordered by set inclusion, with the strong core as the smallest and the weak core as the largest.

**摘要**: 我们在完全偏好的古典模型中检验了核心概念。在标准概念中，强核心可能是空的，而弱核心虽然总是非空的，但可能过于庞大，包括不合理的分配。我们的主要发现是：（1）Balbuzanov和Kotowski（2019）提出的排除核心——一个在严格偏好下复杂环境中被证明优于标准核心的新概念——也可能是空的。我们建立了其非空的必要和充分条件，表明它比强核心更经常是非空的。（2）我们引入了两个新的核心概念，分别建立在排除核心和强核心之上，通过细化对无差异代理可能阻止的假设。这两个概念都是非空的和帕累托有效的，并且在强核心非空的情况下与后者重合。（3）这些核心概念按集合包含关系排序，强核心是最小的，弱核心是最大的。

### 连带责任是否减少了存在代理问题的竞赛中的作弊行为？理论与实验证据
**[Does joint liability reduce cheating in contests with agency problems? Theory and experimental evidence](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.21090)**

**Authors**: Qin Wu, Ralph-C Bayer

**Abstract**: Contest participants often have strong incentives to engage in cheating. Sanctions serve as a common deterrent against such conduct. Often, other agents on the contestant's team (e.g., a coach of an athlete) or a company (a manager of an R\&D engineer) have a vested interest in outcomes and can influence the cheating decision. An agency problem arises when only the contestant faces the penalties for cheating. Our theoretical framework examines joint liability, i.e., shifting some responsibility from the contestant to the other agent, as a solution to this agency problem. Equilibrium analysis shows that extending liability reduces cheating if fines are harsh. Less intuitively, when fines are lenient, a shift in liability can lead to an increase in equilibrium cheating rates. Experimental tests confirm that joint liability is effective in reducing cheating if fines are high. However, the predicted detrimental effect of joint liability for low fines does not occur.

**摘要**: 竞赛参与者通常有强烈的动机去作弊。制裁作为一种常见的威慑手段来对抗这种行为。通常，参赛者团队中的其他代理人（例如，运动员的教练）或公司（研发工程师的经理）对结果有既得利益，并且可以影响作弊决策。当只有参赛者面临作弊的惩罚时，就会出现代理问题。我们的理论框架检验了联合责任，即从参赛者转移到其他代理人身上的责任，作为解决这一代理问题的方法。均衡分析表明，如果罚款严厉，扩大责任范围可以减少作弊行为。不那么直观的是，当罚款宽松时，责任的转移可能会导致均衡作弊率的增加。实验测试证实，如果罚款高，联合责任在减少作弊方面是有效的。然而，对于低罚款，联合责任预测的不利影响并没有发生。

### 在电力市场中，自动化市场力量缓解下的策略性出价响应
**[Strategic bid response under automated market power mitigation in electricity markets](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.20812)**

**Authors**: Chiara Fusar Bassini, Jacqueline Adelowo, Priya L. Donti, Lynn H. Kaack

**Abstract**: In auction markets that are prone to market power abuse, preventive mitigation of bid prices can be applied through automated mitigation procedures (AMP). Despite the widespread application of AMP in US electricity markets, there exists scarce evidence on how firms strategically react to such price-cap-and-penalty regulation: when the price cap rarely leads to penalty mitigation, it is difficult to distinguish whether AMP are an effective deterrent or simply too lax. We investigate their impact on the bids of generation firms, using 2019 data from the New York and New England electricity markets (NYISO, ISO-NE). We employ a regression discontinuity design, which exploits the fact that the price cap with penalty is only activated when a structural index (e.g., congestion, pivotality) exceeds a certain cutoff. By estimating the Local Average Treatment Effect (LATE) of screening activation, we can causally identify successful deterrence of anti-competitive behavior. Around 30-40% of the analyzed bidders per market exhibit a significant strategic response - corresponding to a decrease in maximum bid prices of 4-10 $/MWh to avoid the penalty. However, there is significant heterogeneity between firms, and the regulatory impact on the overall market is not statistically detectable, suggesting lax mitigation thresholds. Using a merit-order simulation, we estimate the welfare impact of more stringent thresholds to lie between 350 and 980 thousand dollars of increased buyer surplus per mitigated hour, with the associated number of mitigated hours being below 33 hours/year. Our results motivate the empirical calibration of mitigation thresholds to improve the efficiency of AMP regulation.

**摘要**: 在容易发生市场权力滥用的拍卖市场中，可以通过自动化缓解程序（AMP）预防性地降低出价。尽管AMP在美国电力市场的广泛应用，但关于公司如何对这种价格上限和惩罚规定做出战略反应的证据却很少：当价格上限很少导致惩罚缓解时，很难区分AMP是否是有效的威慑，或者仅仅是过于宽松。我们利用2019年纽约和新英格兰电力市场（NYISO，ISO-NE）的数据，研究它们对发电公司出价的影响。我们采用了回归不连续设计，利用价格上限和惩罚只有在结构性指数（例如，拥堵，关键性）超过某个截止值时才被激活的事实。通过估计筛选激活的局部平均处理效应（LATE），我们可以因果地识别出对反竞争行为的成功威慑。在每个市场中，大约30-40%的被分析出价者表现出显著的战略反应——对应于为了避免惩罚，最大出价价格降低了4-10美元/兆瓦时。然而，公司之间存在显著的异质性，监管对整体市场的影响在统计上无法检测，这表明缓解阈值过于宽松。通过使用功绩顺序模拟，我们估计更严格的阈值对福利的影响在每小时缓解增加的买家剩余中介于35万至98万美元之间，相关的缓解小时数低于每年33小时。我们的结果激发了对缓解阈值进行实证校准，以提高AMP监管的效率。

### 基于进化的干扰下实验模型
**[On Evolution-Based Models for Experimentation Under Interference](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.21675)**

**Authors**: Sadegh Shirani, Mohsen Bayati

**Abstract**: Causal effect estimation in networked systems is central to data-driven decision making. In such settings, interventions on one unit can spill over to others, and in complex physical or social systems, the interaction pathways driving these interference structures remain largely unobserved. We argue that for identifying population-level causal effects, it is not necessary to recover the exact network structure; instead, it suffices to characterize how those interactions contribute to the evolution of outcomes. Building on this principle, we study an evolution-based approach that investigates how outcomes change across observation rounds in response to interventions, hence compensating for missing network information. Using an exposure-mapping perspective, we give an axiomatic characterization of when the empirical distribution of outcomes follows a low-dimensional recursive equation, and identify minimal structural conditions under which such evolution mappings exist. We frame this as a distributional counterpart to difference-in-differences. Rather than assuming parallel paths for individual units, it exploits parallel evolution patterns across treatment scenarios to estimate counterfactual trajectories. A key insight is that treatment randomization plays a role beyond eliminating latent confounding; it induces an implicit sampling from hidden interference channels, enabling consistent learning about heterogeneous spillover effects. We highlight causal message passing as an instantiation of this method in dense networks while extending to more general interference structures, including influencer networks where a small set of units drives most spillovers. Finally, we discuss the limits of this approach, showing that strong temporal trends or endogenous interference can undermine identification.

**摘要**: 在网络化系统中估计因果效应对于数据驱动的决策至关重要。在这样的环境中，对一个单元的干预可能会波及到其他单元，而在复杂的物理或社会系统中，推动这些干扰结构的互动路径在很大程度上是未被观察到的。我们认为，为了识别人口层面的因果效应，并不必要恢复确切的网络结构；相反，只需描述这些互动如何促成结果的演变。基于这一原则，我们研究了一种基于演变的方法，该方法调查了在干预响应下结果如何在观察轮次中发生变化，从而补偿了缺失的网络信息。利用暴露映射的视角，我们给出了一个公理化的特征描述，即何时结果的经验分布遵循低维递归方程，并确定了存在这种演变映射的最小结构条件。我们将此框架定义为差异中的差异的分布对应物。它不是假设个体单元的平行路径，而是利用跨治疗场景的平行演变模式来估计反事实轨迹。一个关键的洞见是，治疗随机化的作用超出了消除潜在混杂因素；它诱导了从隐藏的干扰通道中隐式抽样，使得能够一致地学习关于异质性溢出效应的信息。我们强调因果信息传递是这种方法在密集网络中的一个实例，同时扩展到更一般的干扰结构，包括影响者网络，其中一小部分单元驱动了大多数溢出。最后，我们讨论了这种方法的局限性，表明强烈的时间趋势或内生干扰可能会破坏识别。

### 希尔伯特空间中相互作用粒子系统的随机最优控制及其应用
**[Stochastic Optimal Control of Interacting Particle Systems in Hilbert Spaces and Applications](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.21646)**

**Authors**: Filippo de Feo, Fausto Gozzi, Andrzej Święch, Lukas Wessels

**Abstract**: Optimal control of interacting particles governed by stochastic evolution equations in Hilbert spaces is an open area of research. Such systems naturally arise in formulations where each particle is modeled by stochastic partial differential equations, path-dependent stochastic differential equations (such as stochastic delay differential equations or stochastic Volterra integral equations), or partially observed stochastic systems. The purpose of this manuscript is to build the foundations for a limiting theory as the number of particles tends to infinity. We prove the convergence of the value functions $u_n$ of finite particle systems to a function $\mathcal{V}$, {which} is the unique {$L$}-viscosity solution of the corresponding mean-field Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman equation {in the space of probability measures}, and we identify its lift with the value function $U$ of the so-called ``lifted'' limit optimal control problem. Under suitable additional assumptions, we show $C^{1,1}$-regularity of $U$, we prove that $\mathcal{V}$ projects precisely onto the value functions $u_n$, and that optimal (resp. optimal feedback) controls of the particle system correspond to optimal (resp. optimal feedback) controls of the lifted control problem started at the corresponding initial condition. To the best of our knowledge, these are the first results of this kind for stochastic optimal control problems for interacting particle systems of stochastic evolution equations in Hilbert spaces. We apply the developed theory to problems arising in economics where the particles are modeled by stochastic delay differential equations and stochastic partial differential equations.

**摘要**: 在希尔伯特空间中，由随机演化方程控制的相互作用粒子的最优控制是一个开放的研究领域。这类系统自然出现在每个粒子都由随机偏微分方程、路径依赖的随机微分方程（例如随机延迟微分方程或随机Volterra积分方程）或部分观测的随机系统建模的表述中。本文的目的是为粒子数量趋于无穷大时的极限理论奠定基础。我们证明了有限粒子系统的值函数$u_n$收敛到函数$\mathcal{V}$，该函数是相应平均场哈密顿-雅可比-贝尔曼方程在概率测度空间中的唯一$L$-粘性解，我们将其提升与所谓的“提升”极限最优控制问题的值函数$U$识别。在适当的额外假设下，我们展示了$U$的$C^{1,1}$-正则性，我们证明了$\mathcal{V}$精确地投影到值函数$u_n$上，并且粒子系统的最优（分别对应最优反馈）控制对应于从相应初始条件开始的提升控制问题的最优（分别对应最优反馈）控制。据我们所知，这些是希尔伯特空间中随机演化方程的相互作用粒子系统的随机最优控制问题的第一类结果。我们将发展的理论研究应用于经济学中的问题，其中粒子由随机延迟微分方程和随机偏微分方程建模。
