### 在评估决策中选择和使用信息
**[Choosing and Using Information in Evaluation Decisions](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.13798)**

**Authors**: Katherine B. Coffman, Scott Kostyshak, Perihan O. Saygin

**Abstract**: We use a controlled experiment to study how information acquisition impacts candidate evaluations. We provide evaluators with group-level information on performance and the opportunity to acquire additional, individual-level performance information before making a final evaluation. We find that, on average, evaluators under-acquire individual-level information, leading to more stereotypical evaluations of candidates. Consistent with stereotyping, we find that (irrelevant) group-level comparisons have a significant impact on how candidates are evaluated; group-level comparisons bias initial assessments, responses to information, and final evaluations. This leads to under-recognition of talented candidates from comparatively weaker groups and over-selection of untalented candidates from comparatively stronger groups.

**摘要**: 我们通过控制实验来研究信息获取如何影响候选人评估。我们为评估者提供有关表现的群体层面信息，并在做出最终评估之前提供获取额外的个体层面表现信息的机会。我们发现，平均而言，评估者对个体层面信息的获取不足，导致对候选人的评价更加刻板。与刻板印象一致，我们发现（无关的）群体层面比较对候选人的评价有显著影响；群体层面比较会偏倚初始评估、对信息的反应和最终评估。这导致对来自相对较弱群体的有才华候选人的认可不足，以及对来自相对较强群体的无才华候选人的过度选择。

### 解析游说领域：来自意见动态模型的洞见
**[Navigating the Lobbying Landscape: Insights from Opinion Dynamics Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.13767)**

**Authors**: Daniele Giachini, Leonardo Ciambezi Verdiana Del Rosso, Fabrizio Fornari, Valentina Pansanella, Lilit Popoyan, Alina Sîrbu

**Abstract**: While lobbying has been demonstrated to have an important effect on public opinion and policy making, existing models of opinion formation do not specifically include its effect. In this work we introduce a new model of opinion dynamics where lobbyists can implement complex strategies and are characterised by a finite budget. Individuals update their opinions through a learning process resembling Bayesian learning, but influenced by cognitive biases such as under-reaction and confirmation bias. We study the model numerically and demonstrate rich dynamics both with and without lobbyists. In the presence of lobbying, we observe two regimes: one in which lobbyists can have full influence on the agent network, and another where the peer-effect generates polarisation. When symmetric lobbyists are present, the lobbyist influence regime is characterised by long opinion oscillations, while in the transition area between the two regimes we observe convergence to the optimistic model when the lobbying influence is long enough. These rich dynamics pave the way for studying real lobbying strategies to validate the model in practice.

**摘要**: 尽管游说已被证明对公众舆论和政策制定有重要影响，但现有的意见形成模型并未特别包含其影响。在这项工作中，我们引入了一个新的意见动态模型，其中游说者可以实施复杂策略，并受有限预算的约束。个体通过类似于贝叶斯学习的学习过程更新他们的意见，但受到认知偏见的影响，如反应不足和确认偏见。我们通过数值研究该模型，并展示了有无游说者情况下的丰富动态。在游说存在的情况下，我们观察到两种状态：一种是游说者可以对代理网络产生完全影响的状态，另一种是同伴效应产生极化的状态。当存在对称游说者时，游说者影响状态的特征是意见的长期振荡，而在两种状态之间的过渡区域，当游说影响足够长时，我们观察到向乐观模型的收敛。这些丰富的动态为研究实际游说策略以验证模型铺平了道路。

### 谁与谁？学习最优匹配政策
**[Who With Whom? Learning Optimal Matching Policies](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.13567)**

**Authors**: Yagan Hazard, Toru Kitagawa

**Abstract**: There are many economic contexts where the productivity and welfare performance of institutions and policies depend on who matches with whom. Examples include caseworkers and job seekers in job search assistance programs, medical doctors and patients, teachers and students, attorneys and defendants, and tax auditors and taxpayers, among others. Although reallocating individuals through a change in matching policy can be less costly than training personnel or introducing a new program, methods for learning optimal matching policies and their statistical performance are less studied than methods for other policy interventions. This paper develops a method to learn welfare optimal matching policies for two-sided matching problems in which a planner matches individuals based on the rich set of observable characteristics of the two sides. We formulate the learning problem as an empirical optimal transport problem with a match cost function estimated from training data, and propose estimating an optimal matching policy by maximizing the entropy regularized empirical welfare criterion. We derive a welfare regret bound for the estimated policy and characterize its convergence. We apply our proposal to the problem of matching caseworkers and job seekers in a job search assistance program, and assess its welfare performance in a simulation study calibrated with French administrative data.

**摘要**: 在许多经济环境中，机构和政策的生产力和福利表现取决于谁与谁匹配。例子包括求职援助计划中的个案工作者和求职者、医生和病人、教师和学生、律师和被告，以及税务审计员和纳税人等。尽管通过改变匹配政策重新分配个体可能比培训人员或引入新计划的成本更低，但学习最优匹配政策及其统计表现的方法比其他政策干预方法的研究要少。本文开发了一种方法，用于学习基于双方丰富的可观察特征匹配个体的双边匹配问题的福利最优匹配政策。我们将学习问题表述为一个经验最优运输问题，其中匹配成本函数是从训练数据中估计的，并提出通过最大化熵正则化的经验福利标准来估计最优匹配政策。我们推导出估计政策的福利遗憾界限，并描述了其收敛性。我们将我们的提议应用于匹配求职援助计划中的个案工作者和求职者的问题，并在模拟研究中使用法国行政数据进行校准，评估其福利表现。

### 稳定币：基础、新出现的问题和开放挑战
**[Stablecoins: Fundamentals, Emerging Issues, and Open Challenges](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.13883)**

**Authors**: Ahmed Mahrous, Maurantonio Caprolu, Roberto Di Pietro

**Abstract**: Stablecoins, with a capitalization exceeding 200 billion USD as of January 2025, have shown significant growth, with annual transaction volumes exceeding 10 trillion dollars in 2023 and nearly doubling that figure in 2024. This exceptional success has attracted the attention of traditional financial institutions, with an increasing number of governments exploring the potential of Central Bank Digital Currencies (CBDCs). Although academia has recognized the importance of stablecoins, research in this area remains fragmented, incomplete, and sometimes contradictory. In this paper, we aim to address the cited gap with a structured literature analysis, correlating recent contributions to present a picture of the complex economic, technical, and regulatory aspects of stablecoins. To achieve this, we formulate the main research questions and categorize scientific contributions accordingly, identifying main results, data sources, methodologies, and open research questions. The research questions we address in this survey paper cover several topics, such as the stability of various stablecoins, novel designs and implementations, and relevant regulatory challenges. The studies employ a wide range of methodologies and data sources, which we critically analyze and synthesize. Our analysis also reveals significant research gaps, including limited studies on security and privacy, underexplored stablecoins, unexamined failure cases, unstudied governance mechanisms, and the treatment of stablecoins under financial accounting standards, among other areas.

**摘要**: 截至2025年1月，稳定币的市值已超过2000亿美元，显示出显著增长，2023年的年交易量超过10万亿美元，在2024年几乎翻倍。这一非凡的成功引起了传统金融机构的关注，越来越多的政府正在探索中央银行数字货币（CBDC）的潜力。尽管学术界已经认识到稳定币的重要性，但这方面的研究仍然零散、不完整，有时甚至相互矛盾。在本文中，我们旨在通过结构化的文献分析来填补上述空白，将最近的贡献联系起来，以展现稳定币复杂的经济、技术和监管方面。为了实现这一点，我们制定了主要的研究问题，并相应地对科学贡献进行分类，识别主要结果、数据来源、方法论和开放性研究问题。我们在这篇综述论文中解决的研究问题涵盖了多个主题，如各种稳定币的稳定性、新的设计和实施，以及相关的监管挑战。这些研究采用了广泛的研究方法和数据来源，我们对其进行了批判性分析和综合。我们的分析还揭示了重要的研究空白，包括对安全和隐私的研究有限、未充分探索的稳定币、未经检验的失败案例、未研究的治理机制，以及在金融会计标准下对稳定币的处理等问题。

### 无观察异质性的去偏机器学习：高维面板和测量误差模型
**[Debiased Machine Learning for Unobserved Heterogeneity: High-Dimensional Panels and Measurement Error Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.13788)**

**Authors**: Facundo Argañaraz, Juan Carlos Escanciano

**Abstract**: Developing robust inference for models with nonparametric Unobserved Heterogeneity (UH) is both important and challenging. We propose novel Debiased Machine Learning (DML) procedures for valid inference on functionals of UH, allowing for partial identification of multivariate target and high-dimensional nuisance parameters. Our main contribution is a full characterization of all relevant Neyman-orthogonal moments in models with nonparametric UH, where relevance means informativeness about the parameter of interest. Under additional support conditions, orthogonal moments are globally robust to the distribution of the UH. They may still involve other high-dimensional nuisance parameters, but their local robustness reduces regularization bias and enables valid DML inference. We apply these results to: (i) common parameters, average marginal effects, and variances of UH in panel data models with high-dimensional controls; (ii) moments of the common factor in the Kotlarski model with a factor loading; and (iii) smooth functionals of teacher value-added. Monte Carlo simulations show substantial efficiency gains from using efficient orthogonal moments relative to ad-hoc choices. We illustrate the practical value of our approach by showing that existing estimates of the average and variance effects of maternal smoking on child birth weight are robust.

**摘要**: 为具有非参数未观测异质性（UH）的模型开发稳健的推断既重要又具有挑战性。我们提出了一种新的去偏机器学习（DML）程序，用于对UH函数的有效推断，允许对多变量目标和高维扰动参数进行部分识别。我们的主要贡献是对非参数UH模型中所有相关Neyman-正交矩的完整描述，其中相关性意味着对感兴趣参数的信息性。在额外的支持条件下，正交矩对UH的分布具有全球鲁棒性。它们可能仍然涉及其他高维扰动参数，但它们的局部鲁棒性减少了正则化偏差，并使得有效的DML推断成为可能。我们将这些结果应用于：（i）具有高维控制的面板数据模型中的共同参数、平均边际效应和UH的方差；（ii）具有因子载荷的Kotlarski模型中的共同因子矩；以及（iii）教师附加值的平滑函数。蒙特卡洛模拟显示，使用有效的正交矩相对于临时选择可以获得显著的效率提升。我们通过展示现有对母亲吸烟对孩子出生体重的平均和方差效应的估计值是稳健的，来说明我们方法的实际价值。

### 结合声明偏好和显示偏好
**[Combining stated and revealed preferences](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.13552)**

**Authors**: Romuald Meango, Marc Henry, Ismael Mourifie

**Abstract**: Can stated preferences inform counterfactual analyses of actual choice? This research proposes a novel approach to researchers who have access to both stated choices in hypothetical scenarios and actual choices, matched or unmatched. The key idea is to use stated choices to identify the distribution of individual unobserved heterogeneity. If this unobserved heterogeneity is the source of endogeneity, the researcher can correct for its influence in a demand function estimation using actual choices and recover causal effects. Bounds on causal effects are derived in the case, where stated choice and actual choices are observed in unmatched data sets. These data combination bounds are of independent interest. We derive a valid bootstrap inference for the bounds and show its good performance in a simulation experiment.

**摘要**: 陈述偏好能否为实际选择的反事实分析提供信息？这项研究为那些能够获得假设情景中的陈述选择和实际选择（匹配或不匹配）的研究人员提出了一种新方法。关键思想是利用陈述选择来识别个体未观察到的异质性的分布。如果这种未观察到的异质性是内生性的来源，研究人员可以在使用实际选择的需求函数估计中纠正其影响，并恢复因果效应。在陈述选择和实际选择在不匹配的数据集中被观察到的情况下，推导出了因果效应的界限。这些数据组合界限具有独立的兴趣。我们推导出了对界限的有效bootstrap推断，并在模拟实验中展示了其良好的性能。

### 二级价格歧视：理论分析、实验设计和实证估计
**[Second-degree Price Discrimination: Theoretical Analysis, Experiment Design, and Empirical Estimation](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.13426)**

**Authors**: Soheil Ghili, K. Sudhir, Nitish Jain, Ankur Garg

**Abstract**: We build on theoretical results from the mechanism design literature to analyze empirical models of second-degree price discrimination (2PD). We show that for a random-coefficients discrete choice ("BLP") model to be suitable for studying 2PD, it must capture the covariance between two key random effects: (i) the "baseline" willingness to pay (affecting all product versions), and (ii) the perceived differentiation between versions. We then develop an experimental design that, among other features, identifies this covariance under common data constraints in 2PD environments. We implement this experiment in the field in collaboration with an international airline. Estimating the theoretically motivated empirical model on the experimental data, we demonstrate its applicability to 2PD decisions. We also show that test statistics from our design can enable qualitative inference on optimal 2PD policy even before estimating a demand model. Our methodology applies broadly across second-degree price discrimination settings.

**摘要**: 我们基于机制设计文献中的理论成果来分析第二度价格歧视（2PD）的经验模型。我们展示了随机系数离散选择（“BLP”）模型要适合研究2PD，必须捕捉两个关键随机效应之间的协方差：（i）“基线”支付意愿（影响所有产品版本），以及（ii）版本之间的感知差异化。然后我们开发了一个实验设计，它在2PD环境中常见的数据约束下识别这种协方差。我们与一家国际航空公司合作，在实地实施了这个实验。在实验数据上估计理论上有动机的经验模型，我们证明了它适用于2PD决策。我们还展示了我们设计中的检验统计量甚至可以在估计需求模型之前，对最优2PD政策进行定性推断。我们的方法论广泛适用于第二度价格歧视的设置。
