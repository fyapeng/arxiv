### 人工智能博弈？以纳什均衡为特例的δ-理性博弈
**[AI Plays? δ-Rationality Games with Nash Equilibrium as Special Case](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.16467)**

**Authors**: Fang-Fang Tang, Yongsheng Xu

**Abstract**: A distortion function, which captures the payoff gap between a player's actual payoff and her true payoff, is introduced and used to analyze games. In our proposed framework, we argue that players' actual payoff functions should be used to explain and predict their behaviors, while their true payoff functions should be used to conduct welfare analysis of the outcomes.

**摘要**: 我们引入了一个扭曲函数，该函数捕捉了玩家实际收益与其真实收益之间的差距，并用它来分析游戏。在我们提出的框架中，我们认为应该使用玩家的实际收益函数来解释和预测他们的行为，而应该使用他们的真实收益函数来进行结果的福利分析。

### 金融网络中的最优监管与投资激励
**[Optimal Regulation and Investment Incentives in Financial Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.16648)**

**Authors**: Matthew O. Jackson, Agathe Pernoud

**Abstract**: We examine optimal regulation of financial networks with debt interdependencies between financial firms. We first characterize when it is firms have an incentive to choose excessively risky portfolios and overly correlate their portfolios with those of their counterparties. We then characterize how optimal regulation depends on a firm's financial centrality and its available investment opportunities. In standard core-periphery networks, optimal regulation depends non-monotonically on the correlation of banks' investments, with maximal restrictions for intermediate levels of correlation. Moreover, it can be uniquely optimal to treat banks asymmetrically: restricting the investments of one core bank while allowing an otherwise identical core bank (in all aspects, including network centrality) to invest freely.

**摘要**: 我们研究了在金融公司之间存在债务相互依赖的金融网络的最佳监管。我们首先描述了公司何时有动机选择过度风险的投资组合，以及过度将他们的投资组合与对手方的投资组合相关联。然后，我们描述了最佳监管如何取决于公司的财务中心性及其可用的投资机会。在标准的内核-边缘网络中，最佳监管非单调地依赖于银行投资的相关性，对于相关性的中间水平有最大的限制。此外，可以唯一地选择不对称地对待银行是最优的：限制一个核心银行的投资，同时允许另一个在所有方面（包括网络中心性）都相同的核心银行自由投资。

### 新的平衡：COVID-19封锁和持续的居家办公
**[A new equilibrium: COVID-19 lockdowns and WFH persistence](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.16671)**

**Authors**: Laura Ketter, Todd Morris, Lizi Yu

**Abstract**: This paper documents a robust link between COVID-19 lockdowns and the uptake and persistence of working from home (WFH) practices. Exploiting rich longitudinal data, we use a difference-in-differences strategy to compare office workers in three heavily locked-down Australian states to similar workers in less affected states. Locked-down workers sustain 43% higher WFH levels through 2023 - 0.5 days per week - with a monotonic dose-response relationship. Persistence is driven by adjustments on both sides of the labor market: employers downsize office space and open remote or hybrid positions, while employees relocate away from city centers and invest in home offices and technology.

**摘要**: 本文记录了COVID-19封锁措施与居家办公（WFH）实践的采纳和持续性之间的牢固联系。利用丰富的纵向数据，我们采用差异对比策略，比较了三个实施严格封锁的澳大利亚州的办公室工作人员与受影响较小的州的类似工作人员。封锁地区的工作者在2023年之前维持了43%更高的居家办公水平——每周0.5天——呈现出单调的剂量-反应关系。持续性是由劳动市场的双方调整驱动的：雇主缩减办公空间并开设远程或混合职位，而员工则从市中心搬迁，并投资于家庭办公室和技术。

### 捕捉不一致
**[Capturing Misalignment](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.17176)**

**Authors**: Pierfrancesco Guarino, Gabriel Ziegler

**Abstract**: We introduce and formalize misalignment, a phenomenon of interactive environments perceived from an analyst's perspective where an agent holds beliefs about another agent's beliefs that do not correspond to the actual beliefs of the latter. We demonstrate that standard frameworks, such as type structures, fail to capture misalignment, necessitating new tools to analyze this phenomenon. To this end, we characterize misalignment through non-belief-closed state spaces and introduce agent-dependent type structures, which provide a flexible tool to understand the varying degrees of misalignment. Furthermore, we establish that appropriately adapted modal operators on agent-dependent type structures behave consistently with standard properties, enabling us to explore the implications of misalignment for interactive reasoning. Finally, we show how speculative trade can arise under misalignment, even when imposing the corresponding assumptions that rule out such trades in standard environments.

**摘要**: 我们引入并形式化了错位这一现象，这是从分析师的角度感知的交互环境中的一种现象，其中一个代理对另一个代理的信念持有的信念与后者的实际信念不符。我们证明，标准框架，如类型结构，未能捕捉到错位，需要新工具来分析这一现象。为此，我们通过非信念封闭状态空间来表征错位，并引入了代理依赖的类型结构，这提供了一个灵活的工具来理解不同程度的错位。此外，我们确立了适当适应的代理依赖类型结构上的模态运算符与标准属性的行为一致，使我们能够探索错位对交互推理的影响。最后，我们展示了在错位下，即使在施加排除标准环境中此类交易的相应假设的情况下，投机性交易如何产生。

### 你知道我的意思吗？一种用于差异性有界意识的句法表示
**[Do You Know What I Mean? A Syntactic Representation for Differential Bounded Awareness](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.16901)**

**Authors**: Ani Guerdjikova, Evan Piermont, John Quiggin

**Abstract**: Without the assumption of complete, shared awareness, it is necessary to consider communication between agents who may entertain different representations of the world. A syntactic (language-based) approach provides powerful tools to address this problem. In this paper, we define translation operators between two languages which provide a ``best approximation'' for the meaning of propositions in the target language subject to its expressive power. We show that, in general, the translation operators preserve some, but not all, logical operations. We derive necessary and sufficient conditions for the existence of a joint state space and a joint language, in which the subjective state spaces of each agent, and their individual languages, may be embedded. This approach allows us to compare languages with respect to their expressiveness and thus, with respect to the properties of the associated state space.

**摘要**: 在没有完整共享意识的假设下，有必要考虑可能对世界持有不同表征的代理之间的沟通问题。一种基于语言的句法方法为解决这一问题提供了强大的工具。在本文中，我们定义了两种语言之间的翻译算子，这些算子在目标语言的表达能力范围内，为命题的含义提供了“最佳近似”。我们展示了，一般来说，翻译算子保留了一些但并非全部的逻辑运算。我们推导出了存在一个联合状态空间和一个联合语言的必要和充分条件，在这些空间和语言中，每个代理的主观状态空间以及他们各自的语言可以被嵌入。这种方法使我们能够比较语言的表达能力，因此，比较与之相关的联合状态空间的属性。

### 自由主义的可实施性
**[The Implementability of Liberalism](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.16059)**

**Authors**: Héctor Hermida-Rivera

**Abstract**: This note shows that under the unrestricted domain, there exists a choice liberal and Nash implementable social choice rule if and only if there are at least three players and the outcome set is at least twice as large as the player set. A social choice rule is choice liberal if and only if for every player, there exists at least one pair of outcomes such that if this player strictly prefers one over the other, the one he prefers is socially desirable and the other one is not. A social choice rule is Nash implementable if and only if there exists a mechanism such that at every preference profile, the set of Nash equilibrium outcomes coincides with the set of socially desirable ones. The proof constructs an intuitive Nash implementing mechanism.

**摘要**: 这篇文章表明，在无限制域下，存在一个选择自由且纳什可实施的社会选择规则，当且仅当至少有三个参与者，并且结果集至少是参与者集的两倍大。如果对于每个参与者，都至少存在一对结果，使得如果这个参与者严格偏好其中一个而不是另一个，他所偏好的那个结果是社会可取的，而另一个则不是，那么一个社会选择规则就是选择自由的。如果存在一个机制，使得在每个偏好配置下，纳什均衡结果集与社会可取结果集相一致，那么一个社会选择规则就是纳什可实施的。证明构建了一个直观的纳什实施机制。

### 不让任何人受到削弱：避免遗憾的政策目标
**[Leave No One Undermined: Policy Targeting with Regret Aversion](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.16430)**

**Authors**: Toru Kitagawa, Sokbae Lee, Chen Qiu

**Abstract**: While the importance of personalized policymaking is widely recognized, fully personalized implementation remains rare in practice. We study the problem of policy targeting for a regret-averse planner when training data gives a rich set of observable characteristics while the assignment rules can only depend on its subset. Grounded in decision theory, our regret-averse criterion reflects a planner's concern about regret inequality across the population, which generally leads to a fractional optimal rule due to treatment effect heterogeneity beyond the average treatment effects conditional on the subset characteristics. We propose a debiased empirical risk minimization approach to learn the optimal rule from data. Viewing our debiased criterion as a weighted least squares problem, we establish new upper and lower bounds for the excess risk, indicating a convergence rate of 1/n and asymptotic efficiency in certain cases. We apply our approach to the National JTPA Study and the International Stroke Trial.

**摘要**: 尽管个性化政策制定的重要性得到了广泛认可，但在实践中完全个性化的实施仍然很少见。我们研究了一个对遗憾敏感的规划者在训练数据提供了丰富的可观察特征集，而分配规则只能依赖于其子集时的政策定位问题。基于决策理论，我们提出的对遗憾敏感的标准反映了规划者对人口中遗憾不平等的担忧，这通常会导致由于治疗效应的异质性超出了基于子集特征的平均治疗效果，而产生一个分数最优规则。我们提出了一种去偏的实证风险最小化方法，从数据中学习最优规则。将我们的去偏标准视为加权最小二乘问题，我们为超额风险建立了新的上界和下界，表明在某些情况下，收敛速度为1/n，并且渐近有效。我们将我们的方法应用于国家JTPA研究和国际中风试验。

### 社交媒体在公众审查度高时可以减少错误信息
**[Social Media Can Reduce Misinformation When Public Scrutiny is High](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.16355)**

**Authors**: Gavin Wang, Haofei Qin, Xiao Tang, Lynn Wu

**Abstract**: Misinformation poses a growing global threat to institutional trust, democratic stability, and public decision-making. While prior research has often portrayed social media as a channel for spreading falsehoods, less is known about the conditions under which it may instead constrain misinformation by enhancing transparency and accountability. Here we show this dual potential in the context of local governments' GDP reporting in China, where data falsifications are widespread. Analyzing official reports from 2011 to 2019, we find that local governments have overstated GDP on average. However, after adopting social media for public communications, the extent of misreporting declines significantly but only in regions where the public scrutiny over political matters is high. In such regions, social media increases the cost of misinformation by facilitating greater information disclosure and bottom-up monitoring. In contrast, in regions with low public scrutiny, adopting social media can exacerbate data manipulation. These findings challenge the prevailing view that social media primarily amplifies misinformation and instead highlight the importance of civic engagement as a moderating force. Our findings show a boundary condition for the spread of misinformation and offer insights for platform design and public policy aimed at promoting accuracy and institutional accountability.

**摘要**: 错误信息对全球机构信任、民主稳定和公共决策构成了日益增长的威胁。尽管先前的研究常常将社交媒体描绘成传播虚假信息的渠道，但对于它在何种条件下可能通过增强透明度和问责制来限制错误信息的情况知之甚少。在这里，我们在中国地方政府GDP报告的背景下展示了这种双重潜力，那里的数据造假现象普遍存在。分析2011年至2019年的官方报告，我们发现地方政府平均高估了GDP。然而，在采用社交媒体进行公共沟通后，报告不实的程度显著下降，但仅限于公众对政治事务审查度高的地区。在这些地区，社交媒体通过促进更多的信息披露和自下而上的监督，增加了错误信息的成本。相比之下，在公众审查度低的地区，采用社交媒体可能会加剧数据操纵。这些发现挑战了社交媒体主要放大错误信息的普遍观点，反而强调了公民参与作为调节力量的重要性。我们的发现显示了错误信息传播的边界条件，并为旨在促进准确性和机构问责的平台设计和公共政策提供了洞见。

### 长期联盟会导致缩减吗？气候俱乐部中触发和共享技术的作用
**[Long Coalition Leads to Shrink? The Roles of Tipping and Technology-Sharing in Climate Clubs](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.16162)**

**Authors**: Lei Zhu, Zhihao Yan, Hongbo Duan, Yongyang Cai, Xiaobing Zhang

**Abstract**: Global cooperation is posited as a pivotal solution to address climate change, yet significant barriers, like free-riding, hinder its realization. This paper develops a dynamic game-theoretic model to analyze the stability of coalitions under multiple stochastic climate tippings, and a technology-sharing mechanism is designed in the model to combat free-ridings. Our results reveal that coalitions tend to shrink over time as temperatures rise, owing to potential free-ridings, despite a large size of initial coalition. The threat of climate tipping reduces the size of stable coalitions compared to the case where tipping is ignored. However, at post-tipping period, coalitions temporarily expand as regions respond to the shock, though this cooperation is short-lived and followed by further shrink. Notably, technology-sharing generates greater collective benefits than sanctions, suggesting that the proposed dynamic technology-sharing pathway bolsters coalition resilience against free-riding while limiting the global warming. This framework highlights the critical role of technology-sharing in fostering long-term climate cooperation under climate tipping uncertainties.

**摘要**: 全球合作被认为是解决气候变化的关键解决方案，然而像搭便车这样的重大障碍阻碍了其实现。本文构建了一个动态博弈论模型，分析了在多重随机气候临界点下联盟的稳定性，并在模型中设计了一个技术共享机制来对抗搭便车行为。我们的结果显示，随着温度的上升，联盟倾向于随着时间的推移而缩小，这是由于潜在的搭便车行为，尽管初始联盟规模很大。气候临界点的威胁减少了与忽视临界点的情况相比的稳定联盟规模。然而，在临界点之后，联盟暂时扩大，因为地区对冲击做出反应，尽管这种合作是短暂的，随后是进一步的缩小。值得注意的是，技术共享产生的集体利益大于制裁，这表明提出的动态技术共享路径增强了联盟对搭便车的韧性，同时限制了全球变暖。这一框架强调了技术共享在气候临界点不确定性下促进长期气候合作的关键作用。

### 人工智能、精益创业方法和产品创新
**[Artificial Intelligence, Lean Startup Method, and Product Innovations](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.16334)**

**Authors**: Gavin Wang, Lynn Wu

**Abstract**: Although AI has the potential to drive significant business innovation, many firms struggle to realize its benefits. We examine how the Lean Startup Method (LSM) influences the impact of AI on product innovation in startups. Analyzing data from 1,800 Chinese startups between 2011 and 2020, alongside policy shifts by the Chinese government in encouraging AI adoption, we find that companies with strong AI capabilities produce more innovative products. Moreover, our study reveals that AI investments complement LSM in innovation, with effectiveness varying by the type of innovation and AI capability. We differentiate between discovery-oriented AI, which reduces uncertainty in novel areas of innovation, and optimization-oriented AI, which refines and optimizes existing processes. Within the framework of LSM, we further distinguish between prototyping focused on developing minimum viable products, and controlled experimentation, focused on rigorous testing such as AB testing. We find that LSM complements discovery oriented AI by utilizing AI to expand the search for market opportunities and employing prototyping to validate these opportunities, thereby reducing uncertainties and facilitating the development of the first release of products. Conversely, LSM complements optimization-oriented AI by using AB testing to experiment with the universe of input features and using AI to streamline iterative refinement processes, thereby accelerating the improvement of iterative releases of products. As a result, when firms use AI and LSM for product development, they are able to generate more high quality product in less time. These findings, applicable to both software and hardware development, underscore the importance of treating AI as a heterogeneous construct, as different AI capabilities require distinct organizational processes to achieve optimal outcomes.

**摘要**: 尽管人工智能（AI）有潜力推动重大的商业创新，但许多公司难以实现其效益。我们研究了精益创业方法（LSM）如何影响AI对初创企业产品创新的影响。通过分析2011年至2020年间1800家中国初创企业的数据，以及中国政府在鼓励采用AI方面的政策转变，我们发现拥有强大AI能力的公司能够生产出更具创新性的产品。此外，我们的研究揭示了AI投资与LSM在创新中的互补性，其有效性因创新类型和AI能力的不同而异。我们区分了以发现为导向的AI，它减少了创新新领域的不确定性，以及以优化为导向的AI，它完善和优化现有流程。在LSM框架内，我们进一步区分了专注于开发最小可行产品的原型设计，以及专注于严格测试（如A/B测试）的控制实验。我们发现LSM通过利用AI扩大寻找市场机会的搜索，并使用原型设计来验证这些机会，从而减少不确定性并促进产品的首次发布。相反，LSM通过使用A/B测试来实验输入特征的宇宙，并使用AI来简化迭代细化流程，从而加速产品的迭代改进。因此，当公司使用AI和LSM进行产品开发时，他们能够在更短的时间内产生更多的高质量产品。这些发现适用于软件开发和硬件开发，强调了将AI视为异质性构建的重要性，因为不同的AI能力需要不同的组织流程来实现最佳结果。

### 通过在线决策问题学习随机效用模型
**[Learning in Random Utility Models Via Online Decision Problems](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.16030)**

**Authors**: Emerson Melo

**Abstract**: This paper examines the Random Utility Model (RUM) in repeated stochastic choice settings where decision-makers lack full information about payoffs. We propose a gradient-based learning algorithm that embeds RUM into an online decision-making framework. Our analysis establishes Hannan consistency for a broad class of RUMs, meaning the average regret relative to the best fixed action in hindsight vanishes over time. We also show that our algorithm is equivalent to the Follow-The-Regularized-Leader (FTRL) method, offering an economically grounded approach to online optimization. Applications include modeling recency bias and characterizing coarse correlated equilibria in normal-form games

### 使用机器学习构建有效的可解释房地产市场模型的现代方法
**[Modern approaches to building effective interpretable models of the property market using machine learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.15723)**

**Authors**: Irina G. Tanashkina, Alexey S. Tanashkin, Alexander S. Maksimchuik, Anna Yu. Poshivailo

**Abstract**: In this article, we review modern approaches to building interpretable models of property markets using machine learning on the base of mass valuation of property in the Primorye region, Russia. The researcher, lacking expertise in this topic, encounters numerous difficulties in the effort to build a good model. The main source of this is the huge difference between noisy real market data and ideal data which is very common in all types of tutorials on machine learning. This paper covers all stages of modeling: the collection of initial data, identification of outliers, the search and analysis of patterns in data, the formation and final choice of price factors, the building of the model, and the evaluation of its efficiency. For each stage, we highlight potential issues and describe sound methods for overcoming emerging difficulties on actual examples. We show that the combination of classical linear regression with interpolation methods of geostatistics allows to build an effective model for land parcels. For flats, when many objects are attributed to one spatial point the application of geostatistical methods is difficult. Therefore we suggest linear regression with automatic generation and selection of additional rules on the base of decision trees, so called the RuleFit method. Thus we show, that despite the strong restriction as the requirement of interpretability which is important in practical aspects, for example, legal matters, it is still possible to build effective models of real property markets.
