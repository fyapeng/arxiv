### 早期生活经济条件对长期健康和人力资本的影响
**[Long-term Health and Human Capital Effects of Early-Life Economic Conditions](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.08159)**

**Authors**: Ruijun Hou, Samuel Baker, Stephanie von Hinke, Hans H. Sievertsen, Emil Sørensen, Nicolai Vitt

**Abstract**: We study the long-term health and human capital impacts of local economic conditions experienced during the first 1,000 days of life. We combine historical data on monthly unemployment rates in urban England and Wales 1952-1967 with data from the UK Biobank on later-life outcomes. Leveraging variation in unemployment driven by national industry-specific shocks weighted by industry's importance in each area, we find no evidence that small, common fluctuations in local economic conditions during the early life period affect health or human capital in older age.

**摘要**: 我们研究了在生命最初的1000天内所经历的地方经济状况对长期健康和人力资本的影响。我们将1952-1967年英格兰和威尔士城市每月失业率的历史数据与英国生物银行关于晚年结果的数据结合起来。利用由国家行业特定冲击引起的失业率变化，这些变化由各地区行业的重要性加权，我们发现没有证据表明早期生活中的小的、常见的地方经济状况波动会影响老年时的健康或人力资本。

### 临时工是否面临更高的工资折扣？来自印度汽车行业的证据
**[Do Temporary Workers Face Higher Wage Markdowns? Evidence from India's Automotive Sector](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.08222)**

**Authors**: Davide Luparello

**Abstract**: Are temporary workers subject to different wage markdowns than permanent workers? This paper examines productivity, output markups, and wage markdowns in India's automotive sector during 2000--2020. I develop a model integrating CES production, nested logit labor supply, and differentiated labor market conduct: Nash-Bertrand wage setting for temporary workers versus Nash bargaining for unionized permanent workers. Results reveal declining output markups as marginal costs outpace prices through productivity deceleration. Rising labor-augmenting productivity cannot offset declining Hicks-neutral productivity, reducing overall TFP. Labor market power substantially compresses worker compensation: wage markdowns persist at 40% for temporary workers and 10% for permanent workers.

**摘要**: 临时工是否比长期工受到不同的工资折扣？本文研究了2000-2020年间印度汽车行业的生产率、产出加成和工资折扣。我开发了一个模型，整合了CES生产、嵌套Logit劳动供给和差异化的劳动市场行为：对于临时工是Nash-Bertrand工资设定，而对于工会化的长期工则是Nash谈判。结果揭示了随着边际成本超过价格，产出加成逐渐下降，这是由于生产率的减速。提高劳动增强型生产率无法抵消Hicks中性生产率的下降，降低了总体TFP。劳动市场的力量显著压缩了工人的薪酬：临时工的工资折扣持续在40%，而长期工的工资折扣为10%。

### 从革命到毁灭：对也门国家崩溃的实证分析
**[From Revolution to Ruin: An Empirical Analysis Yemen's State Collapse](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.08512)**

**Authors**: Riste Ichev, Rok Spruk

**Abstract**: We assess the broad repercussions of Yemen's 2011 revolution and subsequent civil war on its macroeconomic trajectories, human development, and quality of governance by constructing counterfactual benchmarks using a balanced panel of 37 developing countries over 1990-2022. Drawing on matrix-completion estimators with alternative shrinkage regimes and a LASSO-augmented synthetic-control method, we generate Yemen's hypothetical no-conflict paths for key macroeconomic aggregates, demographic and health indicators, and governance metrics. Across the full spectrum of methods, the conflict's outbreak corresponds with a dramatic reversal of economic and institutional development. We find that output and income experience an unprecedented contraction, investment and trade openness deteriorate sharply, and gains in life expectancy and human development are broadly reversed. Simultaneously, measures of political accountability, administrative capacity, rule of law, and corruption control collapse, reflecting systemic institutional breakdown. The concordance of results across a variety of empirical strategies attests to the robustness of our estimates.

**摘要**: 我们通过构建反事实基准，使用1990-2022年间37个发展中国家的平衡面板数据，评估了也门2011年革命及其后续内战对其宏观经济轨迹、人类发展和治理质量的广泛影响。我们利用具有替代收缩机制的矩阵补全估计器和LASSO增强的合成控制方法，生成了也门关键宏观经济总量、人口和健康指标以及治理指标的假设无冲突路径。在各种方法的全谱系中，冲突的爆发与经济和制度发展的戏剧性逆转相对应。我们发现产出和收入经历了前所未有的收缩，投资和贸易开放度急剧恶化，预期寿命和人类发展的增长被广泛逆转。与此同时，政治问责、行政能力、法治和腐败控制的衡量标准崩溃，反映了系统性的制度崩溃。一系列实证策略的结果一致性证明了我们估计的稳健性。

### 倾向得分与因子载荷：巴黎协定的影响
**[Propensity score with factor loadings: the effect of the Paris Agreement](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.08764)**

**Authors**: Angelo Forino, Andrea Mercatanti, Giacomo Morelli

**Abstract**: Factor models for longitudinal data, where policy adoption is unconfounded with respect to a low-dimensional set of latent factor loadings, have become increasingly popular for causal inference. Most existing approaches, however, rely on a causal finite-sample approach or computationally intensive methods, limiting their applicability and external validity. In this paper, we propose a novel causal inference method for panel data based on inverse propensity score weighting where the propensity score is a function of latent factor loadings within a framework of causal inference from super-population. The approach relaxes the traditional restrictive assumptions of causal panel methods, while offering advantages in terms of causal interpretability, policy relevance, and computational efficiency. Under standard assumptions, we outline a three-step estimation procedure for the ATT and derive its large-sample properties using Mestimation theory. We apply the method to assess the causal effect of the Paris Agreement, a policy aimed at fostering the transition to a low-carbon economy, on European stock returns. Our empirical results suggest a statistically significant and negative short-run effect on the stock returns of firms that issued green bonds.

**摘要**: 用于纵向数据的因子模型，其中政策采纳与低维潜在因子载荷集无偏倚，已经成为因果推断中越来越受欢迎的方法。然而，大多数现有方法依赖于因果有限样本方法或计算密集型方法，限制了它们的适用性和外部有效性。在本文中，我们提出了一种基于逆倾向得分加权的面板数据因果推断新方法，其中倾向得分是超总体因果推断框架内潜在因子载荷的函数。这种方法放宽了传统因果面板方法的限制性假设，同时在因果可解释性、政策相关性和计算效率方面提供了优势。在标准假设下，我们概述了ATT的三步估计程序，并使用Mestimation理论推导了其大样本性质。我们将该方法应用于评估《巴黎协定》的因果效应，该政策旨在促进向低碳经济的转型，对欧洲股票回报的影响。我们的实证结果表明，对发行绿色债券的公司股票回报产生了统计上显著的负面影响。

### 提升人工智能能力与不断演变的劳动结果
**[Advancing AI Capabilities and Evolving Labor Outcomes](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.08244)**

**Authors**: Jacob Dominski, Yong Suk Lee

**Abstract**: This study investigates the labor market consequences of AI by analyzing near real-time changes in employment status and work hours across occupations in relation to advances in AI capabilities. We construct a dynamic Occupational AI Exposure Score based on a task-level assessment using state-of-the-art AI models, including ChatGPT 4o and Anthropic Claude 3.5 Sonnet. We introduce a five-stage framework that evaluates how AI's capability to perform tasks in occupations changes as technology advances from traditional machine learning to agentic AI. The Occupational AI Exposure Scores are then linked to the US Current Population Survey, allowing for near real-time analysis of employment, unemployment, work hours, and full-time status. We conduct a first-differenced analysis comparing the period from October 2022 to March 2023 with the period from October 2024 to March 2025. Higher exposure to AI is associated with reduced employment, higher unemployment rates, and shorter work hours. We also observe some evidence of increased secondary job holding and a decrease in full-time employment among certain demographics. These associations are more pronounced among older and younger workers, men, and college-educated individuals. College-educated workers tend to experience smaller declines in employment but are more likely to see changes in work intensity and job structure. In addition, occupations that rely heavily on complex reasoning and problem-solving tend to experience larger declines in full-time work and overall employment in association with rising AI exposure. In contrast, those involving manual physical tasks appear less affected. Overall, the results suggest that AI-driven shifts in labor are occurring along both the extensive margin (unemployment) and the intensive margin (work hours), with varying effects across occupational task content and demographics.

**摘要**: 本研究通过分析与人工智能能力进步相关的职业就业状况和工作时间的实时变化，来调查人工智能对劳动市场的冲击。我们构建了一个基于任务层面评估的动态职业人工智能暴露分数，使用了包括ChatGPT 4o和Anthropic Claude 3.5 Sonnet在内的最先进人工智能模型。我们引入了一个五阶段框架，评估随着技术从传统机器学习发展到代理人工智能，人工智能执行职业任务的能力如何变化。然后将职业人工智能暴露分数与美国当前人口调查联系起来，允许对就业、失业、工作时间和全职状态进行实时分析。我们进行了一个首次差异分析，比较了2022年10月至2023年3月的时期与2024年10月至2025年3月的时期。较高的人工智能暴露与就业减少、失业率提高和工作时间缩短相关。我们还观察到一些证据表明，在某些人群中，次要工作持有增加和全职就业减少。这些关联在年长和年轻工人、男性和受过大学教育的个体中更为明显。受过大学教育的工人往往经历较小的就业下降，但更有可能看到工作强度和工作结构的变化。此外，那些严重依赖复杂推理和问题解决的职业，随着人工智能暴露的增加，全职工作和总体就业的下降幅度往往更大。相比之下，涉及手工体力任务的职业似乎受影响较小。总体而言，结果表明，由人工智能驱动的劳动力转移正在沿着广泛的边际（失业）和密集的边际（工作时间）发生，对职业任务内容和人口统计学的影响各不相同。

### 信号还是噪声？在不同情境变化和人类专家基准下评估大型语言模型在简历筛选中的应用
**[Signal or Noise? Evaluating Large Language Models in Resume Screening Across Contextual Variations and Human Expert Benchmarks](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.08019)**

**Authors**: Aryan Varshney, Venkat Ram Reddy Ganuthula

**Abstract**: This study investigates whether large language models (LLMs) exhibit consistent behavior (signal) or random variation (noise) when screening resumes against job descriptions, and how their performance compares to human experts. Using controlled datasets, we tested three LLMs (Claude, GPT, and Gemini) across contexts (No Company, Firm1 [MNC], Firm2 [Startup], Reduced Context) with identical and randomized resumes, benchmarked against three human recruitment experts. Analysis of variance revealed significant mean differences in four of eight LLM-only conditions and consistently significant differences between LLM and human evaluations (p < 0.01). Paired t-tests showed GPT adapts strongly to company context (p < 0.001), Gemini partially (p = 0.038 for Firm1), and Claude minimally (p > 0.1), while all LLMs differed significantly from human experts across contexts. Meta-cognition analysis highlighted adaptive weighting patterns that differ markedly from human evaluation approaches. Findings suggest LLMs offer interpretable patterns with detailed prompts but diverge substantially from human judgment, informing their deployment in automated hiring systems.

**摘要**: 这项研究调查了大型语言模型（LLMs）在筛选简历与工作描述时是否表现出一致的行为（信号）还是随机变化（噪声），以及它们的表现与人类专家相比如何。我们使用受控数据集，测试了三个LLM（Claude、GPT和Gemini）在不同情境（无公司、Firm1 [跨国公司]、Firm2 [初创公司]、减少上下文）下的表现，使用相同和随机化的简历，并与三名人类招聘专家进行基准比较。方差分析显示，在八个LLM-only条件中有四个显示出显著的平均差异，并且在LLM和人类评估之间始终存在显著差异（p < 0.01）。配对t检验显示GPT强烈适应公司环境（p < 0.001），Gemini部分适应（Firm1的p值为0.038），而Claude适应性最小（p > 0.1），而所有LLM在不同情境下与人类专家相比都存在显著差异。元认知分析突出了与人类评估方法截然不同的适应性加权模式。研究结果表明，LLMs在详细提示下提供了可解释的模式，但在很大程度上偏离了人类判断，这为它们在自动化招聘系统中的部署提供了信息。
