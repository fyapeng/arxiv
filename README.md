### 一个通用的无模型密集精度矩阵估计器类别
**[A General Class of Model-Free Dense Precision Matrix Estimators](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.04663)**

**Authors**: Mehmet Caner Agostino Capponi Mihailo Stojnic

**Abstract**: We introduce prototype consistent model-free, dense precision matrix estimators that have broad application in economics. Using quadratic form concentration inequalities and novel algebraic characterizations of confounding dimension reductions, we are able to: (i) obtain non-asymptotic bounds for precision matrix estimation errors and also (ii) consistency in high dimensions; (iii) uncover the existence of an intrinsic signal-to-noise -- underlying dimensions tradeoff; and (iv) avoid exact population sparsity assumptions. In addition to its desirable theoretical properties, a thorough empirical study of the S&P 500 index shows that a tuning parameter-free special case of our general estimator exhibits a doubly ascending Sharpe Ratio pattern, thereby establishing a link with the famous double descent phenomenon dominantly present in recent statistical and machine learning literature.

**摘要**: 我们介绍了原型一致的无模型密集精度矩阵估计器，这些估计器在经济学中有广泛的应用。利用二次型集中不等式和混淆维度降低的新代数特征，我们能够：（i）获得非渐近精度矩阵估计误差的界限，并且（ii）在高维中保持一致性；（iii）揭示了固有的信噪比——底层维度权衡的存在；以及（iv）避免精确的总体稀疏性假设。除了其理想的理论属性外，对标准普尔500指数的彻底实证研究表明，我们一般估计器的一个无需调整参数的特殊情况表现出双上升的夏普比率模式，从而与最近统计和机器学习文献中占主导地位的著名双重下降现象建立了联系。

### 行为概率加权与半重尾下的组合优化
**[Behavioral Probability Weighting and Portfolio Optimization under Semi-Heavy Tails](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.04208)**

**Authors**: Ayush Jha, Abootaleb Shirvani, Ali M. Jaffri, Svetlozar T. Rachev, Frank J. Fabozzi

**Abstract**: This paper develops a unified framework that integrates behavioral distortions into rational portfolio optimization by extracting implied probability weighting functions (PWFs) from optimal portfolios modeled under Gaussian and Normal-Inverse-Gaussian (NIG) return distributions. Using DJIA constituents, we construct mean-CVaR99 frontiers, alongwith Sharpe- and CVaR-maximizing portfolios, and estimate PWFs that capture nonlinear beliefs consistent with fear and greed. We show that increasing tail fatness amplifies these distortions and that shifts in the term structure of risk-free rates alter their curvature. The results highlight the importance of jointly modeling return asymmetry and belief distortions in portfolio risk management and capital allocation under extreme-risk environments.

**摘要**: 本文建立了一个统一的框架，将行为偏差整合到理性投资组合优化中，通过从高斯和正态-逆高斯（NIG）回报分布下建模的最优投资组合中提取隐含的概率权重函数（PWFs）。使用道琼斯工业平均指数成分股，我们构建了均值-CVaR99前沿，以及夏普比率和CVaR最大化投资组合，并估计了捕捉与恐惧和贪婪一致的非线性信念的PWFs。我们展示了尾部脂肪度的增加放大了这些偏差，并且无风险利率的期限结构变化改变了它们的曲率。结果强调了在极端风险环境下，联合建模回报不对称性和信念偏差在投资组合风险管理和资本配置中的重要性。

### 使用堆积设计识别因果效应
**[Identification of Causal Effects with a Bunching Design](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.05210)**

**Authors**: Carolina Caetano, Gregorio Caetano, Leonard Goff, Eric Nielsen

**Abstract**: We show that causal effects can be identified when there is bunching in the distribution of a continuous treatment variable, without imposing any parametric assumptions. This yields a new nonparametric method for overcoming selection bias in the absence of instrumental variables, panel data, or other popular research designs for causal inference. The method leverages the change of variables theorem from integration theory, relating the selection bias to the ratio of the density of the treatment and the density of the part of the outcome that varies with confounders. At the bunching point, the treatment level is constant, so the variation in the outcomes is due entirely to unobservables, allowing us to identify the denominator. Our main result identifies the average causal response to the treatment among individuals who marginally select into the bunching point. We further show that under additional smoothness assumptions on the selection bias, treatment effects away from the bunching point may also be identified. We propose estimators based on standard software packages and apply the method to estimate the effect of maternal smoking during pregnancy on birth weight.

**摘要**: 我们展示了在连续处理变量分布中出现聚集时，可以识别因果效应，而无需施加任何参数假设。这产生了一种新的非参数方法，用于在没有工具变量、面板数据或其他流行的因果推断研究设计的情况下克服选择偏差。该方法利用了积分理论中的变量变换定理，将选择偏差与处理密度和随混杂因素变化的结果部分的密度比率联系起来。在聚集点，处理水平是恒定的，因此结果的变化完全是由不可观测因素引起的，这使我们能够识别分母。我们的主要结果识别了在边际选择进入聚集点的个体中对处理的平均因果反应。我们进一步展示了在对选择偏差的额外平滑性假设下，远离聚集点的处理效应也可能被识别。我们提出了基于标准软件包的估计器，并将该方法应用于估计孕期母亲吸烟对出生体重的影响。

### 1960-2019年经济增长的地缘政治决定因素
**[The Geopolitical Determinants of Economic Growth, 1960-2019](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.04833)**

**Authors**: Tianyu Fan

**Abstract**: This paper establishes geopolitical relations as a first-order determinant of economic growth. We construct a novel event-based measure of bilateral geopolitical alignment by employing large language models with web search capabilities to analyze over 440,000 political events across 196 countries from 1960--2019. This comprehensive measure enables us to identify the precise timing and magnitude of geopolitical shifts within countries over time. Using local projections with country fixed effects, we find that a one-standard-deviation improvement in geopolitical relations increases GDP per capita by 9.6 log points over 25 years. These persistent effects operate through multiple reinforcing channels -- enhanced political stability, increased investment, expanded trade, and productivity gains. Across our sample, geopolitical factors generate GDP variations ranging from -35% to +30%, with developing nations facing particularly severe penalties from international isolation. Our findings reveal how geopolitical alignment shapes economic prosperity in an increasingly fragmented global economy.

**摘要**: 本文将地缘政治关系确立为经济增长的首要决定因素。我们通过使用具有网络搜索能力的大型语言模型，构建了一个新颖的基于事件的双边地缘政治对齐度量方法，分析了1960年至2019年间196个国家的超过44万个政治事件。这一全面的度量方法使我们能够准确识别各国随时间变化的地缘政治转变的时机和幅度。利用具有国家固定效应的地方投影，我们发现地缘政治关系的一标准差改善在25年内将人均GDP提高了9.6个对数点。这些持久效应通过多个加强渠道发挥作用——增强政治稳定性、增加投资、扩大贸易和提高生产率。在我们的样本中，地缘政治因素产生的GDP变化范围从-35%到+30%，发展中国家尤其面临国际孤立的严重惩罚。我们的发现揭示了在日益分裂的全球经济中，地缘政治对齐如何塑造经济繁荣。

### 测量社交媒体网络效应
**[Measuring Social Media Network Effects](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.04545)**

**Authors**: Sinan Aral, Seth G Benzell, Avinash Collis, Christos Nicolaides

**Abstract**: We use representative, incentive-compatible online choice experiments involving 19,923 Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and X users in the US to provide the first large-scale, empirical measurement of local network effects in the digital economy. Our analysis reveals social media platform value ranges from $78 to $101 per consumer, per month, on average, and that 20-34% of that value is explained by local network effects. We also find 1) stronger ties are more valuable on Facebook and Instagram, while weaker ties are more valuable on LinkedIn and X; 2) connections known through work are most valuable on LinkedIn and least valuable on Facebook, and people looking for work value LinkedIn significantly more and Facebook significantly less than people not looking for work; 3) men value connections to women on social media significantly more than they value connections to other men, particularly on Instagram, Facebook and X, while women value connections to men and women equally; 4) white consumers value relationships with other white consumers significantly more than they value relationships with non-white consumers on Facebook while, on Instagram, connections to alters eighteen years old or younger are valued significantly more than any other age group-two patterns not seen on any other platforms. Social media platforms individually generate between $53B and $215B in consumer surplus per year in the US alone. These results suggest social media generates significant value, local network effects drive a substantial fraction of that value and that these effects vary across platforms, consumers, and connections.

**摘要**: 我们利用代表性的、激励相容的在线选择实验，涉及19,923名美国Facebook、Instagram、LinkedIn和X用户，首次大规模、实证性地测量了数字经济中的本地网络效应。我们的分析显示，社交媒体平台对每个消费者的平均价值每月在78美元到101美元之间，其中20-34%的价值是由本地网络效应解释的。我们还发现：1）在Facebook和Instagram上，更强的联系更有价值，而在LinkedIn和X上，更弱的联系更有价值；2）通过工作认识的联系在LinkedIn上最有价值，在Facebook上最没价值，寻找工作的人对LinkedIn的评价显著高于不找工作的人，而对Facebook的评价则显著低于不找工作的人；3）男性在社交媒体上对女性的联系的价值显著高于对其他男性的价值，特别是在Instagram、Facebook和X上，而女性对男性和女性的联系的价值评价相等；4）白人消费者在Facebook上对与其他白人消费者的关系的价值显著高于他们对非白人消费者的关系的价值，而在Instagram上，与18岁或更年轻的人的关系的价值显著高于任何其他年龄组——这两种模式在其他平台上都看不到。社交媒体平台在美国单独每年为消费者创造的价值在530亿到2150亿美元之间。这些结果表明，社交媒体创造了巨大的价值，本地网络效应驱动了该价值的相当一部分，并且这些效应在平台、消费者和联系之间有所不同。

### 在短视预期和外部性下的联盟稳定性
**[Coalitional stability under myopic expectations and externalities](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.03259)**

**Authors**: Agustin G. Bonifacio, Maria Haydee Fonseca-Mairena, Pablo Neme

**Abstract**: We study coalition formation problems in the presence of externalities, focusing on settings where agents exhibit myopic expectations, that is, they evaluate potential deviations based solely on the immediate outcome, assuming no further reactions or reorganization by others. First, we establish a sufficient condition for the non-emptiness of both the core and the stable set. In the case of the core, our condition for ensuring non-emptiness also provides a characterization of all core partitions. We then turn our attention to problems with order-preserving preferences. Under our sufficient condition, the core and the stable set not only exist but also coincide, and convergence to a stable outcome is guaranteed. Furthermore, using the notion of absorbing set, we draw a connection between problems with order-preserving preferences and those without externalities. This allows us to lift known core non-emptiness results from the latter setting to the former, thereby establishing a novel bridge between these two classes of problems.

**摘要**: 我们研究了存在外部性情况下的联盟形成问题，重点关注了代理人表现出近视预期的情境，即他们仅基于直接结果评估潜在的偏离，假设其他人不会进一步反应或重组。首先，我们建立了核心和稳定集非空的充分条件。在核心的情况下，我们确保非空的条件还提供了所有核心划分的特征描述。然后，我们将注意力转向具有顺序保持偏好的问题。在我们的充分条件下，核心和稳定集不仅存在，而且重合，保证会收敛到一个稳定的结果。此外，利用吸收集的概念，我们在具有顺序保持偏好的问题和没有外部性的问题之间建立了联系。这使我们能够将后者设置中已知的核心非空结果提升到前者，从而建立了这两类问题之间的新桥梁。

### 非参数回归在观察性数据的成本效益分析中的应用——教程
**[Nonparametric regression for cost-effectiveness analyses with observational data -- a tutorial](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.03511)**

**Authors**: Jonas Esser, Mateus Maia, Judith Bosmans, Johanna van Dongen

**Abstract**: Healthcare decision-making often requires selecting among treatment options under budget constraints, particularly when one option is more effective but also more costly. Cost-effectiveness analysis (CEA) provides a framework for evaluating whether the health benefits of a treatment justify its additional costs. A key component of CEA is the estimation of treatment effects on both health outcomes and costs, which becomes challenging when using observational data, due to potential confounding. While advanced causal inference methods exist for use in such circumstances, their adoption in CEAs remains limited, with many studies relying on overly simplistic methods such as linear regression or propensity score matching. We believe that this is mainly due to health economists being generally unfamiliar with superior methodology. In this paper, we address this gap by introducing cost-effectiveness researchers to modern nonparametric regression models, with a particular focus on Bayesian Additive Regression Trees (BART). We provide practical guidance on how to implement BART in CEAs, including code examples, and discuss its advantages in producing more robust and credible estimates from observational data.

**摘要**: 医疗决策常常需要在预算限制下选择治疗方案，特别是当一个选项更有效但也更昂贵时。成本效益分析（CEA）提供了一个框架，用以评估治疗的健康益处是否证明其额外成本的合理性。CEA的一个关键组成部分是对治疗对健康结果和成本的影响的估计，当使用观察数据时，由于潜在的混杂因素，这变得具有挑战性。尽管存在用于此类情况的先进的因果推断方法，它们在CEA中的采用仍然有限，许多研究依赖于过于简单的方法，如线性回归或倾向得分匹配。我们认为，这主要是因为卫生经济学家普遍不熟悉更优越的方法论。在本文中，我们通过向成本效益研究人员介绍现代非参数回归模型来填补这一空白，特别关注贝叶斯加性回归树（BART）。我们提供了如何在CEA中实施BART的实用指导，包括代码示例，并讨论了它在从观察数据中产生更稳健和可信估计方面的优势。

### 用户位置披露未能阻止海外批评，反而加剧了中国社交媒体上的地区分歧。
**[User Location Disclosure Fails to Deter Overseas Criticism but Amplifies Regional Divisions on Chinese Social Media](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.03238)**

**Authors**: Leo Yang Yang, Yiqing Xu

**Abstract**: We examine the behavioral impact of a user location disclosure policy implemented on Sina Weibo, China's largest microblogging platform, using a high-frequency, real-time dataset of uncensored user engagement with 165 leading government and media accounts. Leveraging a natural experiment result from the platform's sudden rollout of location tagging on April 28, 2022, we compare millions of time-stamped observations of user behavior in the comment sections of these accounts before and after the policy change. Although the policy appeared intended to deter overseas users from spreading information deemed harmful by the regime, we find no reduction in their engagement. Instead, the policy sharply reduced domestic users' willingness to comment on posts about local issues outside their own provinces. This effect was especially pronounced among out-of-province commenters and disproportionately curtailed criticisms. Using large language models, we further show that location disclosure triggered a rise in regionally discriminatory replies, which in turn heightened the perceived risk of cross-provincial engagement and reshaped the norms of online participation. Our findings suggest that authoritarian regimes can reinforce censorship not only through top-down control, but by mobilizing social cleavages, here, regional divisions, to suppress dissent and fragment public discourse.

**摘要**: 我们研究了中国最大的微博平台——新浪微博上实施的用户位置披露政策对用户行为的影响，使用了与165个主要政府和媒体账户相关的高频、实时的未审查用户互动数据集。利用该平台于2022年4月28日突然推出位置标记的自然实验结果，我们比较了政策变化前后这些账户评论部分中数百万带有时间戳的用户行为观察结果。尽管该政策看似旨在阻止海外用户传播被政权视为有害的信息，但我们发现他们的参与度并没有减少。相反，该政策大幅降低了国内用户对本地问题帖子发表评论的意愿，尤其是在他们自己省份之外的问题。这一效应在跨省评论者中尤为明显，并且不成比例地遏制了批评。利用大型语言模型，我们进一步表明位置披露引发了地域歧视性回复的增加，这反过来又提高了跨省参与的感知风险，并重塑了在线参与的规范。我们的发现表明，威权政权不仅可以通过自上而下的控制来加强审查，还可以通过动员社会分裂，即这里的地域分歧，来压制异议和分裂公共话语。

### 数据驱动的说服
**[Data-Driven Persuasion](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.03203)**

**Authors**: Maxwell Rosenthal

**Abstract**: This paper develops a data-driven approach to Bayesian persuasion. **[Heterogeneous participation and allocation skews: when is choice "worth it"?](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.03600)**

**Authors**: Nikhil Garg

**Abstract**: A core ethos of the Economics and Computation (EconCS) community is that people have complex private preferences and information of which the central planner is unaware, but which an appropriately designed mechanism can uncover to improve collective decisionmaking. This ethos underlies the community's largest deployed success stories, from stable matching systems to participatory budgeting. I ask: is this choice and information aggregation ``worth it''? In particular, I discuss how such systems induce \textit{heterogeneous participation}: those already relatively advantaged are, empirically, more able to pay time costs and navigate administrative burdens imposed by the mechanisms. I draw on three case studies, including my own work -- complex democratic mechanisms, resident crowdsourcing, and school matching. I end with lessons for practice and research, challenging the community to help reduce participation heterogeneity and design and deploy mechanisms that meet a ``best of both worlds'' north star: \textit{use preferences and information from those who choose to participate, but provide a ``sufficient'' quality of service to those who do not.}

**摘要**: 经济学与计算（EconCS）社区的一个核心理念是，人们拥有复杂的私人偏好和信息，中央规划者并不了解这些信息，但是一个适当设计的机制可以揭示这些信息，以改善集体决策。这一理念是该社区最大的部署成功案例的基础，从稳定的匹配系统到参与式预算编制。我想问：这种选择和信息聚合“值得吗”？特别是，我讨论了这样的系统如何诱导“异质性参与”：那些已经相对处于优势地位的人，在经验上，更有能力支付时间成本和应对机制所施加的行政负担。我借鉴了三个案例研究，包括我自己的工作——复杂的民主机制、居民众包和学校匹配。最后，我总结了实践和研究的教训，挑战社区帮助减少参与的异质性，并设计和部署那些达到“两全其美”的北极星目标的机制：利用那些选择参与的人的偏好和信息，但为那些不参与的人提供“足够”的服务质量。

### 一种新的高效去偏估计一般处理模型的方法，通过平衡神经网络加权
**[A New and Efficient Debiased Estimation of General Treatment Models by Balanced Neural Networks Weighting](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.04044)**

**Authors**: Zeqi Wu, Meilin Wang, Wei Huang, Zheng Zhang

**Abstract**: Estimation and inference of treatment effects under unconfounded treatment assignments often suffer from bias and the `curse of dimensionality' due to the nonparametric estimation of nuisance parameters for high-dimensional confounders. Although debiased state-of-the-art methods have been proposed for binary treatments under particular treatment models, they can be unstable for small sample sizes. Moreover, directly extending them to general treatment models can lead to computational complexity. We propose a balanced neural networks weighting method for general treatment models, which leverages deep neural networks to alleviate the curse of dimensionality while retaining optimal covariate balance through calibration, thereby achieving debiased and robust estimation. Our method accommodates a wide range of treatment models, including average, quantile, distributional, and asymmetric least squares treatment effects, for discrete, continuous, and mixed treatments. Under regularity conditions, we show that our estimator achieves rate double robustness and $\sqrt{N}$-asymptotic normality, and its asymptotic variance achieves the semiparametric efficiency bound. We further develop a statistical inference procedure based on weighted bootstrap, which avoids estimating the efficient influence/score functions. Simulation results reveal that the proposed method consistently outperforms existing alternatives, especially when the sample size is small. Applications to the 401(k) dataset and the Mother's Significant Features dataset further illustrate the practical value of the method for estimating both average and quantile treatment effects under binary and continuous treatments, respectively.

**摘要**: 在未混杂处理分配下估计和推断处理效应时，由于高维混杂因素的非参数估计，常常受到偏差和“维数诅咒”的影响。尽管已经提出了针对二元处理在特定处理模型下的去偏最先进方法，但它们在小样本量下可能不稳定。此外，直接将它们扩展到一般处理模型可能导致计算复杂性。我们提出了一种平衡神经网络加权方法，用于一般处理模型，该方法利用深度神经网络来缓解维数诅咒，同时通过校准保持最优协变量平衡，从而实现去偏和稳健估计。我们的方法适用于包括平均值、分位数、分布和不对称最小二乘处理效应在内的广泛处理模型，适用于离散、连续和混合处理。在正则性条件下，我们证明了我们的估计器实现了双重稳健性速率和$\sqrt{N}$-渐近正态性，其渐近方差达到了半参数效率界限。我们进一步开发了一种基于加权bootstrap的统计推断程序，避免了估计高效影响/得分函数。模拟结果表明，所提出的方法在小样本量时一致优于现有替代方案。对401(k)数据集和母亲重要特征数据集的应用进一步说明了该方法在估计二元和连续处理下的平均值和分位数处理效应方面的实际价值。

### 事前真实分布报告机制
**[Ex-Ante Truthful Distribution-Reporting Mechanisms](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.04030)**

**Authors**: Xiaotie Deng, Yanru Guan, Ningyuan Li, Zihe Wang, Jie Zhang

**Abstract**: This paper studies mechanism design for revenue maximization in a distribution-reporting setting, where the auctioneer does not know the buyers' true value distributions. Instead, each buyer reports and commits to a bid distribution in the ex-ante stage, which the auctioneer uses as input to the mechanism. Buyers strategically decide the reported distributions to maximize ex-ante utility, potentially deviating from their value distributions. As shown in previous work, classical prior-dependent mechanisms such as the Myerson auction fail to elicit truthful value distributions at the ex-ante stage, despite satisfying Bayesian incentive compatibility at the interim stage. We study the design of ex-ante incentive compatible mechanisms, and aim to maximize revenue in a prior-independent approximation framework. We introduce a family of threshold-augmented mechanisms, which ensures ex-ante incentive compatibility while boosting revenue through ex-ante thresholds. Based on these mechanisms, we construct the Peer-Max Mechanism, which achieves an either-or approximation guarantee for general non-identical distributions. Specifically, for any value distributions, its expected revenue either achieves a constant fraction of the optimal social welfare, or surpasses the second-price revenue by a constant fraction, where the constants depend on the number of buyers and a tunable parameter. We also provide an upper bound on the revenue achievable by any ex-ante incentive compatible mechanism, matching our lower bound up to a constant factor. Finally, we extend our approach to a setting where multiple units of identical items are sold to buyers with multi-unit demands.

**摘要**: 本文研究了在分配报告环境中，为了最大化收入而设计的机制，拍卖人不知道买家的真实价值分布。相反，每位买家在事前阶段报告并承诺一个出价分布，拍卖人将此作为机制的输入。买家会策略性地决定报告的分布以最大化事前效用，可能会偏离他们的价值分布。正如先前的工作所示，像Myerson拍卖这样的经典先验依赖机制未能在事前阶段引出真实的价值分布，尽管它们在临时阶段满足了贝叶斯激励兼容性。我们研究了事前激励兼容机制的设计，并旨在先验独立的近似框架中最大化收入。我们引入了一类阈值增强机制，这些机制确保了事前激励兼容性，同时通过事前阈值提高收入。基于这些机制，我们构建了Peer-Max机制，该机制为一般非相同分布提供了一种非此即彼的近似保证。具体来说，对于任何价值分布，其预期收入要么实现了最优社会福利的一定比例，要么超过了第二价格收入的一定比例，其中常数取决于买家数量和一个可调参数。我们还提供了任何事前激励兼容机制所能实现收入的上界，与我们的下界匹配，直到一个常数因子。最后，我们将我们的方法扩展到一个设置中，即向具有多单位需求的买家出售多个相同物品的单位。

### 在基数偏好下的概率序列机制的紧密效率界限
**[Tight Efficiency Bounds for the Probabilistic Serial Mechanism under Cardinal Preferences](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2507.03359)**

**Authors**: Jugal Garg, Yixin Tao, László A. Végh

**Abstract**: The Probabilistic Serial (PS) mechanism -- also known as the simultaneous eating algorithm -- is a canonical solution for the assignment problem under ordinal preferences. It guarantees envy-freeness and ordinal efficiency in the resulting random assignment. However, under cardinal preferences, its efficiency may degrade significantly: it is known that PS may yield allocations that are $\Omega(\ln{n})$-worse than Pareto optimal, but whether this bound is tight remained an open question.
Our first result resolves this question by showing that the PS mechanism guarantees $(\ln(n)+2)$-approximate Pareto efficiency, even in the more general submodular setting introduced by Fujishige, Sano, and Zhan (ACM TEAC 2018). This is established by showing that, although the PS mechanism may incur a loss of up to $O(\sqrt{n})$ in utilitarian social welfare, it still achieves a $(\ln{n}+2)$-approximation to the maximum Nash welfare. In addition, we present a polynomial-time algorithm that computes an allocation which is envy-free and $e^{1/e}$-approximately Pareto-efficient, answering an open question posed by Tröbst and Vazirani (EC 2024).
The PS mechanism also applies to the allocation of chores instead of goods. We prove that it guarantees an $n$-approximately Pareto-efficient allocation in this setting, and that this bound is asymptotically tight. This result provides the first known approximation guarantee for computing a fair and efficient allocation in the assignment problem with chores under cardinal preferences.

**摘要**: 概率序列（PS）机制——也称为同时进食算法——是序数偏好下分配问题的典型解决方案。它保证了结果随机分配中的无嫉妒性和序数效率。然而，在基数偏好下，其效率可能会显著下降：已知PS可能会产生比帕累托最优差$\Omega(\ln{n})$的分配，但这个界限是否紧密仍然是一个未解决的问题。
我们的第一个结果通过展示PS机制即使在由Fujishige、Sano和Zhan（ACM TEAC 2018）引入的更一般的次模设置中，也能保证$(\ln(n)+2)$-近似帕累托效率，从而解决了这个问题。这是通过展示PS机制虽然可能在功利主义社会福利中造成高达$O(\sqrt{n})$的损失，但它仍然实现了对最大纳什福利的$(\ln{n}+2)$-近似来建立的。此外，我们提出了一个多项式时间算法，用于计算一个无嫉妒且$e^{1/e}$-近似帕累托效率的分配，回答了Tröbst和Vazirani（EC 2024）提出的一个未解决问题。
PS机制也适用于家务的分配而不是商品。我们证明，在这种设置中，它保证了$n$-近似帕累托效率的分配，并且这个界限是渐近紧密的。这个结果为在基数偏好下，计算家务分配问题中的公平和有效分配提供了第一个已知的近似保证。
