### 在市场中关于资产价值范围的信息披露
**[The disclosure of information about the range of asset value in market](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.11405)**

**Authors**: Jianhao Su, Yanliang Zhang

**Abstract**: The information released to investors in financial markets has various forms. We refer to range information as information about the upper and lower bound which the payoff of a risky asset may reach in the future. This study develops rational expectation models to explore the market impacts of disclosure of range information. Our model shows that its disclosure can decrease the sensitivity of market price to private signal and increase market liquidity. The market impact of its disclosure depends on the position and precision of the disclosed range. When the linear combination of private signal and noise trading volume is distant from the disclosed range, the reaction of price to a variation in private signal will almost vanish, whereas a movement of the disclosed range can affect the price efficiently. If the midpoint of the disclosed range is higher (lower) than a criterion which is specified in this study, the disclosure will reduce (raise) asset premium.

**摘要**: 在金融市场中，向投资者披露的信息有多种形式。我们将范围信息定义为关于风险资产未来可能达到的收益上限和下限的信息。本研究发展了理性预期模型，以探索披露范围信息对市场的影响。我们的模型显示，披露范围信息可以降低市场价格对私人信号的敏感性，并增加市场流动性。其披露对市场的影响取决于所披露范围的位置和精确度。当私人信号与噪声交易量的线性组合远离所披露的范围时，价格对私人信号变化的反应几乎会消失，而披露范围的变动可以有效地影响价格。如果所披露范围的中点高于（低于）本研究中指定的标准，那么披露将降低（提高）资产溢价。

### 带有朋友和敌人的同伴选择
**[Peer Selection with Friends and Enemies](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.11157)**

**Authors**: Francis Bloch, Bhaskar Dutta, Marcin Dziubiński

**Abstract**: A planner wants to select one agent out of n agents on the basis of a binary characteristic that is commonly known to all agents but is not observed by the planner. Any pair of agents can either be friends or enemies or impartials of each other. An individual's most preferred outcome is that she be selected. If she is not selected, then she would prefer that a friend be selected, and if neither she herself or a friend is selected, then she would prefer that an impartial agent be selected. Finally, her least preferred outcome is that an enemy be selected. The planner wants to design a dominant strategy incentive compatible mechanism in order to be able choose a desirable agent. We derive sufficient conditions for existence of efficient and DSIC mechanisms when the planner knows the bilateral relationships between agents. We also show that if the planner does not know these relationships, then there is no efficient and DSIC mechanism and we compare the relative efficiency of two ``second-best'' DSIC mechanisms. Finally, we obtain sharp characterization results when the network of friends and enemies satisfies structural balance.

**摘要**: 一个规划者想要在n个代理中选择一个代理，这个选择基于一个所有代理都普遍知道但规划者没有观察到的二元特征。任何两个代理之间可以是朋友、敌人或彼此中立。一个人最希望的结果是她被选中。如果她没有被选中，那么她更希望一个朋友被选中，如果她自己或一个朋友都没有被选中，那么她更希望一个中立的代理被选中。最后，她最不希望的结果是她的敌人被选中。规划者想要设计一个占优策略激励相容机制，以便能够选择一个理想的代理。我们推导出了当规划者知道代理之间的双边关系时，存在有效和DSIC机制的充分条件。我们还展示了如果规划者不知道这些关系，那么就不存在有效和DSIC机制，我们比较了两种“次优”DSIC机制的相对效率。最后，当朋友和敌人的网络满足结构平衡时，我们得到了清晰的特征结果。

### 分散人们对爱泼斯坦文件的注意力？媒体关注与特朗普在Truth Social帖子上的短期变化
**[Distracting from the Epstein files? Media attention and short-run shifts in Trump's Truth Social posts](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.11532)**

**Authors**: Andrew J. Peterson

**Abstract**: Political "circuses" may undermine democratic accountability if leaders facing scandal can reliably pull media coverage toward fresh topics and away from substantive investigations or evaluations. We investigate whether politicians strategically alter their messaging during damaging media coverage ("strategic diversion") or maintain consistent provocative communication regardless of scandal coverage ("always-on circus"). Using computational text analysis of Donald Trump's Truth Social posts during the 2025 Epstein revelations, we find that a one-standard-deviation increase in scandal coverage is associated with communication patterns that deviate from baseline by 0.28 standard deviations over a 4-day window. Although these findings do not provide formal causal identification, they are robust to timing placebos and falsification tests, are consistent with the interpretation that leaders may deploy diversionary communication specifically within their own friendly media ecosystem, which has implications for accountability in polarized democracies.

**摘要**: 政治“马戏”可能会破坏民主问责制，如果面临丑闻的领导人能够可靠地将媒体报道引向新话题，远离实质性的调查或评估。我们调查了政治家是否在损害性媒体报道期间策略性地改变他们的信息传递（“战略性转移”），或者无论丑闻报道如何都保持一致的挑衅性沟通（“始终如一的马戏”）。通过使用计算文本分析唐纳德·特朗普在2025年爱泼斯坦事件揭露期间的Truth Social帖子，我们发现丑闻报道增加一个标准差与4天内基线偏差0.28个标准差的沟通模式相关联。虽然这些发现没有提供正式的因果识别，但它们对时间安慰剂和证伪测试是稳健的，与领导人可能在其自己的友好媒体生态系统中特别部署转移性沟通的解释一致，这对两极分化的民主国家的问责制有影响。

### 一个简单的模型，用于描述场外批发市场中新鲜产品的人口动态。
**[A simple model for the population dynamics in OTC wholesale fresh product markets](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.11024)**

**Authors**: Ali Ellouze, Bastien Fernandez

**Abstract**: Given the combined evidences of bounded rationality, limited information and short-term optimization, over-the-counter (OTC) fresh product markets provide a perfect instance where to develop a behavioural approach to the analysis of micro-economic systems. Aiming at characterizing via a rigorous mathematical analysis, the main features of the spontaneous organization and functioning of such markets, we introduce and we study a stylized dynamical model for the time evolution of buyers populations and prices/attractiveness at each wholesaler. The dynamics is governed by immediate reactions of the actors to changes in basic indicators. Buyers are influenced by some degree of loyalty to their regular suppliers. Yet, at times, they also prospect for potential better offers. On the other hand, sellers primarily aim at maximising their profit. Yet, they can be also prone to improving their competitiveness in case of clientele deficit.
Our results reveal that, in spite of being governed by simple and immediate rules, the competition between sellers self-regulates in time, as it constrains to bounded ranges the dispersion of both prices and clientele volumes, does similarly for the mean clientele volume, and it generates oscillatory behaviours that prevent any seller to dominate permanently its competitors (and to be dominated forever). Long-term behaviours are also investigated, with focus on asymptotic convergence to an equilibrium, as can be expected for a standard functioning mode. In particular, in the simplest case of 2 competing sellers, a normal-form-like analysis proves that such convergence holds, provided that the buyer's loyalty is sufficiently high or the sellers' reactivity is sufficiently low. In other words, this result identifies and proves those characteristics of the system that are responsible for long term stability and asymptotic damping of the oscillations.

**摘要**: 鉴于有限理性、信息有限和短期优化的综合证据，场外（OTC）生鲜产品市场为发展微观经济系统的行为分析方法提供了一个完美的实例。我们的目标是通过严谨的数学分析来表征这些市场的自发组织和运作的主要特征，我们引入并研究了一个理想化的动态模型，用于分析买家群体的时间演变以及每个批发商的价格/吸引力。动态变化受行为者对基本指标变化的即时反应所支配。买家受到一定程度的对常规供应商的忠诚度影响。然而，有时他们也在寻找潜在的更好报价。另一方面，卖家主要目标是最大化他们的利润。然而，在客户流失的情况下，他们也可能会提高自己的竞争力。

我们的结果揭示了，尽管受到简单和直接规则的支配，卖家之间的竞争会随时间自我调节，因为它将价格和客户量的分散限制在有限的范围内，对平均客户量也做同样的限制，并产生振荡行为，防止任何卖家永久性地主导其竞争对手（并永远被主导）。我们还研究了长期行为，重点关注对均衡的渐近收敛，这是标准运作模式所预期的。特别是在最简单的2个竞争卖家的情况下，类似正常形式的分析证明了这种收敛成立，前提是买家的忠诚度足够高或卖家的反应性足够低。换句话说，这个结果识别并证明了系统负责长期稳定性和振荡渐近衰减的特征。

### 完整交换机制
**[Complete Exchange Mechanisms](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.11278)**

**Authors**: Minoru Kitahara, Hiroshi Uno

**Abstract**: This paper studies one-sided matching under a complete exchange (CE) requirement, where each agent must be assigned an object different from its initial endowment. We introduce assignment partition -- a partition of agents and choice sets that builds CE into feasibility -- and, within this structure, propose two new mechanisms.
Chain Serial Dictatorship (C-SD) operates within the partition as a binding-choice chain: the highest-priority agent picks from its allowed set and the right to pick passes to the owner of the chosen object; if that owner has already picked, the right reverts to the highest-priority remaining agent. Two-Stage Serial Dictatorship (T-SD) operates within the partition as a nominate-then-assign procedure: in Stage 1, agents tentatively nominate objects in exogenous priority, and the owners of nominated objects determine an endogenous final priority; in Stage 2, serial dictatorship runs within the partition using that final priority. For any given assignment partition, C-SD and T-SD simultaneously satisfy strategy-proofness, respecting improvement, and efficiency relative to the partition.
We then examine the limits of pursuing market-wide efficiency under the CE constraint. As a benchmark, we study a modified TTC, CE-TTC, which first enforces a CE-compliant reassignment and then runs a self-avoiding top-trading-cycles phase; CE-TTC achieves efficiency within the CE constraint and strategy-proofness but fails respecting improvement. Moreover, for three and four agents, no mechanism can simultaneously achieve efficiency within the CE constraint, respecting improvement, and strategy-proofness. These findings underscore the value of partition-based design for truthful implementation with investment incentives under a hard CE mandate.

**摘要**: 本文研究了在完全交换（CE）要求下的单边匹配问题，其中每个代理人必须被分配一个与其初始禀赋不同的对象。我们引入了分配分区——一种构建CE可行性的代理人和选择集的分区——并在这一结构内提出了两种新机制。
链式序列独裁（C-SD）在分区内作为一个具有约束选择权的链条运作：最优先的代理人从其允许的集合中挑选，挑选权传递给被选中对象的所有者；如果该所有者已经挑选，挑选权则转回给剩余的最优先代理人。两阶段序列独裁（T-SD）在分区内作为一个提名然后分配的程序运作：在第一阶段，代理人根据外生的优先级试探性地提名对象，被提名对象的所有者确定一个内生的最终优先级；在第二阶段，使用该最终优先级在分区内运行序列独裁。对于任何给定的分配分区，C-SD和T-SD同时满足策略证明性、尊重改进和相对于分区的效率。
然后，我们检验了在CE约束下追求市场范围效率的局限性。作为一个基准，我们研究了一个修改后的TTC，CE-TTC，它首先执行一个符合CE的重新分配，然后运行一个自我避免的顶级交易周期阶段；CE-TTC在CE约束内实现了效率和策略证明性，但未能尊重改进。此外，对于三名和四名代理人，没有任何机制能够同时实现在CE约束内的效率、尊重改进和策略证明性。这些发现强调了在硬性CE任务下，基于分区的设计对于真实实施和投资激励的价值。

### 人工智能与员工福祉：不同世代群体和性别之间的差异影响
**[AI and Worker Well-Being: Differential Impacts Across Generational Cohorts and Genders](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.11021)**

**Authors**: Voraprapa Nakavachara

**Abstract**: This paper investigates the relationship between AI use and worker well-being outcomes such as mental health, job enjoyment, and physical health and safety, using microdata from the OECD AI Surveys across seven countries. The results reveal that AI users are significantly more likely to report improvements across all three outcomes, with effects ranging from 8.9% to 21.3%. However, these benefits vary by generation and gender. Generation Y (1981-1996) shows the strongest gains across all dimensions, while Generation X (1965-1980) reports moderate improvements in mental health and job enjoyment. In contrast, Generation Z (1997-2012) benefits only in job enjoyment. As digital natives already familiar with technology, Gen Z workers may not receive additional gains in mental or physical health from AI, though they still experience increased enjoyment from using it. Baby Boomers (born before 1965) experience limited benefits, as they may not find these tools as engaging or useful. Women report stronger mental health gains, whereas men report greater improvements in physical health. These findings suggest that AI's workplace impact is uneven and shaped by demographic factors, career stage, and the nature of workers' roles.

**摘要**: 本文利用经合组织人工智能调查的微观数据，调查了人工智能使用与工人福祉结果之间的关系，包括心理健康、工作享受以及身体健康和安全。结果显示，人工智能用户在所有三个结果上都有显著的改善，效果范围从8.9%到21.3%。然而，这些好处因世代和性别而异。Y世代（1981-1996年出生）在所有维度上显示出最强的增长，而X世代（1965-1980年出生）在心理健康和工作享受上报告了适度的改善。相比之下，Z世代（1997-2012年出生）仅在工作享受上受益。作为已经熟悉技术的数字原住民，Z世代工人可能不会从人工智能中获得额外的心理或身体健康收益，尽管他们仍然从使用中获得更多的享受。婴儿潮一代（1965年之前出生）体验到的好处有限，因为他们可能不会觉得这些工具那么吸引人或有用。女性报告了更强的心理健康收益，而男性则报告了身体健康的更大改善。这些发现表明，人工智能在工作场所的影响是不均匀的，并且受到人口统计因素、职业阶段和工人角色性质的影响。

### 非对称离散布洛托上校博弈
**[Non-symmetric discrete Colonel Blotto game](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.10827)**

**Authors**: Marcin Dziubiński

**Abstract**: We study equilibrium strategies and the value of the asymmetric variant of the discrete Colonel Blotto game with $K \geq 2$ battlefields, $B \geq 1$ resources of the weaker player and $A > B$ resources of the stronger player. We derive equilibrium strategies and the formulas for the value of the game for the cases where the number of resources of the weaker player, $B$, is at least $2(\lceil A/K \rceil - 1)$ as well as for the cases where this number is at most $\lfloor A/K \rfloor$. In particular, we solve all the cases of the game which can be solved using the discrete General Lotto game of~\cite{Hart08}. We propose a constrained variant of the discrete General Lotto game and use it to derive equilibrium strategies in the discrete Colonel Blotto game, that go beyond the General Lotto solvable cases game.

**摘要**: 我们研究了具有$K \geq 2$个战场、较弱玩家拥有$B \geq 1$个资源和较强玩家拥有$A > B$个资源的离散型不对称布洛托游戏的均衡策略和游戏价值。我们推导出了当较弱玩家的资源数量$B$至少为$2(\lceil A/K \rceil - 1)$以及当这个数量最多为$\lfloor A/K \rfloor$时的均衡策略和游戏价值的公式。特别是，我们解决了所有可以通过离散型通用乐透游戏解决的布洛托游戏案例~\cite{Hart08}。我们提出了一种受限的离散型通用乐透游戏变体，并用它来推导离散型布洛托游戏中的均衡策略，这些策略超越了通用乐透游戏可解案例游戏。

### 在依赖数据的双重/去偏机器学习中的邻里稳定性
**[Neighborhood Stability in Double/Debiased Machine Learning with Dependent Data](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.10995)**

**Authors**: Jianfei Cao, Michael P. Leung

**Abstract**: This paper studies double/debiased machine learning (DML) methods applied to weakly dependent data. We allow observations to be situated in a general metric space that accommodates spatial and network data. Existing work implements cross-fitting by excluding from the training fold observations sufficiently close to the evaluation fold. We find in simulations that this can result in exceedingly small training fold sizes, particularly with network data. We therefore seek to establish the validity of DML without cross-fitting, building on recent work by Chen et al. (2022). They study i.i.d. data and require the machine learner to satisfy a natural stability condition requiring insensitivity to data perturbations that resample a single observation. We extend these results to dependent data by strengthening stability to "neighborhood stability," which requires insensitivity to resampling observations in any slowly growing neighborhood. We show that existing results on the stability of various machine learners can be adapted to verify neighborhood stability.

**摘要**: 本文研究了应用于弱依赖数据的双重/去偏机器学习方法（DML）。我们允许观测值位于一个通用的度量空间中，以适应空间和网络数据。现有工作通过从训练数据中排除与评估数据足够接近的观测值来实现交叉拟合。我们在模拟中发现，这可能会导致训练数据集的规模异常小，特别是在网络数据中。因此，我们寻求在不进行交叉拟合的情况下建立DML的有效性，基于Chen等人（2022年）的最新工作。他们研究了独立同分布（i.i.d.）数据，并要求机器学习器满足一个自然的稳定性条件，即对重新抽样单个观测值的数据扰动不敏感。我们将这些结果扩展到依赖数据，通过将稳定性加强为“邻域稳定性”，这要求对在任何缓慢增长的邻域中重新抽样观测值不敏感。我们展示了现有的关于各种机器学习器稳定性的结果可以被适应以验证邻域稳定性。

### 美国房地产市场的季节性：大流行后的转变与区域动态
**[Seasonality in the U.S. Housing Market: Post-Pandemic Shifts and Regional Dynamics](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.10808)**

**Authors**: Yihan Hu, Yifei Huang, Weizhao Wang

**Abstract**: Seasonality has traditionally shaped the U.S. housing market, with activity peaking in spring-summer and declining in autumn-winter. However, recent disruptions, particularly post-COVID-19, raise questions about shift in these patterns. This study analyzes housing market date (1991-2024) to examine evolving seasonality and regional heterogeneity. Using Housing Price Index (HPI), inventory and sales data from the Federal Housing Finance Agency and U.S. Census Bureau, seasonal components are extracted via the X-13-ARIMA procedure, and statistical tests assess variations across regions. The results confirm seasonal fluctuations in prices and volumes, with recent shifts toward earlier annual peak (March-April) and amplified seasonal effects.
