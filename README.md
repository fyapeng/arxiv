### 在有限总体中检验单调性
**[Testing Monotonicity in a Finite Population](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.25032)**

**Authors**: Jiafeng Chen, Jonathan Roth, Jann Spiess

**Abstract**: We consider the extent to which we can learn from a completely randomized experiment whether everyone has treatment effects that are weakly of the same sign, a condition we call monotonicity. From a classical sampling perspective, it is well-known that monotonicity is untestable. By contrast, we show from the design-based perspective -- in which the units in the population are fixed and only treatment assignment is stochastic -- that the distribution of treatment effects in the finite population (and hence whether monotonicity holds) is formally identified. We argue, however, that the usual definition of identification is unnatural in the design-based setting because it imagines knowing the distribution of outcomes over different treatment assignments for the same units. We thus evaluate the informativeness of the data by the extent to which it enables frequentist testing and Bayesian updating. We show that frequentist tests can have nontrivial power against some alternatives, but power is generically limited. Likewise, we show that there exist (non-degenerate) Bayesian priors that never update about whether monotonicity holds. We conclude that, despite the formal identification result, the ability to learn about monotonicity from data in practice is severely limited.

**摘要**: 我们考虑在完全随机实验中，我们能否学习到每个人是否都具有相同符号的治疗效应，我们将这一条件称为单调性。从经典的抽样视角来看，众所周知单调性是不可测试的。相比之下，我们从基于设计的视角——即在该视角中，总体中的单位是固定的，只有治疗分配是随机的——展示了有限总体中治疗效应的分布（因此是否持有单调性）是正式被识别的。然而，我们认为，在基于设计的背景下，通常的识别定义是不自然的，因为它想象着知道相同单位在不同治疗分配下的结果分布。因此，我们通过数据能够使频率主义测试和贝叶斯更新到何种程度来评估数据的信息量。我们展示了频率主义测试可以对某些替代方案具有非平凡的效力，但效力通常是受限的。同样，我们展示了存在（非退化的）贝叶斯先验，它们从不更新关于是否持有单调性的信息。我们得出结论，尽管有正式的识别结果，但在实践中从数据中学习单调性的能力是严重受限的。

### 消费者遗憾频率的前因：决策主体性、地位信号传递和在线购物偏好的作用
**[Antecedents of Consumer Regret Frequency: The Roles of Decision Agency, Status Signaling, and Online Shopping Preference](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.24862)**

**Authors**: Shawn Berry

**Abstract**: Consumer regret is a widespread post-purchase emotion that significantly impacts satisfaction, product returns, complaint behavior, and customer loyalty. Despite its prevalence, there is a limited understanding of why certain consumers experience regret more frequently as a chronic aspect of their engagement in the marketplace. This study explores the antecedents of consumer regret frequency by integrating decision agency, status signaling motivations, and online shopping preferences into a cohesive framework. By analyzing survey data (n=338), we assess whether consumers' perceived agency and decision-making orientation correlate with the frequency of regret, and whether tendencies towards status-related consumption and preferences for online shopping environments exacerbate regret through mechanisms such as increased social comparison, expanded choice sets, and continuous exposure to alternative offers. The findings reveal that regret frequency is significantly linked to individual differences in decision-related orientations and status signaling, with a preference for online shopping further contributing to regret-prone consumption behaviors. These results extend the scope of regret and cognitive dissonance research beyond isolated decision episodes by emphasizing regret frequency as a persistent consumer outcome. From a managerial standpoint, the findings suggest that retailers can alleviate regret-driven dissatisfaction by enhancing decision support, minimizing choice overload, and developing post-purchase reassurance strategies tailored to segments prone to regret..

**摘要**: 消费者遗憾是一种普遍的购买后情绪，显著影响满意度、产品退货、投诉行为和顾客忠诚度。尽管它很普遍，但对于某些消费者为何更频繁地将遗憾作为一种长期市场参与的特征，人们的理解仍然有限。本研究通过将决策主体性、地位信号动机和在线购物偏好整合到一个统一的框架中，探索消费者遗憾频率的前因。通过分析调查数据（n=338），我们评估消费者感知的主体性和决策导向是否与遗憾频率相关，以及与地位相关的消费倾向和对在线购物环境的偏好是否通过增加社会比较、扩大选择集和持续接触替代品等机制加剧遗憾。研究结果揭示了遗憾频率与决策相关取向和地位信号的个体差异显著相关，而对在线购物的偏好进一步促成了易产生遗憾的消费行为。这些结果将遗憾和认知失调研究的范围扩展到了孤立的决策事件之外，强调遗憾频率作为一种持续的消费者结果。从管理的角度来看，这些发现表明零售商可以通过增强决策支持、最小化选择过载和为易产生遗憾的细分市场制定购买后保证策略，来减轻由遗憾引发的不满。

First, (a) it examines the contemporary landscape and proposes precise definitions for the key notions involved, ranging from intelligence to Agentic AI. ### 大型语言模型对在线新闻消费和生产的影响
**[The Impact of LLMs on Online News Consumption and Production](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.24968)**

**Authors**: Hangcheng Zhao, Ron Berman

**Abstract**: Large language models (LLMs) change how consumers acquire information online; their bots also crawl news publishers' websites for training data and to answer consumer queries; and they provide tools that can lower the cost of content creation. These changes lead to predictions of adverse impact on news publishers in the form of lowered consumer demand, reduced demand for newsroom employees, and an increase in news "slop." Consequently, some publishers strategically responded by blocking LLM access to their websites using the this http URL file standard.
Using high-frequency granular data, we document four effects related to the predicted shifts in news publishing following the introduction of generative AI (GenAI). First, we find a consistent and moderate decline in traffic to news publishers occurring after August 2024. Second, using a difference-in-differences approach, we find that blocking GenAI bots can have adverse effects on large publishers by reducing total website traffic by 23% and real consumer traffic by 14% compared to not blocking. Third, on the hiring side, we do not find evidence that LLMs are replacing editorial or content-production jobs yet. The share of new editorial and content-production job listings increases over time. Fourth, regarding content production, we find no evidence that large publishers increased text volume; instead, they significantly increased rich content and use more advertising and targeting technologies.
Together, these findings provide early evidence of some unforeseen impacts of the introduction of LLMs on news production and consumption.

**摘要**: 大型语言模型（LLMs）改变了消费者在线获取信息的方式；它们的机器人还爬取新闻发布者的网站以获取训练数据和回答消费者查询；它们提供的工具可以降低内容创作的成本。这些变化导致预测新闻发布者将受到不利影响，表现为消费者需求降低、新闻编辑室员工需求减少以及新闻“粗制滥造”的增加。因此，一些发布者策略性地通过使用这个http URL文件标准阻止LLM访问他们的网站来做出回应。
利用高频详细数据，我们记录了与生成性人工智能（GenAI）引入后新闻出版预测变化相关的四种效应。首先，我们发现在2024年8月之后，新闻发布者的流量出现了一致且适度的下降。其次，使用差异对比方法，我们发现阻止GenAI机器人访问可能会对大型发布者产生不利影响，与不阻止相比，总网站流量减少了23%，实际消费者流量减少了14%。第三，在招聘方面，我们没有发现LLMs正在取代编辑或内容制作工作的证据。新的编辑和内容制作工作招聘信息的比例随时间增加。第四，关于内容生产，我们没有发现大型发布者增加了文本量；相反，他们显著增加了丰富内容，并更多地使用广告和定位技术。
综合这些发现，为LLMs引入对新闻生产和消费的一些未预见影响提供了早期证据。

### 二项式复合估计
**[Compound Estimation for Binomials](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.25042)**

**Authors**: Yan Chen, Lihua Lei

**Abstract**: Many applications involve estimating the mean of multiple binomial outcomes as a common problem -- assessing intergenerational mobility of census tracts, estimating prevalence of infectious diseases across countries, and measuring click-through rates for different demographic groups. The most standard approach is to report the plain average of each outcome. Despite simplicity, the estimates are noisy when the sample sizes or mean parameters are small. In contrast, the Empirical Bayes (EB) methods are able to boost the average accuracy by borrowing information across tasks. Nevertheless, the EB methods require a Bayesian model where the parameters are sampled from a prior distribution which, unlike the commonly-studied Gaussian case, is unidentified due to discreteness of binomial measurements. Even if the prior distribution is known, the computation is difficult when the sample sizes are heterogeneous as there is no simple joint conjugate prior for the sample size and mean parameter.
In this paper, we consider the compound decision framework which treats the sample size and mean parameters as fixed quantities. We develop an approximate Stein's Unbiased Risk Estimator (SURE) for the average mean squared error given any class of estimators. For a class of machine learning-assisted linear shrinkage estimators, we establish asymptotic optimality, regret bounds, and valid inference. Unlike existing work, we work with the binomials directly without resorting to Gaussian approximations. This allows us to work with small sample sizes and/or mean parameters in both one-sample and two-sample settings. We demonstrate our approach using three datasets on firm discrimination, education outcomes, and innovation rates.

**摘要**: 许多应用都涉及到估计多个二项结果的平均值这一共同问题——评估人口普查区域的代际流动性，估计跨国传染病的流行率，以及测量不同人群的点击率。最标准的方法就是报告每个结果的简单平均值。尽管这种方法简单，但当样本量或均值参数较小时，估计值会带有噪声。相比之下，经验贝叶斯（EB）方法能够通过跨任务借用信息来提高平均准确性。然而，EB方法需要一个贝叶斯模型，其中参数是从先验分布中抽取的，这与通常研究的高斯情况不同，由于二项测量的离散性而无法识别。即使已知先验分布，当样本量不同时，计算也变得困难，因为样本量和均值参数之间没有简单的联合共轭先验。

在本文中，我们考虑复合决策框架，将样本量和均值参数视为固定量。我们为任何类别的估计器开发了一个近似的Stein无偏风险估计器（SURE），用于平均均方误差。对于一类机器学习辅助的线性收缩估计器，我们建立了渐近最优性、遗憾界限和有效推断。与现有工作不同，我们直接处理二项分布，而不依赖于高斯近似。这使我们能够在单样本和双样本设置中处理小样本量和/或均值参数。我们使用关于公司歧视、教育成果和创新率的三个数据集来演示我们的方法。

### 扩大慈善激励：政策选择、信念和来自现场实验的证据
**[Scaling Charitable Incentives: Policy Selection, Beliefs, and Evidence from a Field Experiment](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.24852)**

**Authors**: Shusaku Sasaki, Takunori Ishihara, Hirofumi Kurokawa

**Abstract**: Why are interventions with weak evidence still adopted? We study charitable incentives for physical activity in Japan using three linked methods, including a randomized field experiment (N=808), a stakeholder belief survey (local government officials and private-sector employees, N=2,400), and a conjoint experiment on policy choice. Financial incentives increase daily steps by about 1,000, whereas charitable incentives deliver a precisely estimated null. Nonetheless, stakeholders greatly overpredict charitable incentives' effects on walking, participation, and prosociality. Conjoint choices show policymakers value step gains as well as other outcomes, shaping policy choice. Adoption thus reflects multidimensional beliefs and objectives, highlighting policy selection as a scaling challenge.

**摘要**: 为什么证据不足的干预措施仍然被采纳？我们使用三种相互关联的方法研究了日本对体力活动的慈善激励，包括一个随机现场实验（N=808），一个利益相关者信念调查（地方政府官员和私营部门员工，N=2,400），以及一个关于政策选择的联合实验。财务激励使每日步数增加了大约1000步，而慈善激励则产生了精确估计的零效果。尽管如此，利益相关者极大地高估了慈善激励对步行、参与度和亲社会性的影响。联合选择显示政策制定者既重视步数增加，也重视其他结果，这影响了政策选择。因此，采纳反映了多维信念和目标，突显了政策选择作为一个扩大挑战。

### 在一个不平等的世界中的最佳碳价格：地区福利权重的作用
**[Optimal Carbon Prices in an Unequal World: The Role of Regional Welfare Weights](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.24520)**

**Authors**: Simon F. Lang

**Abstract**: How should nations price carbon? This paper examines how the treatment of global inequality, captured by regional welfare weights, affects optimal carbon prices. I develop theory to identify the conditions under which accounting for differences in marginal utilities of consumption across countries leads to more stringent global climate policy in the absence of international transfers. I further establish a connection between the optimal uniform carbon prices implied by different welfare weights and heterogeneous regional preferences over climate policy stringency. In calibrated simulations, I find that accounting for global inequality reduces optimal global emissions relative to an inequality-insensitive benchmark. This holds both when carbon prices are regionally differentiated, with emissions 21% lower, and when they are constrained to be globally uniform, with the uniform carbon price 15% higher.

**摘要**: 国家应如何定价碳排放？本文考察了全球不平等的处理，通过区域福利权重来捕捉，如何影响最优碳价格。我发展了理论，以确定在没有国际转移的情况下，考虑不同国家间消费边际效用差异会导致更严格的全球气候政策的条件。我进一步建立了不同福利权重所暗示的最优统一碳价格与对气候政策严格性的异质区域偏好之间的联系。在校准的模拟中，我发现考虑全球不平等会降低相对于不敏感于不平等的基准的最优全球排放量。这在碳价格区域差异化时成立，排放量降低了21%，而在碳价格被限制为全球统一时也成立，统一碳价格提高了15%。

### 使用工具变量评估反事实政策
**[Evaluating Counterfactual Policies Using Instruments](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.24096)**

**Authors**: Michal Kolesár, José Luis Montiel Olea, Jonathan Roth

**Abstract**: We study settings in which a researcher has an instrumental variable (IV) and seeks to evaluate the effects of a counterfactual policy that alters treatment assignment, such as a directive encouraging randomly assigned judges to release more defendants. We develop a general and computationally tractable framework for computing sharp bounds on the effects of such policies. Our approach does not require the often tenuous IV monotonicity assumption. Moreover, for an important class of policy exercises, we show that IV monotonicity -- while crucial for a causal interpretation of two-stage least squares -- does not tighten the bounds on the counterfactual policy impact. We analyze the identifying power of alternative restrictions, including the policy invariance assumption used in the marginal treatment effect literature, and develop a relaxation of this assumption. We illustrate our framework using applications to quasi-random assignment of bail judges in New York City and prosecutors in Massachusetts.

**摘要**: 我们研究了这样一种情况：研究者拥有一个工具变量（IV），并试图评估改变治疗分配的反事实政策的效果，例如鼓励随机分配的法官释放更多被告的指令。我们为计算这类政策效果的尖锐界限开发了一个通用且计算上可行的框架。我们的方法不需要通常很脆弱的工具变量单调性假设。此外，对于一类重要的政策练习，我们展示了工具变量单调性——尽管对于两阶段最小二乘法的因果解释至关重要——并不会收紧对反事实政策影响的界限。我们分析了替代限制的识别能力，包括边际处理效应文献中使用的政策不变性假设，并发展了这一假设的放松。我们使用纽约市保释法官和马萨诸塞州检察官的准随机分配应用来说明我们的框架。

### 结构化生产系统：可行性
**[Structured Production Systems: Viability](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.24777)**

**Authors**: Robert P. Gilles, Marialaura Pesce

**Abstract**: This paper introduces a novel framework for analysing equilibrium in structured production systems incorporating a static social division of labour by distinguishing between consumption goods traded in competitive markets and intermediate goods exchanged through bilateral relationships. We develop the concept of viability -- the requirement that all producers earn positive incomes -- as a foundational equilibrium prerequisite.
Our main theoretical contribution establishes that acyclic production systems -- those without circular conversion processes among goods -- are always viable, a condition that implies coherence. We characterise completely viable systems through input restrictions demonstrating that prohibiting consumption goods as inputs for other consumption goods is necessary for ensuring viable prices exist for all consumption good price vectors. The analysis reveals fundamental relationships between production system architectural design and economic sustainability.
The introduced framework bridges Leontief-Sraffa production theory with modern network economics while capturing institutional realities of contemporary production systems. This also results in a contribution of the literature on the existence of a positive output price system and the Hawkins-Simon condition.

### 能源共享的自动市场做市

### RepoMech：一种减少回购中介资产负债表影响的方法

**Authors**: Daniel J. Aronoff, Robert M. Townsend, Madars Virza

**Abstract**: A repo trade involves the sale of a security coupled with a contract to repurchase at a later time. Following the 2008 financial crisis, accounting standards were updated to require repo intermediaries, who are mostly banks, to increase recorded assets at the time of the first transaction. ### 矩阵时间序列的模态加性因子模型
**[Modewise Additive Factor Model for Matrix Time Series](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2512.25025)**

**Authors**: Elynn Chen, Yuefeng Han, Jiayu Li, Ke Xu

**Abstract**: We introduce a Modewise Additive Factor Model (MAFM) for matrix-valued time series that captures row-specific and column-specific latent effects through an additive structure, offering greater flexibility than multiplicative frameworks such as Tucker and CP factor models. In MAFM, each observation decomposes into a row-factor component, a column-factor component, and noise, allowing distinct sources of variation along different modes to be modeled separately. We establish convergence rates for the estimated factor loading matrices under proper conditions. We further derive asymptotic distributions for the loading matrix estimators and develop consistent covariance estimators, yielding a data-driven inference framework that enables confidence interval construction and hypothesis testing. **摘要**: 我们引入了一种针对矩阵值时间序列的模态加性因子模型（MAFM），该模型通过加性结构捕获行特定和列特定的潜在效应，比Tucker和CP因子模型等乘性框架提供更大的灵活性。在MAFM中，每个观测值分解为行因子分量、列因子分量和噪声，允许沿着不同模式的不同变异源被单独建模。我们开发了一种计算效率高的两阶段估计过程：首先使用模态内积特征分解（MINE）进行初始化，然后使用补投影交替子空间估计（COMPAS）进行迭代细化。关键的方法论创新是，正交补投影在估计每个载荷空间时完全消除了跨模态干扰。我们在适当条件下为估计的因子载荷矩阵建立了收敛速率。我们进一步推导出载荷矩阵估计器的渐近分布，并开发了一致的协方差估计器，从而形成了一个数据驱动的推断框架，使得置信区间构建和假设检验成为可能。作为一个独立的技术贡献，我们为依赖矩阵时间序列的二次型建立了矩阵伯恩斯坦不等式。在合成数据和真实数据上的数值实验展示了所提出方法相对于现有方法的优势。
