### 粗糙的纳什谈判解
**[The Coarse Nash Bargaining Solutions](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.05019)**

**Authors**: Satoshu Nakada, Kensei Nakamura

**Abstract**: This paper studies the axiomatic bargaining problem and proposes a new class of bargaining solutions, called coarse Nash solutions. These solutions assign to each problem a set of outcomes coarser than that chosen by the classical Nash solution (Nash, 1950). Our main result shows that these solutions can be characterized by new rationality axioms for choice correspondences, which are modifications of Nash's independence of irrelevant alternatives (or more precisely, Arrow's (1959) choice axiom), when combined with standard axioms.

**摘要**: 本文研究了公理化谈判问题，并提出了一类新的谈判解决方案，称为粗纳什解决方案。这些解决方案为每个问题分配了一组比经典纳什解决方案（纳什，1950年）所选择的结果更粗的结果集。我们的主要结果表明，这些解决方案可以通过新的选择对应合理性公理来表征，这些公理是纳什的无关选择独立性（或者更准确地说，是阿罗（1959年）的选择公理）的修改，当它们与标准公理结合时。

### 考试成绩能帮助教师给学生提供更好的选课建议吗？一项主成分分层分析
**[Do Test Scores Help Teachers Give Better Track Advice to Students? A Principal Stratification Analysis](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.05128)**

**Authors**: Andrea Ichino, Fabrizia Mealli, Javier Viviens

**Abstract**: We study whether access to standardized test scores improves the quality of teachers' secondary school track recommendations, using Dutch data and a metric based on Principal Stratification in a quasi-randomized setting. Allowing teachers to revise their recommendations when test results exceed expectations increases the share of students successfully placed in more demanding tracks by at least 6%, but misplaces 7% of weaker students. However, only implausibly high weights on the short-term losses of students who must change track because of misplacement would justify prohibiting test-score-based upgrades. Access to test scores also induces fairer recommendations for immigrant and low-SES students.

**摘要**: 我们研究了获取标准化考试成绩是否提高了教师对中学生分流推荐的质量，使用了荷兰的数据，并在准随机设置中基于主分层的度量。允许教师在考试成绩超出预期时修改他们的推荐，至少将成功安置在更具挑战性轨道的学生比例提高了6%，但错误地安置了7%的较弱学生。然而，只有对学生因错位而必须更换轨道的短期损失赋予不可信的高权重，才会证明禁止基于考试成绩的升级是合理的。获取考试成绩还促使对移民和低社会经济地位学生的推荐更加公平。

### 最优获取信息的成本
**[The Cost of Optimally Acquired Information](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.05466)**

**Authors**: Alexander W. Bloedel, Weijie Zhong

**Abstract**: This paper introduces a framework for modeling the cost of information acquisition based on the principle of cost-minimization. We study the reduced-form \emph{indirect cost} of information generated by the sequential minimization of a primitive \emph{direct cost} function. Indirect cost functions: (i) are characterized by a novel recursive property, \emph{sequential learning-proofness}; (ii) provide an optimization foundation for the popular class of ``uniformly posterior separable'' costs; and (iii) can often be tractably calculated from their underlying direct costs. We apply the framework by identifying fundamental modeling tradeoffs in the rational inattention literature and two new indirect cost functions that balance these tradeoffs.

**摘要**: 本文介绍了一个基于成本最小化原则的信息获取成本建模框架。我们研究了由原始直接成本函数顺序最小化产生的信息的简化形式间接成本。间接成本函数：（i）具有一种新颖的递归属性，即“顺序学习证明性”；（ii）为流行的“统一后验可分”成本类别提供了优化基础；（iii）通常可以从其底层的直接成本中方便地计算出来。我们通过在理性忽视文献中识别基本的建模权衡，并提出两个新的间接成本函数来平衡这些权衡，来应用这个框架。

### 工人选择与效率
**[Worker Selection and Efficiency](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.05338)**

**Authors**: Yonghang Ji, Allen Vong

**Abstract**: We study a model where a manager repeatedly selects one worker from a group of homogeneous workers to perform a task. We characterize the largest set of parameters under which an equilibrium achieving efficient worker performance exists. We then show that this is the set of parameters given which the following manager's strategy constitutes an efficient equilibrium: the manager cyclically orders all workers and if the task is undesirable (resp., desirable), a worker is selected until good (resp., bad) performance, after which the manager randomizes between reselecting him and moving to the next worker; the reselection probability is set to be as high as effort incentives permit. Our findings extend to repeated selection of multiple workers.

**摘要**: 我们研究了一个模型，其中一位经理反复从一组同质工人中选择一名工人来执行一项任务。我们描述了在哪些参数集下，能够实现有效工人表现的均衡存在的最大部分。然后我们展示了，对于以下经理策略，这些参数集构成了一个有效的均衡：经理循环地安排所有工人，如果任务是不受欢迎的（或受欢迎的），则选择一名工人直到表现良好（或不佳），之后经理在重新选择他和转向下一名工人之间随机化；重新选择的概率被设置为努力激励所允许的最高水平。我们的发现扩展到了重复选择多名工人的情况。

### 大规模食品强化对营养充足饮食成本的影响：一项涵盖89个国家的建模研究
**[Impacts of large-scale food fortification on the cost of nutrient-adequate diets: a modeling study in 89 countries](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.05438)**

**Authors**: Leah Costlow, Yan Bai, Katherine P. Adams, Ty Beal, Kathryn G. Dewey, Christopher M. Free, Valerie M. Friesen, Mduduzi N. N. Mbuya, Stella Nordhagen, Florencia C. Vasta, William A. Masters

**Abstract**: Large-scale food fortification (LSFF) is a widely accepted intervention to alleviate micronutrient deficiencies, yet policy implementation is often incomplete and its effects on diet costs are not well established. We estimated the extent to which LSFF reduces the cost of nutrient-adequate diets using retail food prices and fortification policy data from 89 countries. In total, we modeled 5,874 least-cost diets across 22 sex-age groups and 3 nutrient-adequacy scenarios: meeting nutrient requirements only; adding minimum intakes for starchy staples and fruits and vegetables; and aligning food group shares with national consumption patterns. Assuming 90% implementation of existing LSFF standards, we found median cost reductions of 1.7%, 2.4%, and 4.5% across the three scenarios. Cost reductions varied widely by sex-age groups, national fortification strategies and food price structures. These findings highlight that LSFF may improve diet affordability when policies are carefully designed for local contexts, making it a valuable complement to other efforts that improve access to nutritious diets.

**摘要**: 大规模食品强化（LSFF）是一项被广泛接受的干预措施，旨在减轻微量营养素缺乏，然而政策实施往往不完整，其对饮食成本的影响尚未得到很好的确立。我们利用来自89个国家的零售食品价格和强化政策数据，估算了LSFF在多大程度上降低了营养充足饮食的成本。总共，我们为22个性别-年龄组和3种营养充足情景模拟了5,874种最低成本饮食：仅满足营养需求；增加淀粉主食和水果蔬菜的最低摄入量；以及使食品组份额与国家消费模式相一致。假设现有LSFF标准的实施率达到90%，我们发现三个情景中的中位数成本分别降低了1.7%、2.4%和4.5%。成本降低在性别-年龄组、国家强化策略和食品价格结构之间差异很大。这些发现强调，当政策为当地环境精心设计时，LSFF可能会提高饮食的可负担性，使其成为提高营养饮食获取努力的宝贵补充。

### 通过固定人口一致性对TU博弈中比例分配价值的特征描述
**[Characterizations of Proportional Division Value in TU-Games via Fixed-Population Consistency](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.05001)**

**Authors**: Yukihiko Funaki, Yukio Koriyama, Satoshi Nakada, Yuki Tamura

**Abstract**: We study the proportional division value in TU-games, which distributes the worth of the grand coalition in proportion to each player's stand-alone worth. Focusing on fixed-population consistency, we characterize the proportional division value through three types of axioms: a homogeneity axiom, composition axioms, and a nullified-game consistency axiom. The homogeneity axiom captures scale invariance with respect to the grand coalition's worth. The composition axioms ensure that payoffs remain consistent when the game is decomposed and recomposed. The nullified-game consistency axiom requires that when some players' payoffs are fixed, the solution for the remaining players, computed in the game adjusted to account for these fixed payoffs, coincides with their original payoffs. Together with efficiency and a fairness-related axiom, these axioms characterize the proportional division value.

**摘要**: 我们研究了TU博弈中的比例分配价值，它根据每个玩家单独的价值按比例分配大联盟的价值。关注固定人口的一致性，我们通过三种类型的公理来描述比例分配价值：一个齐次性公理、组合公理和无效游戏一致性公理。齐次性公理捕捉了大联盟价值的尺度不变性。组合公理确保当游戏被分解和重新组合时，收益保持一致。无效游戏一致性公理要求当一些玩家的收益被固定时，在调整游戏以考虑这些固定收益后，计算剩余玩家的解决方案与他们原来的收益相一致。结合效率和一个与公平相关的公理，这些公理描述了比例分配价值。

### 用固定人口不变性公理来刻画ELS值
**[Characterizing the ELS Values with Fixed-Population Invariance Axioms](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.04996)**

**Authors**: Yukihiko Funaki, Yukio Koriyama, Satoshi Nakada, Yuki Tamura

**Abstract**: We study efficient, linear, and symmetric (ELS) values, a central family of allocation rules for cooperative games with transferable-utility (TU-games) that includes the Shapley value, the CIS value, and the ENSC value. We first show that every ELS value can be written as the Shapley value of a suitably transformed TU-game. We then introduce three types of invariance axioms for fixed player populations. The first type consists of composition axioms, and the second type is active-player consistency. Each of these two types yields a characterization of a subclass of the ELS values that contains the family of least-square values. Finally, the third type is nullified-game consistency: we define three such axioms, and each axiom yields a characterization of one of the Shapley, CIS, and ENSC values.

**摘要**: 我们研究了合作博弈中可转移效用（TU-games）的核心分配规则——高效、线性、对称（ELS）值，这一家族包括了夏普利值、CIS值和ENSC值。首先，我们展示了每一个ELS值都可以被写成一个适当转换后的TU博弈的夏普利值。然后，我们为固定玩家群体引入了三类不变性公理。第一类是组合公理，第二类是活跃玩家一致性。这两类公理各自产生了一个包含最小二乘值家族的ELS值的子类的表征。最后，第三类是无效博弈一致性：我们定义了三个这样的公理，每个公理都产生了夏普利值、CIS值和ENSC值中的一个的表征。

### 选举与主观幸福感：来自2024年美国总统选举的证据
**[Election and Subjective Well-Being:Evidence from the 2024 U.S. Presidential Election](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.04912)**

**Authors**: Dongyoung Kim, Young-Il Albert Kim, Haedong Aiden Rho

**Abstract**: This paper uses daily Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System data to estimate the causal effect of the 2024 U.S. presidential election, a highly competitive race whose outcome resolved lingering uncertainty on election day, on mental-health and life-satisfaction outcomes through a regression discontinuity design. Following the resolution of electoral uncertainty on election day, we find a sharp and persistent post-election decline in subjective well-being, concentrated among female, non-White, urban, and more-educated respondents. These findings reveal an expected-outcome shock, showing that political polarization itself, not electoral surprise, can act as a chronic psychological stressor.

**摘要**: 本文利用每日行为风险因素监测系统的数据，通过回归不连续性设计来估计2024年美国总统大选的因果效应。这场竞争激烈的选举结果在选举日解决了悬而未决的不确定性，对心理健康和生活满意度结果产生了影响。在选举日选举不确定性解决后，我们发现主观幸福感出现了急剧而持久的下降，主要集中在女性、非白人、城市和受过更多教育的受访者中。这些发现揭示了一个预期结果冲击，表明政治极化本身，而非选举意外，可以作为一种慢性心理压力。

### 言辞重要：利用企业文本数据预测经济下行风险
**[Words Matter: Forecasting Economic Downside Risks with Corporate Textual Data](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.04935)**

**Authors**: Cansu Isler

**Abstract**: Accurate forecasting of downside risks to economic growth is critically important for policymakers and financial institutions, particularly in the wake of recent economic crises. This paper extends the Growth-at-Risk (GaR) approach by introducing a novel daily sentiment indicator derived from textual analysis of mandatory corporate disclosures (SEC 10-K and 10-Q reports) to forecast downside risks to economic growth. Using the Loughran--McDonald dictionary and a word-count methodology, I compute firm-level tone growth as the year-over-year difference between positive and negative sentiment expressed in corporate filings. These firm-specific sentiment metrics are aggregated into a weekly tone index, weighted by firms' market capitalizations to capture broader, economy-wide sentiment dynamics. Integrated into a mixed-data sampling (MIDAS) quantile regression framework, this sentiment-based indicator enhances the prediction of GDP growth downturns, outperforming traditional financial market indicators such as the National Financial Conditions Index (NFCI). The findings underscore corporate textual data as a powerful and timely resource for macroeconomic risk assessment and informed policymaking.

**摘要**: 对经济增长下行风险的准确预测对于政策制定者和金融机构至关重要，特别是在最近的经济危机之后。本文通过引入一个新颖的每日情绪指标来扩展风险增长（GaR）方法，该指标是从强制性公司披露（SEC 10-K和10-Q报告）的文本分析中派生出来的，以预测经济增长的下行风险。使用Loughran-McDonald词典和词频方法，我计算了公司层面的语气增长，即公司文件中表达的积极和消极情绪之间的年对年差异。这些公司特定的情绪指标被聚合成一个每周的语气指数，按公司的市值加权，以捕捉更广泛的、经济范围内的情绪动态。将这个基于情绪的指标整合到混合数据抽样（MIDAS）分位数回归框架中，增强了对GDP增长下滑的预测，超越了传统的金融市场指标，如国家金融状况指数（NFCI）。这些发现强调了公司文本数据作为宏观经济风险评估和知情政策制定的强大且及时的资源。

### 澄清有效下限的困境
**[Clearing Up the Effective Lower Bound Morass](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.04782)**

**Authors**: Haochun Ma, Jordan Roulleau-Pasdeloup

**Abstract**: Depending on the persistence of the underlying Markov chain shock, the standard New Keynesian model predicts starkly different conclusions at the Effective Lower Bound. We clear up this morass by using a truncated Markov chain. We prove that the expectations-driven trap à la Mertens & Ravn (2014) doesn't arise as an equilibrium outcome. In addition, the equilibrium under a truncated Markov chain is guaranteed to be unique, the effect of government spending is positive on consumption and does not switch signs but may grow unbounded \textemdash a puzzle.

**摘要**: 根据底层马尔可夫链冲击的持续性，标准新凯恩斯模型在有效下限处预测出截然不同的结论。我们通过使用截断的马尔可夫链来澄清这一混乱局面。我们证明了，Mertens & Ravn（2014）提出的预期驱动型陷阱不会作为均衡结果出现。此外，在截断马尔可夫链下的均衡是唯一确定的，政府支出对消费的效应是正向的，不会改变符号，但可能会无限增长——这是一个谜题。

### 使用多重分割进行训练和测试：分割样本估计器的中心极限定理
**[Training and Testing with Multiple Splits: A Central Limit Theorem for Split-Sample Estimators](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.04957)**

**Authors**: Bruno Fava

**Abstract**: As predictive algorithms grow in popularity, using the same dataset to both train and test a new model has become routine across research, policy, and industry. Sample-splitting attains valid inference on model properties by using separate subsamples to estimate the model and to evaluate it. However, this approach has two drawbacks, since each task uses only part of the data, and different splits can lead to widely different estimates. Averaging across multiple splits, I develop an inference approach that uses more data for training, uses the entire sample for testing, and improves reproducibility. I address the statistical dependence from reusing observations across splits by proving a new central limit theorem for a large class of split-sample estimators under arguably mild and general conditions. Importantly, I make no restrictions on model complexity or convergence rates. I show that confidence intervals based on the normal approximation are valid for many applications, but may undercover in important cases of interest, such as comparing the performance between two models. I develop a new inference approach for such cases, explicitly accounting for the dependence across splits. Moreover, I provide a measure of reproducibility for p-values obtained from split-sample estimators. Finally, I apply my results to two important problems in development and public economics: predicting poverty and learning heterogeneous treatment effects in randomized experiments. I show that my inference approach with repeated cross-fitting achieves better power than previous alternatives, often enough to find statistical significance that would otherwise be missed.

**摘要**: 随着预测算法的流行，使用相同的数据集来训练和测试新模型已成为研究、政策和行业的常规做法。样本分割通过使用不同的子样本来估计模型和评估模型，从而获得模型属性的有效推断。然而，这种方法有两个缺点，因为每项任务只使用部分数据，不同的分割可能导致截然不同的估计结果。通过对多个分割进行平均，我开发了一种推断方法，该方法在训练时使用更多数据，在测试时使用整个样本，并提高了可复制性。我通过证明一个新的中心极限定理，解决了在分割样本估计器中重复使用观测值的统计依赖性问题，该定理适用于一个广泛的分割样本估计器类别，且在可辩解的温和和一般条件下成立。重要的是，我对模型复杂性或收敛速度没有任何限制。我展示了基于正态近似的置信区间对许多应用是有效的，但在比较两个模型性能等重要案例中可能会低估。我为这些情况开发了一种新的推断方法，明确考虑了分割间的依赖性。此外，我为分割样本估计器得到的p值提供了一个可复制性的度量。最后，我将我的研究结果应用于发展和公共经济学中的两个重要问题：预测贫困和在随机实验中学习异质性处理效应。我展示了我的重复交叉拟合的推断方法比以往的替代方法具有更好的统计功效，通常足以发现否则会被遗漏的统计显著性。

### 2010年以来美国的技术获取、收入差距和求职情况
**[Local Technological Access, Income Disparities, and Job-Seeking in the United States Since 2010](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2511.05294)**

**Authors**: Shaolong Wu

**Abstract**: In the modern U.S. labor market, digital infrastructures strongly influence how individuals locate opportunities, build skills, and advance wages. Regional differences in computing access, broadband coverage, and digital literacy have significant labor implications for equity and sustainability. Drawing on longitudinal data from the NLSY97 (National Longitudinal Surveys of Youth) cohort, this study examines how place-based technological factors, personal demographics, household characteristics, and education shape income levels and decisions to seek new employment. The regression analyses reveal that educational attainment, marital status, and frequency of Internet usage strongly predict both wages and individuals' job-seeking intensity. Regional disparities in income underscore the need for more localized interventions to ensure equitable access to technology. This study raises key questions about how digital infrastructures can reinforce or challenge systemic inequalities in underserved communities.
