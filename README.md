### 定向广告平台：数据共享与客户挖角
**[Targeted Advertising Platforms: Data Sharing and Customer Poaching](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2510.27112)**

**Authors**: Klajdi Hoxha

**Abstract**: E-commerce platforms are rolling out ambitious targeted advertising initiatives that rely on merchants sharing customer data with each other via the platform. Yet current platform designs fail to address participating merchants' concerns about customer poaching. This paper proposes a model of designing targeted advertising platforms that incentivizes merchants to voluntarily share customer data despite poaching concerns. I characterize the optimal mechanism that maximizes a weighted sum of platform's revenues, customer engagement and merchants' surplus. In sufficiently large platforms, the optimal mechanism can be implemented through the design of three markets: $i)$ selling market, where merchants can sell all their data at a posted price $p$, $ii)$ exchange market, where merchants share all their data in exchange for high click-through rate (CTR) ads, and $iii)$ buying market, where high-value merchants buy high CTR ads at the full price. The model is broad in scope with applications in other market design settings like the greenhouse gas credit markets and reallocating public resources, and points toward new directions in combinatorial market exchange designs.

### 翻译失败
**[Delegate Pricing Decisions to an Algorithm? Experimental Evidence](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2510.27636)**

**Authors**: Hans-Theo Normann, Nina Rulié, Olaf Stypa, Tobias Werner

**Abstract**: We analyze the delegation of pricing by participants, representing firms, to a collusive, self-learning algorithm in a repeated Bertrand experiment. In the baseline treatment, participants set prices themselves. In the other treatments, participants can either delegate pricing to the algorithm at the beginning of each supergame or receive algorithmic recommendations that they can override. Participants delegate more when they can override the algorithm's decisions. In both algorithmic treatments, prices are lower than in the baseline. Our results indicate that while self-learning pricing algorithms can be collusive, they can foster competition rather than collusion with humans-in-the-loop.

**摘要**: 我们分析了参与者（代表公司）在重复的贝特朗实验中，将定价权委托给一个共谋的、自我学习的算法。在基线处理中，参与者自己设定价格。在其他处理中，参与者可以在每个超级游戏开始时将定价委托给算法，或者接收算法的建议，但他们可以覆盖这些建议。当参与者可以覆盖算法的决定时，他们更倾向于委托。在两种算法处理中，价格都低于基线处理。我们的结果表明，虽然自我学习的定价算法可能是共谋的，但它们可以在人为干预的情况下促进竞争而不是共谋。

### 政治权力与死亡率：美国投票权法案的异质性效应
**[Political Power and Mortality: Heterogeneous Effects of the U.S. Voting Rights Act](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2510.26857)**

**Authors**: Atheendar Venkataramani, Rourke O'Brien, Elizabeth Bair, Christopher Lowenstein

**Abstract**: We study the health consequences of redistributing political power through the 1975 extension of the Voting Rights Act, which eliminated barriers to voting for previously disenfranchised nonwhite populations. The intervention led to broad declines in under-five mortality but sharply contrasting effects in other age groups: mortality fell among non-white children, younger adults, and older women, yet rose among whites and older non-white men. These differences cannot be reconciled by changes in population composition or material conditions. Instead, we present evidence suggesting psychosocial stress and retaliatory responses arising from perceived status threat as key mechanisms.

**摘要**: 我们研究了通过1975年《投票权法》的扩展重新分配政治权力对健康后果的影响，该法案消除了以前被剥夺选举权的非白人群体的投票障碍。这一干预措施导致五岁以下儿童死亡率普遍下降，但在其他年龄组中产生了截然不同的效果：非白人儿童、年轻成年人和老年女性的死亡率下降，然而白人和老年非白人男性的死亡率却上升。这些差异不能通过人口构成或物质条件的变化来解释。相反，我们提供的证据表明，由于感知到的地位威胁而产生的心理社会压力和报复性反应是关键机制。

### 雇佣内在动机的代理人：委托人的困境
**[Hiring Intrinsically Motivated Agents: A Principal's Dilemma](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2510.27625)**

**Authors**: Andrew Leal

**Abstract**: Employers are concerned not only with a prospective worker's ability, but also their propensity to avoid shirking. This paper proposes a new experimental framework to study how Principals trade-off measures of ability and prosocial behavior when ranking Agents for independent jobs. Subjects participate in a simulated, incentivized job market. In an initial session, subjects are Workers and generate a database of signals and job results. Managers in subsequent sessions observe the signals of Worker behavior and ability and job details before a rank-and-value task, ranking and reporting a value for each Worker for two distinct jobs. Results highlight Managers' preference for ability over prosocial behavior on average, especially for Managers in STEM fields. There is evidence of homophily: the relative value of prosocial behavior is higher for highly prosocial Managers, compensating for ability or even surpassing it in value.

**摘要**: 雇主不仅关心潜在工人的能力，还关心他们避免偷懒的倾向。本文提出了一个新的实验框架，研究委托人在为独立工作排名代理人时如何在能力和亲社会行为的衡量之间进行权衡。参与者在一个模拟的、有激励的就业市场中参与。在初始会议中，参与者作为工人，生成信号和工作结果的数据库。在随后的会议中，管理者观察工人行为和能力的信号以及工作细节，然后进行排名和价值评估任务，为两个不同工作对每个工人进行排名并报告价值。结果突出显示，管理者平均而言更倾向于能力而非亲社会行为，特别是对于STEM领域的管理者。有证据表明同质性偏好：对于高度亲社会的管理者来说，亲社会行为的相对价值更高，补偿能力甚至在价值上超越它。

### 利用卫星和街景图像预测印度两个城市的家庭用水量
**[Predicting Household Water Consumption Using Satellite and Street View Images in Two Indian Cities](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2510.26957)**

**Authors**: Qiao Wang, Joseph George

**Abstract**: Monitoring household water use in rapidly urbanizing regions is hampered by costly, time-intensive enumeration methods and surveys. We investigate whether publicly available imagery-satellite tiles, Google Street View (GSV) segmentation-and simple geospatial covariates (nightlight intensity, population density) can be utilized to predict household water consumption in Hubballi-Dharwad, India. We compare four approaches: survey features (benchmark), CNN embeddings (satellite, GSV, combined), and GSV semantic maps with auxiliary data. Under an ordinal classification framework, GSV segmentation plus remote-sensing covariates achieves 0.55 accuracy for water use, approaching survey-based models (0.59 accuracy). Error analysis shows high precision at extremes of the household water consumption distribution, but confusion among middle classes is due to overlapping visual proxies. We also compare and contrast our estimates for household water consumption to that of household subjective income. Our findings demonstrate that open-access imagery, coupled with minimal geospatial data, offers a promising alternative to obtaining reliable household water consumption estimates using surveys in urban analytics.

**摘要**: 在快速城市化的地区监测家庭用水情况受到成本高昂、耗时的枚举方法和调查的阻碍。我们研究了是否可以利用公开可用的图像——卫星瓦片、谷歌街景（GSV）分割——以及简单的地理空间协变量（夜间灯光强度、人口密度）来预测印度Hubballi-Dharwad的家庭用水量。我们比较了四种方法：调查特征（基准）、CNN嵌入（卫星、GSV、结合）以及GSV语义地图和辅助数据。在序数分类框架下，GSV分割加上遥感协变量在用水方面的准确率达到0.55，接近基于调查的模型（0.59准确率）。错误分析显示，在家庭用水量分布的极端值上具有高精确度，但由于视觉代理的重叠，导致中间阶层的混淆。我们还将家庭用水量的估计与家庭主观收入进行了比较和对比。我们的发现表明，开放获取的图像，结合最少的地理空间数据，为城市分析中使用调查获取可靠的家庭用水量估计提供了一个有希望的替代方案。

### 算法性掠夺：动态寡头市场中平滑市场份额的均衡分析
**[Algorithmic Predation: Equilibrium Analysis in Dynamic Oligopolies with Smooth Market Sharing](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2510.27008)**

**Authors**: Fabian Raoul Pieroth, Ole Petersen, Martin Bichler

**Abstract**: Predatory pricing -- where a firm strategically lowers prices to undermine competitors -- is a contentious topic in dynamic oligopoly theory, with scholars debating practical relevance and the existence of predatory equilibria. Although finite-horizon dynamic models have long been proposed to capture the strategic intertemporal incentives of oligopolists, the existence and form of equilibrium strategies in settings that allow for firm exit (drop-outs following loss-making periods) have remained an open question. We focus on the seminal dynamic oligopoly model by Selten (1965) that introduces the subgame perfect equilibrium and analyzes smooth market sharing. Equilibrium can be derived analytically in models that do not allow for dropouts, but not in models that can lead to predatory pricing. In this paper, we leverage recent advances in deep reinforcement learning to compute and verify equilibria in finite-horizon dynamic oligopoly games. Our experiments reveal two key findings: first, state-of-the-art deep reinforcement learning algorithms reliably converge to equilibrium in both perfect- and imperfect-information oligopoly models; second, when firms face asymmetric cost structures, the resulting equilibria exhibit predatory pricing behavior. These results demonstrate that predatory pricing can emerge as a rational equilibrium strategy across a broad variety of model settings. By providing equilibrium analysis of finite-horizon dynamic oligopoly models with drop-outs, our study answers a decade-old question and offers new insights for competition authorities and regulators.

**摘要**: 掠夺性定价——企业为了削弱竞争对手而策略性地降低价格——是动态寡头理论中一个有争议的话题，学者们就其实际相关性和掠夺性均衡的存在进行了辩论。尽管有限视野的动态模型早已被提出以捕捉寡头的战略性时间激励，但在允许企业退出（亏损期后的退出）的设置中，均衡策略的存在和形式一直是一个未解之谜。我们关注Selten（1965）提出的开创性动态寡头模型，该模型引入了子博弈完美均衡，并分析了平滑的市场共享。在不允许退出的模型中可以分析得出均衡，但在可能导致掠夺性定价的模型中则不行。在本文中，我们利用深度强化学习的最新进展来计算和验证有限视野动态寡头博弈中的均衡。我们的实验揭示了两个关键发现：首先，最先进的深度强化学习算法在完美和不完全信息寡头模型中都能可靠地收敛到均衡；其次，当企业面临不对称的成本结构时，产生的均衡表现出掠夺性定价行为。这些结果表明，掠夺性定价可以作为理性均衡策略在广泛的模型设置中出现。通过提供有限视野动态寡头模型中退出情况的均衡分析，我们的研究回答了一个十年未解的问题，并为竞争当局和监管机构提供了新的见解。

### 紧急医疗服务中的新兴动态空间边界：从基本原理出发的纳维-斯托克斯框架
**[Emergent Dynamical Spatial Boundaries in Emergency Medical Services: A Navier-Stokes Framework from First Principles](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2510.26810)**

**Authors**: Tatsuru Kikuchi

**Abstract**: Emergency medical services (EMS) response times are critical determinants of patient survival, yet existing approaches to spatial coverage analysis rely on discrete distance buffers or ad-hoc geographic information system (GIS) isochrones without theoretical foundation. This paper derives continuous spatial boundaries for emergency response from first principles using fluid dynamics (Navier-Stokes equations), demonstrating that response effectiveness decays exponentially with time: $\tau(t) = \tau_0 \exp(-\kappa t)$, where $\tau_0$ is baseline effectiveness and $\kappa$ is the temporal decay rate. Using 10,000 simulated emergency incidents from the National Emergency Medical Services Information System (NEMSIS), I estimate decay parameters and calculate critical boundaries $d^*$ where response effectiveness falls below policy-relevant thresholds. The framework reveals substantial demographic heterogeneity: elderly populations (85+) experience 8.40-minute average response times versus 7.83 minutes for younger adults (18-44), with 33.6\% of poor-access incidents affecting elderly populations despite representing 5.2\% of the sample. Non-parametric kernel regression validation confirms exponential decay is appropriate (mean squared error 8-12 times smaller than parametric), while traditional difference-in-differences analysis validates treatment effect existence (DiD coefficient = -1.35 minutes, $p < 0.001$). The analysis identifies vulnerable populations--elderly, rural, and low-income communities--facing systematically longer response times, informing optimal EMS station placement and resource allocation to reduce health disparities.

**摘要**: 紧急医疗服务（EMS）的响应时间是决定患者生存的关键因素，然而现有的空间覆盖分析方法依赖于离散的距离缓冲区或没有理论基础的地理信息系统（GIS）等时线。本文利用流体动力学（纳维-斯托克斯方程）从基本原理推导出紧急响应的连续空间边界，证明响应效率随时间指数衰减：$\tau(t) = \tau_0 \exp(-\kappa t)$，其中$\tau_0$是基线效率，$\kappa$是时间衰减率。通过使用国家紧急医疗服务信息系统（NEMSIS）模拟的10,000起紧急事件，我估计了衰减参数，并计算了响应效率低于政策相关阈值的关键边界$d^*$。该框架揭示了显著的人口统计学异质性：85岁及以上的老年人口平均响应时间为8.40分钟，而年轻成年人（18-44岁）为7.83分钟，尽管老年人口仅占样本的5.2%，但33.6%的低可达性事件影响了老年人口。非参数核回归验证确认指数衰减是适当的（均方误差比参数方法小8-12倍），而传统的差异-差异分析验证了处理效应的存在（DiD系数=-1.35分钟，$p < 0.001$）。分析识别了面临系统性更长响应时间的脆弱人群——老年人、农村和低收入社区，为优化EMS站点布局和资源分配以减少健康差异提供了信息。
