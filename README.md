### 列昂惕夫生产技术在卫生系统建模中的属性：马拉维的Thanzi la Onse模型
**[The properties of a Leontief production technology for Health System Modeling: the Thanzi la Onse model for Malawi](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2508.21699)**

**Authors**: Martin Chalkley, Sakshi Mohan, Margherita Molaro, Bingling She, Wiktoria Tafesse

**Abstract**: As health system modeling (HSM) advances to include more complete descriptions of the production of healthcare, it is important to establish a robust conceptual characterisation of the production process. For the Thanzi La Onse model in Malawi we have incorporated an approach to production that is based on a form of Leontief technology -- fixed input proportions. At first sight, this form of technology appears restrictive relative to the general conception of a production function employed in economics. In particular, the Leontief technology is associated with constant returns to scale, and level sets that are piecewise linear, both of which are highly restrictive properties. In this article we demonstrate that once incorporated into an all disease, agent-based model these properties are no longer present and the Leontief framework becomes a rich structure for describing healthcare production, and hence for examining the returns to health systems investments.

**摘要**: 随着卫生系统建模（HSM）的发展，以包含更完整的对医疗保健生产的描述，建立一个健全的概念性特征描述生产过程变得非常重要。对于马拉维的Thanz La Onse模型，我们采用了一种基于列昂惕夫技术的生产方法——固定投入比例。乍一看，这种技术形式相对于经济学中使用的一般生产函数概念似乎有限制性。特别是，列昂惕夫技术与规模报酬不变和分段线性的水平集相关联，这两个都是非常限制性的特性。在这篇文章中，我们展示了一旦将这些特性纳入到一个涵盖所有疾病的基于代理的模型中，这些特性就不再存在，列昂惕夫框架成为了描述医疗保健生产的丰富结构，因此也用于检查卫生系统投资的回报。

### 评估中国民族收入差距：以辽宁和内蒙古的汉族、蒙古族和满族为例
**[Evaluating Ethnic Income Gap in China: The Case of Han, Mongol, and Manchu in Liaoning and Inner Mongolia](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2508.21625)**

**Authors**: Xinyan Deng

**Abstract**: This study analyzes the 2018 Chinese Household Income Project survey data to evaluate the income gaps between an "outsider" ethnic minority group, the Mongols, an "insider" ethnic minority group, the Manchus, and the majority Han group in urban and rural areas of Liaoning province and Inner Mongolia in China. Three statistical methods, a simple first-order OLS linear regression, linear regressions with interaction terms, and the Blinder-Oaxaca Decomposition, are used to investigate the income disparity amongst the three groups. The results indicate that Mongols suffer a significant ethnic wage penalty attributable to possible discrimination in the rural areas of these two provinces, while the urban income gaps between the three groups can mostly be explained by participation in public sector occupations or affiliation with the Chinese Communist Party. In rural settings, Mongols also have higher returns to public sector jobs and CCP membership compared to the other two ethnic groups. The findings suggest that Chinese affirmative actions regarding ethnic policy are effective in accelerating the integration of ethnic minorities with Han in the outcomes of the labor market. This conclusion is consistent with previous studies.

**摘要**: 本研究分析了2018年中国家庭收入项目调查数据，以评估在中国辽宁省和内蒙古自治区的城市和农村地区，“外来”少数民族群体蒙古人、“内部”少数民族群体满族和多数汉族群体之间的收入差距。研究使用了三种统计方法：简单的一阶OLS线性回归、带有交互项的线性回归和Blinder-Oaxaca分解方法，来调查这三个群体之间的收入差异。结果表明，蒙古人在这两个省份的农村地区遭受显著的民族工资惩罚，这归因于可能的歧视，而三个群体之间的城市收入差距大多可以通过参与公共部门职业或与中国共产觉的联系来解释。在农村环境中，与另外两个民族群体相比，蒙古人对公共部门工作和中共党籍的回报也更高。研究结果表明，中国关于民族政策的积极行动在加速少数民族与汉族在劳动市场结果中的融合方面是有效的。这一结论与先前的研究一致。

### 边际处理效应：每个人都是边际的
**[Treatment effects at the margin: Everyone is marginal](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2508.21583)**

**Authors**: Haotian Deng

**Abstract**: This paper develops a framework for identifying treatment effects when a policy simultaneously alters both the incentive to participate and the outcome of interest -- such as hiring decisions and wages in response to employment subsidies; or working decisions and wages in response to job trainings. This framework was inspired by my PhD project on a Belgian reform that subsidised first-time hiring, inducing entry by marginal firms yet meanwhile changing the wages they pay. Standard methods addressing selection-into-treatment concepts (like Heckman selection equations and local average treatment effects), or before-after comparisons (including simple DiD or RDD), cannot isolate effects at this shifting margin where treatment defines who is observed. I introduce marginality-weighted estimands that recover causal effects among policy-induced entrants, offering a policy-relevant alternative in settings with endogenous selection. This method can thus be applied widely to understanding the economic impacts of public programmes, especially in fields largely relying on reduced-form causal inference estimation (e.g. labour economics, development economics, health economics).

**摘要**: 本文构建了一个框架，用于在政策同时改变参与激励和感兴趣的结果时识别处理效应——例如，就业补贴对招聘决策和工资的影响；或职业培训对工作决策和工资的影响。这一框架的灵感来自于我的博士项目，该项目研究了比利时的一项改革，该改革为首次招聘提供补贴，促使边缘公司进入市场，同时改变了它们支付的工资。处理选择性进入处理概念的标准方法（如Heckman选择方程和局部平均处理效应）或前后比较方法（包括简单的双重差分法或工具变量法）无法在这种变化的边际上隔离效应，因为处理定义了谁是被观察到的。我引入了边缘性加权估计量，以恢复政策诱导的进入者之间的因果效应，为具有内生选择的设置提供了一个政策相关的替代方案。这种方法因此可以广泛应用于理解公共项目的经济影响，特别是在大量依赖于简化形式因果推断估计的领域（例如劳动经济学、发展经济学、健康经济学）。

### 德国失业救济金II的未领取：使用行政数据的纵向视角
**[Non-Take-Up of Unemployment Benefit II in Germany: A Longitudinal Perspective Using Administrative Data](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2508.21535)**

**Authors**: Jürgen Wiemers

**Abstract**: Extensive research demonstrates that many households eligible for means-tested benefits do not claim them, a phenomenon known as non-take-up. Empirical studies frequently conceptualise non-take-up as a rational decision, occurring when the perceived net utility of claiming is negative. Theoretically, long-term factors can substantially impact this decision. Despite the potential relevance of longitudinal aspects, evidence on their influence remains limited.
This study addresses this gap by incorporating long-term factors in the analysis of non-take-up behaviour relating to Unemployment Benefit II (UB II), Germany's basic means-tested welfare programme. Using data from the German Panel Study Labour Market and Social Security (PASS) from 2008 to 2020, linked with administrative data from Germany's Federal Employment Agency (PASS-ADIAB), this study reconstructs households' benefit receipt and income histories, even during non-survey periods. This allows modelling benefit non-take-up for eligible households using the duration and frequency of past benefit receipt. In addition, the use of administrative data mitigates bias from self-reported benefit receipt. Household eligibility for UB II is simulated using GETTSIM, an open-source microsimulation model, applied to the PASS dataset for the first time.
Findings indicate that long-term factors significantly influence the probability of claiming UB II. Specifically, a longer history of benefit receipt increases this probability, whereas higher income potential and positive income shocks reduce it. Including long-term factors substantially affects the estimated impact of traditionally used determinants of non-take-up, indicating a potential misspecification in existing models that neglect them.

**摘要**: 大量研究表明，许多有资格获得经济状况调查福利的家庭并未申请这些福利，这种现象被称为不领取。实证研究通常将不领取视为一种理性决策，当申请的感知净效用为负时就会发生。从理论上讲，长期因素可以显著影响这一决策。尽管纵向因素可能具有相关性，但关于它们影响的证据仍然有限。
本研究通过将长期因素纳入对德国基本经济状况调查福利计划——失业救济金II（UB II）的不领取行为的分析中，填补了这一空白。利用2008年至2020年德国劳动市场和社会安全面板研究（PASS）的数据，并与德国联邦就业局的行政数据（PASS-ADIAB）链接，本研究重建了家庭的福利领取和收入历史，甚至包括非调查期间。这允许使用过去福利领取的持续时间和频率对符合条件的家庭进行福利不领取建模。此外，使用行政数据减少了自我报告福利领取的偏差。通过GETTSIM，一个开源的微观模拟模型，首次应用于PASS数据集，模拟了家庭对UB II的资格。
研究结果表明，长期因素显著影响申请UB II的概率。具体来说，更长的福利领取历史增加了这一概率，而更高的收入潜力和正向收入冲击则降低了这一概率。包括长期因素显著影响了传统使用的不领取决定因素的估计影响，表明现有模型可能存在规范性错误，因为它们忽视了这些因素。

### 跨越时间和（产品）空间：以能力为中心的相关性和经济复杂性模型
**[Across Time and (Product) Space: A Capability-Centric Model of Relatedness and Economic Complexity](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2508.21616)**

**Authors**: Ziang Huang, Huashan Chen

**Abstract**: Economic complexity - a group of dimensionality-reduction methods that apply network science to trade data - represented a paradigm shift in development economics towards materializing the once-intangible concept of capabilities as inferrable and quantifiable. Measures such as the Economic Complexity Index (ECI) and the Product Space have proven their worth as robust estimators of an economy's subsequent growth; less obvious, however, is how they have come to be so. Despite ECI drawing its micro-foundations from a combinatorial model of capabilities, where a set of homogeneous capabilities combine to form products and the economies which can produce them, such a model is consistent with neither the fact that distinct product classes draw on distinct capabilities, nor the interrelations between different products in the Product Space which so much of economic complexity is based upon.
In this paper, we extend the combinatorial model of economic complexity through two innovations: an underlying network which governs the relatedness between capabilities, and a production function which trades the original binary specialization function for a fine-grained, product-level output function. Using country-product trade data across 216 countries, 5000 products and two decades, we show that this model is able to accurately replicate both the characteristic topology of the Product Space and the complexity distribution of countries' export baskets. In particular, the model bridges the gap between the ECI and capabilities by transforming measures of economic complexity into direct measures of the capabilities held by an economy - a transformation shown to both improve the informativeness of the Economic Complexity Index in predicting economic growth and enable an interpretation of economic complexity as a proxy for productive structure in the form of capability substitutability.

**摘要**: 经济复杂性——一组将网络科学应用于贸易数据的降维方法——代表了发展经济学向将一度无形的能力概念具体化和量化的范式转变。诸如经济复杂性指数（ECI）和产品空间等指标已被证明是衡量一个经济体随后增长的强有力的估计器；然而，它们为何如此有效则不那么明显。尽管ECI源自于一个能力组合模型的微观基础，其中一组同质的能力结合形成产品和能够生产它们的经济体，但这样的模型既不符合不同产品类别依赖不同能力的事实，也不符合产品空间中不同产品之间的相互关系，而经济复杂性的大部分基础正是基于这种相互关系。

在本文中，我们通过两项创新扩展了经济复杂性的组合模型：一个管理能力之间相关性的底层网络，以及一个生产函数，它用一个细粒度的产品级产出函数取代了原始的二元专业化函数。利用216个国家、5000种产品和二十年的国别-产品贸易数据，我们展示了这个模型能够准确复制产品空间的特征拓扑结构和国家出口篮子的复杂性分布。特别是，该模型通过将经济复杂性的度量转化为直接衡量一个经济体所持有能力的度量，弥合了ECI和能力之间的差距——这种转化被证明既能提高经济复杂性指数在预测经济增长方面的信息量，也能将经济复杂性解释为以能力可替代性形式呈现的生产结构的代理。

### 对LLM的金融大脑扫描
**[A Financial Brain Scan of the LLM](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2508.21285)**

**Authors**: Hui Chen, Antoine Didisheim, Luciano Somoza, Hanqing Tian

**Abstract**: Emerging techniques in computer science make it possible to "brain scan" large language models (LLMs), identify the plain-English concepts that guide their reasoning, and steer them while holding other factors constant. We show that this approach can map LLM-generated economic forecasts to concepts such as sentiment, technical analysis, and timing, and compute their relative importance without reducing performance. We also show that models can be steered to be more or less risk-averse, optimistic, or pessimistic, which allows researchers to correct or simulate biases. The method is transparent, lightweight, and replicable for empirical research in the social sciences.

**摘要**: 计算机科学中的新兴技术使得对大型语言模型（LLMs）进行“大脑扫描”成为可能，识别指导它们推理的普通英语概念，并在保持其他因素不变的情况下引导它们。我们展示了这种方法可以将LLM生成的经济预测映射到情绪、技术分析和时机等概念，并计算它们的重要性，而不会降低性能。我们还展示了可以引导模型变得更或更少的风险规避、乐观或悲观，这允许研究人员纠正或模拟偏见。这种方法对于社会科学的经验研究来说是透明、轻量级和可复制的。

### 社会学习中的逆向动机：带有非从众偏好的信息瀑布
**[Contrarian Motives in Social Learning: Information Cascades with Nonconformist Preferences](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2508.21446)**

**Authors**: Georgy Lukyanov, Vasilii Ivanik

**Abstract**: We embed a taste for nonconformism into a canonical Bikhchandani-Hirshleifer-Welch social-learning model. Agents value both correctness and choosing the minority action (fixed or proportion-based bonus). We study exogenous signals and endogenous acquisition with a fixed entry cost and convex cost of precision in a Gaussian-quadratic specification. Contrarian motives shift equilibrium cutoffs away from 1/2 and expand the belief region where information is purchased, sustaining informative actions; conditional on investing, chosen precision is lower near central beliefs. Welfare is shaped by a trade-off: mild contrarianism counteracts premature herding, whereas strong contrarianism steers actions against informative social signals and induces low-value experimentation. A tractable characterization delivers closed-form cutoffs, comparative statics, and transparent welfare comparisons. Applications include scientific priority races and academic diffusion, where distinctiveness yields rents yet excessive contrarianism erodes information aggregation.

**摘要**: 我们将对非从众行为的偏好嵌入到一个经典的Bikhchandani-Hirshleifer-Welch社会学习模型中。在这个模型中，代理人既重视正确性，也重视选择少数派行动（固定或基于比例的奖励）。我们研究了外生信号和内生获取信号，其中包含了固定的进入成本和凸形的精度成本，在高斯-二次规格中进行研究。逆向动机将均衡截断点从1/2移开，并扩大了购买信息的信念区域，从而维持了有信息的行动；在投资的条件下，选择的精度在中心信念附近较低。福利由一个权衡塑造：适度的逆向行为可以抵消过早的羊群效应，而强烈的逆向行为则将行动引导至与信息性社会信号相反的方向，并诱导低价值的实验。一个可行的特征提供了封闭形式的截断点、比较静态和透明的福利比较。应用包括科学优先竞赛和学术传播，其中独特性可以带来租金，但过度的逆向行为会侵蚀信息聚合。

### 原材料进口禁令对垂直对外直接投资的影响：理论洞见与准实验证据
**[The Impact of a Raw Material Import Ban on Vertical Outward FDI: Theoretical Insights and Quasi-Experimental Evidence](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2508.21291)**

**Authors**: Sajid Anwar, Sizhong Sun

**Abstract**: This paper examines how adverse supply-side shocks in domestic input markets influence firms' vertical outward foreign direct investment (OFDI) decisions. While the theoretical basis for cost-driven OFDI is well established, empirical evidence on the causal mechanisms remains limited. We develop a framework in which input cost shocks raise unit production costs, but firms undertake vertical OFDI only when shocks are sufficiently severe or when baseline costs are already high. Firm heterogeneity leads to a sorting pattern, whereby more productive firms are more likely to invest abroad. To test this mechanism, we exploit China's 2017 waste paper import ban as an exogenous shock and leverage a distinctive feature of the paper product industry's supply chain. Using a difference-in-differences strategy and firm-level data from 2000 to 2023, we find that the policy shock increased the probability of vertical OFDI by approximately 16% in the post-policy period relative to a control group. These results provide robust evidence that firms respond to domestic input shocks by reallocating production across borders, highlighting vertical OFDI as a strategic response to supply-side disruptions. The findings contribute to understanding the micro-foundations of global production decisions in the face of input market volatility.

**摘要**: 本文研究了国内投入品市场的不利供给侧冲击如何影响企业垂直型对外直接投资（OFDI）的决策。尽管成本驱动型OFDI的理论基础已经得到了很好的确立，但关于因果机制的实证证据仍然有限。我们构建了一个框架，其中投入成本冲击提高了单位生产成本，但企业只有在冲击足够严重或基线成本已经很高时才会进行垂直OFDI。企业异质性导致了一种排序模式，即生产率更高的企业更有可能在海外投资。为了测试这种机制，我们利用中国2017年废纸进口禁令作为外生冲击，并利用纸制品行业供应链的一个独特特征。通过使用差异-差异策略和2000年至2023年的公司层面数据，我们发现政策冲击使垂直OFDI的可能性在政策后期间相对于对照组增加了约16%。这些结果为企业提供了一个强有力的证据，即企业通过跨境重新分配生产来应对国内投入品冲击，突出了垂直OFDI作为应对供给侧中断的战略性反应。这些发现有助于理解在全球生产决策中输入市场波动的微观基础。

### EconAgentic 在 DePIN 市场中：一种大型语言模型方法应用于去中心化物理基础设施的共享经济
**[EconAgentic in DePIN Markets: A Large Language Model Approach to the Sharing Economy of Decentralized Physical Infrastructure](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2508.21368)**

**Authors**: Yulin Liu, Mocca Schweitzer

**Abstract**: The Decentralized Physical Infrastructure (DePIN) market is revolutionizing the sharing economy through token-based economics and smart contracts that govern decentralized operations. By 2024, DePIN projects have exceeded \$10 billion in market capitalization, underscoring their rapid growth. However, the unregulated nature of these markets, coupled with the autonomous deployment of AI agents in smart contracts, introduces risks such as inefficiencies and potential misalignment with human values. To address these concerns, we introduce EconAgentic, a Large Language Model (LLM)-powered framework designed to mitigate these challenges. Our research focuses on three key areas: 1) modeling the dynamic evolution of DePIN markets, 2) evaluating stakeholders' actions and their economic impacts, and 3) analyzing macroeconomic indicators to align market outcomes with societal goals. Through EconAgentic, we simulate how AI agents respond to token incentives, invest in infrastructure, and adapt to market conditions, comparing AI-driven decisions with human heuristic benchmarks. Our results show that EconAgentic provides valuable insights into the efficiency, inclusion, and stability of DePIN markets, contributing to both academic understanding and practical improvements in the design and governance of decentralized, tokenized economies.

### 表征和最小化元广告实验中的发散交付
**[Characterizing and Minimizing Divergent Delivery in Meta Advertising Experiments](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2508.21251)**

**Authors**: Gordon Burtch, Robert Moakler, Brett R. Gordon, Poppy Zhang, Shawndra Hill

**Abstract**: Many digital platforms offer advertisers experimentation tools like Meta's Lift and A/B tests to optimize their ad campaigns. Lift tests compare outcomes between users eligible to see ads versus users in a no-ad control group. In contrast, A/B tests compare users exposed to alternative ad configurations, absent any control group. The latter setup raises the prospect of divergent delivery: ad delivery algorithms may target different ad variants to different audience segments. This complicates causal interpretation because results may reflect both ad content effectiveness and changes to audience composition. We offer three key contributions. First, we make clear that divergent delivery is specific to A/B tests and intentional, informing advertisers about ad performance in practice. Second, we measure divergent delivery at scale, considering 3,204 Lift tests and 181,890 A/B tests. Lift tests show no meaningful audience imbalance, confirming their causal validity, while A/B tests show clear imbalance, as expected. Third, we demonstrate that campaign configuration choices can reduce divergent delivery in A/B tests, lessening algorithmic influence on results. While no configuration guarantees eliminating divergent delivery entirely, we offer evidence-based guidance for those seeking more generalizable insights about ad content in A/B tests.

**摘要**: 许多数字平台为广告商提供了实验工具，如Meta的Lift测试和A/B测试，以优化他们的广告活动。Lift测试比较了有资格看到广告的用户与没有广告的对照组用户之间的结果。相比之下，A/B测试比较了接触到不同广告配置的用户，没有对照组。后者的设置引发了不同的交付前景：广告交付算法可能会将不同的广告变体针对不同的受众群体。这使得因果解释变得复杂，因为结果可能同时反映了广告内容的有效性和受众构成的变化。我们提供了三个关键贡献。首先，我们明确指出，不同的交付是A/B测试特有的，并且是有意为之，告知广告商广告在实践中的表现。其次，我们在大规模上测量了不同的交付，考虑了3,204个Lift测试和181,890个A/B测试。Lift测试显示没有显著的受众不平衡，确认了它们的因果有效性，而A/B测试显示了明显的不平衡，正如预期的那样。第三，我们展示了活动配置选择可以减少A/B测试中的不同交付，减少算法对结果的影响。虽然没有配置可以保证完全消除不同的交付，但我们为那些寻求更普遍的广告内容见解的人提供了基于证据的指导。
