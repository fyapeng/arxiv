### 具有许多协变量的线性回归的Wild Bootstrap推断
**[Wild Bootstrap Inference for Linear Regressions with Many Covariates](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20972)**

**Authors**: Wenze Li

**Abstract**: We propose a simple modification to the wild bootstrap procedure and establish its asymptotic validity for linear regression models with many covariates and heteroskedastic errors. Monte Carlo simulations show that the modified wild bootstrap has excellent finite sample performance compared with alternative methods that are based on standard normal critical values, especially when the sample size is small and/or the number of controls is of the same order of magnitude as the sample size.

**摘要**: 我们提出了一种对野生自助程序的简单修改，并建立了其在具有许多协变量和异方差误差的线性回归模型中的渐近有效性。蒙特卡洛模拟表明，与基于标准正态临界值的替代方法相比，修改后的野生自助在有限样本性能上表现出色，特别是在样本量较小和/或控制变量的数量与样本量同一数量级时。

### 利用受限贝叶斯估计的多层分解广义熵测度：以日本地区数据为例的应用
**[Multilevel Decomposition of Generalized Entropy Measures Using Constrained Bayes Estimation: An Application to Japanese Regional Data](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.21213)**

**Authors**: Yuki Kawakubo, Kazuhiko Kakamu

**Abstract**: We propose a method for multilevel decomposition of generalized entropy (GE) measures that explicitly accounts for nested population structures such as national, regional, and subregional levels. Standard approaches that estimate GE separately at each level do not guarantee compatibility with multilevel decomposition. Our method constrains lower-level GE estimates to match higher-level benchmarks while preserving hierarchical relationships across layers. We apply the method to Japanese income data to estimate GE at the national, prefectural, and municipal levels, decomposing national inequality into between-prefecture and within-prefecture inequality, and further decomposing prefectural GE into between-municipality and within-municipality inequality.

**摘要**: 我们提出了一种用于多层次分解广义熵（GE）度量的方法，该方法明确考虑了国家、区域和子区域等嵌套人口结构。标准方法分别在每个层面估计GE，但不能保证与多层次分解的兼容性。我们的方法通过约束较低层级的GE估计以匹配较高层级的基准，同时保持各层级间的层级关系。我们将这种方法应用于日本收入数据，以估计国家、县和市层面的GE，将国家不平等分解为县际和县内不平等，并进一步将县层面的GE分解为市际和市内不平等。

### 制度噪声、战略偏差与跨时崩溃：矿工在协议不确定性下行为的正式模型
**[Institutional Noise, Strategic Deviation, and Intertemporal Collapse: A Formal Model of Miner Behaviour under Protocol Uncertainty](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20992)**

**Authors**: Craig Steven Wright

**Abstract**: This paper develops a formal game-theoretic model to examine how protocol mutability disrupts cooperative mining behaviour in blockchain systems. Using a repeated game framework with stochastic rule shocks, we show that even minor uncertainty in institutional rules increases time preference and induces strategic deviation. Fixed-rule environments support long-term investment and stable equilibrium strategies; in contrast, mutable protocols lead to short-termism, higher discounting, and collapse of coordinated engagement. Simulation results identify instability zones in the parameter space where rational mining gives way to extractive or arbitrage conduct. These findings support an Austrian economic interpretation: calculability requires rule stability. Institutional noise undermines the informational basis for productive action. We conclude that protocol design must be treated as a constitutional economic constraint, not a discretionary variable, if sustainable cooperation is to emerge in decentralised systems.

**摘要**: 本文建立了一个正式的博弈论模型，以检验协议可变性如何破坏区块链系统中的合作挖矿行为。我们使用带有随机规则冲击的重复博弈框架，展示了即使是制度规则中的微小不确定性也会增加时间偏好并诱导策略性偏离。固定规则环境支持长期投资和稳定的均衡策略；相比之下，可变协议导致短期行为、更高的贴现率和协调参与的崩溃。模拟结果识别了参数空间中的不稳定区域，其中理性挖矿让位于掠夺性或套利行为。这些发现支持奥地利经济学的解释：可计算性需要规则稳定性。制度噪声破坏了生产性行动的信息基础。我们得出结论，如果去中心化系统中要出现可持续合作，协议设计必须被视为宪法经济约束，而非自由裁量变量。

### 欧洲足球中的悬念与惊喜
**[Suspense and Surprise in European Football](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.21253)**

**Authors**: Raphael Flepp, Tim Pawlowski, Travis Richardson

**Abstract**: We propose utilizing match-level suspense and surprise - which capture the entertainment utility created by competitive balance and outcome uncertainty for sports spectators - as alternative policy targets for league organizers and managers. Through simulations, we derive a benchmark range for suspense and surprise based on a perfectly balanced match before analyzing over 25,000 men's matches (2010/11-2023/24) and 725 women's matches (2023/24) from Europe's top football leagues. Our findings reveal that an average match generates lower suspense compared to the benchmark range, particularly for top teams, while surprise values consistently align with the benchmark. Moreover, we observe nuanced trends over time in men's football and highlight notable differences across leagues and clubs in both men's and women's competitions. These insights enhance our understanding of how the attractiveness of matches arises from competitive balance and carry important policy implications.

**摘要**: 我们建议利用比赛层面的悬念和惊喜——这些能够捕捉到体育观众因竞技平衡和结果不确定性而产生的娱乐效用——作为联盟组织者和管理者的替代政策目标。通过模拟，我们首先基于一场完全平衡的比赛得出悬念和惊喜的基准范围，然后分析了来自欧洲顶级足球联赛的超过25,000场男子比赛（2010/11-2023/24赛季）和725场女子比赛（2023/24赛季）。我们的发现揭示了平均比赛产生的悬念低于基准范围，尤其是对于顶级球队，而惊喜值则始终与基准相符。此外，我们观察到男子足球随时间变化的微妙趋势，并强调了男女比赛中不同联赛和俱乐部之间的显著差异。这些见解增强了我们对比赛吸引力如何从竞技平衡中产生以及其重要的政策含义的理解。

### 理性矿工行为、协议稳定性和时间偏好：对比特币激励环境的奥地利学派和博弈论分析
**[Rational Miner Behaviour, Protocol Stability, and Time Preference: An Austrian and Game-Theoretic Analysis of Bitcoin's Incentive Environment](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20965)**

**Authors**: Craig Steven Wright

**Abstract**: This paper integrates Austrian capital theory with repeated game theory to examine strategic miner behaviour under different institutional conditions in blockchain systems. It shows that when protocol rules are mutable, effective time preference rises, undermining rational long-term planning and cooperative equilibria. Using formal game-theoretic analysis and Austrian economic principles, the paper demonstrates how mutable protocols shift miner incentives from productive investment to political rent-seeking and influence games. The original Bitcoin protocol is interpreted as an institutional anchor: a fixed rule-set enabling calculability and low time preference. Drawing on the work of Bohm-Bawerk, Mises, and Hayek, the argument is made that protocol immutability is essential for restoring strategic coherence, entrepreneurial confidence, and sustainable network equilibrium.

**摘要**: 本文将奥地利资本理论与重复博弈理论相结合，以研究区块链系统中不同制度条件下矿工的战略行为。文章表明，当协议规则可变时，有效的时间偏好上升，破坏了理性的长期规划和合作均衡。利用正式的博弈理论分析和奥地利经济原则，本文展示了可变协议如何将矿工的激励从生产性投资转移到政治寻租和影响游戏。原始的比特币协议被解释为一个制度锚点：一个固定的规则集，使可计算性和低时间偏好成为可能。借鉴Bohm-Bawerk、Mises和Hayek的工作，文章认为协议的不可变性对于恢复战略一致性、企业家信心和可持续网络均衡至关重要。

### 非歧视性个性化定价
**[Non-Discriminatory Personalized Pricing](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20925)**

**Authors**: Philipp Strack, Kai Hao Yang

**Abstract**: A monopolist offers personalized prices to consumers with unit demand, heterogeneous values, and idiosyncratic costs, who differ in a protected characteristic, such as race or gender. The seller is subject to a non-discrimination constraint: consumers with the same cost, but different characteristics must face identical prices. Such constraints arise in regulated markets like credit or insurance. The setting reduces to an optimal transport, and we characterize the optimal pricing rule. Under this rule, consumers may retain surplus, and either group may benefit. Strengthening the constraint to cover transaction prices redistributes surplus, harming the low-value group and benefiting the high-value group.

**摘要**: 一个垄断者向具有单位需求、异质价值和特有成本的消费者提供个性化定价，这些消费者在受保护的特征上有所不同，例如种族或性别。卖家受到非歧视约束：具有相同成本但不同特征的消费者必须面临相同的价格。这种约束出现在信贷或保险等受监管的市场中。该设置简化为最优运输问题，我们描述了最优定价规则。在这一规则下，消费者可能保留剩余，任何一组都可能受益。加强约束以涵盖交易价格重新分配剩余，损害低价值群体并使高价值群体受益。

### 不同加密资产对系统性和特有风险的异质性暴露：分而治之的方法
**[Heterogeneous Exposures to Systematic and Idiosyncratic Risk across Crypto Assets: A Divide-and-Conquer Approach](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.21100)**

**Authors**: Nektarios Aslanidis, Aurelio Bariviera, George Kapetanios, Vasilis Sarafidis

**Abstract**: This paper analyzes realized return behavior across a broad set of crypto assets by estimating heterogeneous exposures to idiosyncratic and systematic risk. A key challenge arises from the latent nature of broader economy-wide risk sources: macro-financial proxies are unavailable at high-frequencies, while the abundance of low-frequency candidates offers limited guidance on empirical relevance. To address this, we develop a two-stage ``divide-and-conquer'' approach. The first stage estimates exposures to high-frequency idiosyncratic and market risk only, using asset-level IV regressions. The second stage identifies latent economy-wide factors by extracting the leading principal component from the model residuals and mapping it to lower-frequency macro-financial uncertainty and sentiment-based indicators via high-dimensional variable selection. Structured patterns of heterogeneity in exposures are uncovered using Mean Group estimators across asset categories. The method is applied to a broad sample of crypto assets, covering more than 80% of total market capitalization. We document short-term mean reversion and significant average exposures to idiosyncratic volatility and illiquidity. Green and DeFi assets are, on average, more exposed to market-level and economy-wide risk than their non-Green and non-DeFi counterparts. By contrast, stablecoins are less exposed to idiosyncratic, market-level, and economy-wide risk factors relative to non-stablecoins. At a conceptual level, our study develops a coherent framework for isolating distinct layers of risk in crypto markets. Empirically, it sheds light on how return sensitivities vary across digital asset categories -- insights that are important for both portfolio design and regulatory oversight.

**摘要**: 本文通过估计对特有风险和系统性风险的异质性暴露，分析了广泛的加密资产的实现回报行为。一个关键挑战来自于更广泛经济范围内风险源的潜在性质：宏观金融代理在高频率上不可用，而大量低频率候选者提供的实证相关性指导有限。为了解决这个问题，我们开发了一个两阶段的“分而治之”方法。第一阶段仅使用资产层面的工具变量回归来估计对高频特有和市场风险的暴露。第二阶段通过从模型残差中提取主要主成分，并将其通过高维变量选择映射到低频宏观金融不确定性和基于情绪的指标，来识别潜在的经济范围因素。使用Mean Group估计器在资产类别中发现异质性暴露的结构化模式。该方法应用于广泛的加密资产样本，涵盖了超过80%的总市值。我们记录了短期均值回归和对特有波动性和流动性的显著平均暴露。平均而言，绿色和DeFi资产比非绿色和非DeFi资产更多地暴露于市场层面和经济范围风险。相比之下，稳定币相对于非稳定币较少暴露于特有、市场层面和经济范围风险因素。从概念上讲，我们的研究为在加密市场中隔离不同层次的风险提供了一个连贯的框架。从实证上讲，它揭示了数字资产类别之间的回报敏感性如何变化——这些见解对于投资组合设计和监管监督都很重要。

### 双向聚类的分析推断
**[Analytic inference with two-way clustering](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20749)**

**Authors**: Laurent Davezies, Xavier D'Haultfœuille, Yannick Guyonvarch

**Abstract**: This paper studies analytic inference along two dimensions of clustering. In such setups, the commonly used approach has two drawbacks. First, the corresponding variance estimator is not necessarily positive. Second, inference is invalid in non-Gaussian regimes, namely when the estimator of the parameter of interest is not asymptotically Gaussian. We consider a simple fix that addresses both issues. In Gaussian regimes, the corresponding tests are asymptotically exact and equivalent to usual ones. Otherwise, the new tests are asymptotically conservative. We also establish their uniform validity over a certain class of data generating processes. Independently of our tests, we highlight potential issues with multiple testing and nonlinear estimators under two-way clustering. Finally, we compare our approach with existing ones through simulations.

**摘要**: 本文研究了在聚类两个维度上的解析推断。在这种设置中，常用的方法有两个缺点。首先，相应的方差估计器不一定是正的。其次，在非高斯分布的情况下，推断是无效的，即当感兴趣的参数估计器不是渐近高斯分布时。我们考虑一个简单的修正，可以解决这两个问题。在高斯分布的情况下，相应的检验是渐近精确的，并且等同于通常的检验。否则，新的检验是渐近保守的。我们还建立了它们在一定类别的数据生成过程上的统一有效性。与我们的检验无关，我们强调了在双向聚类下多重检验和非线性估计器的潜在问题。最后，我们通过模拟比较了我们的方法与现有方法。

### 凸回归的正交性条件
**[Orthogonality conditions for convex regression](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.21110)**

**Authors**: Sheng Dai, Timo Kuosmanen, Xun Zhou

**Abstract**: Econometric identification generally relies on orthogonality conditions, which usually state that the random error term is uncorrelated with the explanatory variables. In convex regression, the orthogonality conditions for identification are unknown. Applying Lagrangian duality theory, we establish the sample orthogonality conditions for convex regression, including additive and multiplicative formulations of the regression model, with and without monotonicity and homogeneity constraints. We then propose a hybrid instrumental variable control function approach to mitigate the impact of potential endogeneity in convex regression. The superiority of the proposed approach is shown in a Monte Carlo study and examined in an empirical application to Chilean manufacturing data.

**摘要**: 计量经济学的识别通常依赖于正交性条件，这些条件通常表明随机误差项与解释变量不相关。在凸回归中，用于识别的正交性条件是未知的。通过应用拉格朗日对偶理论，我们建立了包括加法和乘法形式的回归模型的样本正交性条件，无论是否包含单调性和齐次性约束。然后，我们提出了一种混合工具变量控制函数方法，以减轻凸回归中潜在内生性的影响。在蒙特卡洛研究中展示了所提出方法的优越性，并在智利制造业数据的实证应用中进行了检验。

### 本地生产网络中企业层面系统性风险的演变与决定因素
**[Evolution and determinants of firm-level systemic risk in local production networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.21426)**

**Authors**: Anna Mancini, Balázs Lengyel, Riccardo Di Clemente, Giulio Cimini

**Abstract**: Recent crises like the COVID-19 pandemic and geopolitical tensions have exposed vulnerabilities and caused disruptions of supply chains, leading to product shortages, increased costs, and economic instability. This has prompted increasing efforts to assess systemic risk, namely the effects of firm disruptions on entire economies. However, the ability of firms to react to crises by rewiring their supply links has been largely overlooked, limiting our understanding of production networks resilience. Here we study dynamics and determinants of firm-level systemic risk in the Hungarian production network from 2015 to 2022. We use as benchmark a heuristic maximum entropy null model that generates an ensemble of production networks at equilibrium, by preserving the total input (demand) and output (supply) of each firm at the sector level. We show that the fairly stable set of firms with highest systemic risk undergoes a structural change during COVID-19, as those enabling economic exchanges become key players in the economy -- a result which is not reproduced by the null model. Although the empirical systemic risk aligns well with the null value until the onset of the pandemic, it becomes significantly smaller afterwards as the adaptive behavior of firms leads to a more resilient economy. Furthermore, firms' international trade volume (being a subject of disruption) becomes a significant predictor of their systemic risk. However, international links cannot provide an unequivocal explanation for the observed trends, as imports and exports have opposing effects on local systemic risk through the supply and demand channels.

**摘要**: 近期的危机，如COVID-19大流行和地缘政治紧张局势，暴露了脆弱性并导致了供应链的中断，进而引发产品短缺、成本增加和经济不稳定。这促使人们越来越努力地评估系统性风险，即企业中断对整个经济的影响。然而，企业通过重新配置供应链联系来应对危机的能力在很大程度上被忽视了，限制了我们对生产网络弹性的理解。在这里，我们研究了2015年至2022年匈牙利生产网络中企业层面系统性风险的动态和决定因素。我们使用启发式最大熵零模型作为基准，该模型通过保持每个企业在部门层面的总输入（需求）和输出（供应），生成一系列处于平衡状态的生产网络。我们展示了在COVID-19期间，具有最高系统性风险的相当稳定的企业集合经历了结构性变化，因为那些促进经济交换的企业成为经济中的关键参与者——这一结果没有被零模型复制。尽管实证系统性风险在大流行开始之前与零值非常吻合，但在之后变得明显更小，因为企业的适应行为导致了一个更有弹性的经济体。此外，企业的国际贸易量（作为中断的主题）成为其系统性风险的一个重要预测因子。然而，国际联系不能为观察到的趋势提供明确的解释，因为进口和出口通过供应和需求渠道对本地系统性风险产生相反的影响。

### 用高斯-维特种群生态学系统模拟收入分配
**[Modeling Income Distribution with the Gause-Witt Population Ecology System](https://arxiv.org/abs/arXiv:2506.20881)**

**Authors**: Marcelo B. Ribeiro

**Abstract**: This paper presents an empirical application of the Gause-Witt model of population ecology and ecosystems to the income distribution competitive dynamics of social classes in economic systems. The Gause-Witt mathematical system of coupled nonlinear first-order ordinary differential equations employed to model population of species sharing the same ecological niche and competing for the same resources was applied to the income data of Brazil. Previous studies using Brazilian income data from 1981 to 2009 showed that the complementary cumulative distribution functions built from yearly datasets have two distinct segments: the lower income region comprising of about 99% of the population can be represented by the Gompertz curve, whereas the richest 1% is described by the Pareto power-law. The results of applying the Gause-Witt system to Brazilian income data in order to describe the distributive competition dynamics of these two population shares indicate that the 99% and 1% income classes are mostly in the dynamic state of stable coexistence.
